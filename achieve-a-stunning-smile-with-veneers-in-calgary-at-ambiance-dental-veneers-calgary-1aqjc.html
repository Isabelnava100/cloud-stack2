Reasons to Consider Veneers at Ambiance Dental

What Are Veneers and Their Advantages

Veneers consist of ultra-thin shells made from high-quality porcelain or composite resin that cover the visible surface of your teeth. They are custom crafted to match the color and shape of your natural teeth, producing an aesthetically pleasing effect. With veneers, you can effectively conceal flaws like stains, chips, and spacing issues, ultimately refining your smile. Additionally, veneers are known for their strength, allowing you to maintain your new smile for years when properly cared for.

The Veneer Application Process at Ambiance Dental

Receiving veneers in Calgary at Ambiance Dental is an uncomplicated journey that initiates with an initial consultation. Our dental experts will evaluate your dental condition and gather insights regarding your envisioned outcome to ascertain whether veneers are a suitable choice for you. Upon confirmation, we prepare your teeth and capture impressions to fabricate custom veneers tailored just for you. Finally, the bonding process securely adheres the veneers to your teeth, achieving seamless integration with your smile.

Key Aftercare Steps for Your Veneers

Once your veneers are in place, following proper aftercare measures is crucial for preserving their appeal and durability. Establishing a robust oral care routine that includes consistent brushing and flossing is vital. It’s advisable to avoid harsh or chewy foods that could compromise your veneers, and booking regular check-ups at Ambiance Dental will help maintain optimal dental health. With attentive care, your veneers can retain their brilliance for many years.
