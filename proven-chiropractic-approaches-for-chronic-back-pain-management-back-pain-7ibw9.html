Identifying Back Pain: Origins and Indicators

Back pain is a widespread condition impacting millions globally every year. It may stem from multiple sources such as improper posture, injury, or hidden health issues. Understanding the signs of back pain is essential for successful interventions. Common indicators include sharp or dull discomfort, rigidity, or restricted mobility.
