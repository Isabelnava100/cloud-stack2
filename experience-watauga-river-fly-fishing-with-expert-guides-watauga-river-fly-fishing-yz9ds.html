Frequently Asked Questions

High Flying Flies and Guide Service specializes in guided fly fishing river float trips for trout in Eastern Tennessee. We provide a unique experience on popular rivers such as the South Holston River in Bristol and the Watauga River in Johnson City. Our trips cater to all skill levels, making it perfect for both beginners and experienced anglers. Whether you're looking for a full-day or half-day scenic float, we have you covered with all necessary equipment included in your trip.

Our guided fly fishing trips are designed to create a stress-free and enjoyable experience. First, you’ll meet your guide who will provide all the necessary equipment for Watauga River fly fishing or South Holston River fishing. After a brief safety and instructional session tailored to your skill level, you will embark on a scenic float where our experienced guides will help you find the best spots for trout fishing. Throughout the trip, techniques and skills will be shared, ensuring you get the most out of your experience.

While enjoying your guided fly fishing trip in Eastern Tennessee, you can expect to encounter both rainbow and brown trout. These species are abundant in the South Holston River and Watauga River, making them prime locations for your fishing adventure. Our experienced guides will provide tips on techniques that are most effective for catching these beautiful trout during your float trip.

No prior fishing experience is required to enjoy our guided fly fishing trips. Whether you are a first-time angler or have some experience under your belt, our guides are dedicated to making your trip enjoyable and educational. We will provide all the necessary equipment and training, ensuring that you can fully immerse yourself in the Eastern Tennessee trout fishing experience without any prior knowledge.

When joining us for a guided fly fishing trip, we recommend bringing personal items such as sunscreen, polarized sunglasses, a hat, and facial coverings for sun protection. Comfortable clothing suited for outdoor activities is also advised. If you have your own gear and prefer to use it, feel free to bring it along. However, all essential fly fishing equipment will be provided for you on the trip, so you don't need to worry about that.

What are the best times of year for Watauga River fly fishing?
The best time for Watauga River fly fishing typically varies, but spring and fall are generally considered prime fishing seasons due to optimal water temperatures and fish activity. During these periods, the fish are more likely to be active and feeding. However, our guides are experienced in knowing when and where to fish throughout the year, so no matter when you choose to book your trip, you're likely to have an enjoyable fishing experience in Eastern Tennessee.
