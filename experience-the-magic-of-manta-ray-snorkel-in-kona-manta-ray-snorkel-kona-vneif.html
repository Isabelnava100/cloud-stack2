Why Choose KONASTYLE for Manta Ray Tours?

KONASTYLE is a Native Hawaiian-owned business with deep roots in the community, dedicated to offering extraordinary ocean experiences. Our late night manta ray snorkel tours are tailored to provide the best opportunities for guests to interact with these magnificent animals. Because we maintain smaller group sizes, each guest receives personalized attention from our certified and licensed crew members, ensuring safety and comfort throughout the tour. We are proud to generate excitement and education while promoting marine conservation during each tour, making us the top choice for a unique adventure in Kona.

What to Expect During Your Manta Ray Snorkel Tour

Our Late Night Manta Ray Snorkel Tour begins at sunset, providing a magical atmosphere as we head out on the calm waters of the Kona Coast. You will receive all necessary equipment to comfortably snorkel, including wetsuits and snorkeling gear, so you can focus on the experience. Once you are in the water, our knowledgeable guides will help you spot the manta rays as they gracefully glide through the ocean, feeding on plankton. With an emphasis on safety, our crew ensures that everyone has a memorable, enjoyable experience while respecting the marine life we encounter.

Community Support and Our Commitment to Aloha

At KONASTYLE, we are devoted to giving back to the local community that we love. A portion of our tour proceeds goes towards supporting Hawaiian cultural events and non-profit organizations. We believe that fostering a connection with the local community is vital to preserving Hawaiian culture and marine ecosystems. Our tours not only aim to provide enjoyment but also to educate guests about the importance of environmental stewardship and the magic of the ocean surrounding us in Kona.
