How do I care for my Fruit Shirt t-shirt?
To keep your Fruit Shirt t-shirt looking vibrant and fresh, we recommend washing it in cold water with like colors to prevent fading. Avoid using bleach and tumble dry on low heat or hang-dry for longevity. A gentle iron can be used if needed, but please avoid direct contact with the printed designs. Following these care instructions will help ensure that your fun and health-inspired apparel lasts for years to come.
