Exploring Your Options for Financing Solar Energy Solutions

Different Types of Solar Financing Choices

When looking into financing solar energy, it’s important to know the range of options that exist. Homeowners might pursue solar loans, which allow you to borrow funds to acquire the solar system outright. Leases provide the opportunity to pay a fixed monthly fee for using a solar energy system maintained by another entity. Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) permit payment solely for the energy created by your solar solution, which can yield notable savings over the course of time. These varied financing avenues enable you to customize your selection based on fiscal constraints and energy requirements.
