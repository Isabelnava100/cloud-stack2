Extra FAQ

How often do I need to wear my aligners?

It's recommended to wear your aligners for at least 20-22 hours a day to achieve the best results in the shortest time.

How do I care for my Invisalign aligners?

You can clean your aligners by rinsing them under lukewarm water and brushing them gently with a soft toothbrush. Avoid using hot water, as it can warp the aligners.
