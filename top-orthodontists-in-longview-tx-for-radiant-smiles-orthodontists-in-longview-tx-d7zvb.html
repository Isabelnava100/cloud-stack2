How often should I come for orthodontic appointments?
Typically, appointments for adjustments are spaced from 4 to 8 weeks apart; however, your orthodontist will develop a schedule that is tailored to your specific treatment regimen.

What should I do to care for my braces?
To maintain your braces properly, it’s crucial to uphold good oral hygiene, steer clear of hard or sticky foods, and show up for all scheduled visits to advance your treatment effectively.
