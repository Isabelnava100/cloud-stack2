Essential Information on Dual Diagnosis & Treatment in MA

Dual diagnosis refers to the co-occurrence of mental health disorders and substance use disorders. This complex condition requires specialized treatment to address both issues simultaneously. At South Shore Recovery Center in Plymouth, MA, we understand the challenges faced by individuals with dual diagnosis. Our dedicated team is here to provide the support and resources necessary for effective dual diagnosis treatment and recovery.

Recognizing the signs of dual diagnosis is crucial for timely intervention. Many individuals may struggle with anxiety, depression, or other mental health issues while also battling addiction. Our mental health clinic in Plymouth, Massachusetts offers a safe space for individuals to explore the challenges of co-occurring disorders. We are committed to helping you achieve lasting recovery through personalized treatment plans tailored to your unique needs.
