Frequently Asked Questions

Eyebrow threading is a hair removal technique that uses a twisted thread to remove unwanted hair from the follicle, resulting in a precise and clean brow shape.

Typically, eyebrow threading results can last anywhere from 2 to 6 weeks, depending on individual hair growth.

Many clients report that eyebrow threading is less painful than waxing, although sensitivities may vary from person to person.

We offer a range of waxing services, including eyebrow waxing, lip waxing, and full body waxing, tailored to meet your grooming needs.

To prepare for your waxing appointment, ensure the hair is at least 1/4 inch long and exfoliate your skin 24 hours prior to your session.
