The Importance of Radon Testing in Calgary

Radon testing is crucial for ensuring the safety of your indoor environment. In Calgary, many homes and businesses may be at risk of radon exposure, which is a known carcinogen. Regular radon testing helps detect any harmful levels of this gas, allowing for timely mitigation efforts. At Calgary Radon Detection, we emphasize the significance of testing in maintaining healthy living and working spaces.

Understanding Radon Mitigation Techniques

Once radon levels are identified, appropriate mitigation techniques must be implemented to reduce these levels. Common methods include active soil depressurization, sealing gaps and cracks, and improving ventilation. These techniques are designed to prevent radon from entering buildings and to protect occupants from exposure.
