Exploring Sciatica and Its Underlying Causes

Sciatica is defined as pain that follows the trajectory of the sciatic nerve, which stretches from the lower back through both legs. The most frequent causes of sciatica consist of herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and muscular strains that exert pressure on the nerve. Identifying the source of your sciatica is vital for successful treatment. At Keep it Kinetic Chiropractic, we conduct detailed evaluations to formulate the most appropriate treatment plan for your specific condition.
