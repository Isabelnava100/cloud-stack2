Materials Used in RTA Cabinets

RTA cabinets are primarily made from ½” plywood for the sides and back, providing sturdy support and resistance to warping and cracking. The front frame, doors, and drawers of our cabinets are crafted from solid birch, ensuring beauty and durability. This combination of materials is ideal for homeowners looking for long-lasting kitchen solutions. At Current Cabinetry, we guarantee that the materials used not only meet but exceed industry standards, offering both style and functionality for your kitchen space.
