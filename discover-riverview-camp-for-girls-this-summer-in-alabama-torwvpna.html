Discover Riverview Camp for Girls This Summer in Alabama

Greetings from Riverview Camp for Girls, the ultimate destination for a summer filled with joy! Exciting Activities at Riverview Camp

At Riverview Camp for Girls, we provide an extensive variety of adventurous activities aimed at motivating and challenging all campers. Girls will have the opportunity to kayak along Little River, explore our scenic nature paths, and enjoy our ropes course. With our horseback riding summer camp in Alabama, girls can connect with both horses and fellow campers while acquiring essential riding techniques. With more than 20 fun-filled activities offered, each day promises a fresh experience!

Building Lasting Friendships

Attending summer camp is about more than just activities; it’s a time to forge friendships that can last a lifetime. At Riverview Camp, girls experience meaningful connections that endure well beyond the summer. Through shared challenges and cooperative activities, your daughter will create friendships in a Christian setting. Whether it's around the campfire sharing stories or working together in activities, camaraderie thrives within our nurturing community.

Getting Ready for Camp Life
