Mechanism of Action

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that play crucial roles in cell signaling and biological functions. They can stimulate the body’s natural processes, promoting healing and regeneration. The mechanism of action comprises hormonal regulation, neurotransmitter effects, and modulation of immune responses, making them powerful agents in therapeutic applications.
