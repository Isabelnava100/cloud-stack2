What maintenance steps can I take to avoid water heater repairs?

Regularly scheduled maintenance, like flushing the tank each year, inspecting the pressure relief valve, and checking for leaks, will aid in extending the lifespan of your water heater and diminish repair needs. It's also recommended to arrange routine maintenance with our experts.
