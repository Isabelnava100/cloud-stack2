What distinguishes concrete sealing from concrete staining?
Concrete sealing and concrete staining serve distinct purposes in enhancing and protecting your concrete surfaces. Concrete sealing involves applying a protective layer to the surface to prevent moisture penetration, stains, and damage from chemicals, thereby extending the life of your concrete. Conversely, concrete staining is a decorative process that adds color and improves the appearance of the concrete. At Team Red Coatings, we provide both services to help you achieve the desired look and durability for your concrete surfaces.
