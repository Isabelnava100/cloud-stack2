Is there a vaccination requirement for dogs staying at Happy Pups Manor?
Yes, for the safety and well-being of all our guests, Happy Pups Manor requires that all dogs be up-to-date on their vaccinations before boarding. This includes rabies, distemper, and Bordetella vaccines. We ask you to provide proof of vaccination upon arrival. This policy helps us maintain a healthy environment for all pets and ensures that your furry friend will enjoy their luxury dog boarding experience safely.

What happens if my dog has special needs or medical conditions?
At Happy Pups Manor, we understand that each dog is unique and may have special needs or medical conditions. Our experienced team is committed to providing personalized care to all our guests. When making your reservation, please discuss any specific needs or concerns with us, including dietary restrictions, medication schedules, or behavioral issues. We will do our best to accommodate your dog's requirements, ensuring their comfort and well-being during their stay.
