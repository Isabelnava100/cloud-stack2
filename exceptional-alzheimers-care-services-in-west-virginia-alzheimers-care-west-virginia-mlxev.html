Exceptional Alzheimer's Care Services in West Virginia

At Braley Care Homes, we are dedicated to providing the highest quality of Alzheimer's care in West Virginia. As the only freestanding Alzheimer's facility in the state, our innovative and tailored approach to care ensures each resident receives the best support. Our 24/7 trained staff, including RNs, LPNs, and social workers, understands the unique needs of those living with Alzheimer's and dementia. We prioritize compassionate family support, making us the premier choice for families facing this challenging journey. Established in 2005, Braley Care Homes was founded by Chris and Dean Braley, who are driven by a personal connection to dementia. Our commitment goes beyond mere caregiving; we strive to alleviate family challenges and foster a nurturing environment. With specialized training and dedicated resources, our team is devoted to delivering exceptional memory care services. Together, we can navigate the complexities of Alzheimer's together with dignity and understanding.
