Discover Vintage Treasures at Re-psychles Art Gallery

Welcome to Re-psychles Vintage Shop & Art Gallery, where nostalgia meets creativity in stunning Palm Springs, CA. Our unique collection features vintage clothing from iconic brands like Carhartt, alongside upcycled fashion designed by h.m. Peavy—perfect for anyone with a taste for the extraordinary. Explore our art gallery showcasing African and Asian artifacts, colourful Hawaiian shirts, and elegant oriental rugs, creating an ambiance of cultural richness. Join us on a journey through time, where every piece tells a story and celebrates individuality.
