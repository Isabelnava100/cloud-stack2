How can I enhance the efficiency of my HVAC system?
Improving the efficiency of your HVAC system can be achieved through several straightforward steps. Regular maintenance is crucial; ensure you schedule annual check-ups with 32 Degrees. Additionally, keep your filters clean and replace them as needed to maintain proper airflow. Sealing any leaks in your ductwork and ensuring adequate insulation in your home can also boost efficiency.
