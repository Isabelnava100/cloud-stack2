Signs of a Gas Leak to Watch For

It's crucial to be vigilant about signs of a gas leak in your home. Look out for unusual odors, such as a sulfur or rotten egg smell, which indicates a potential leak. You may also notice hissing sounds near gas lines or see dead vegetation around your outdoor gas lines. If you experience sudden increases in your gas bill, it could also suggest a gas leak that needs immediate attention.
