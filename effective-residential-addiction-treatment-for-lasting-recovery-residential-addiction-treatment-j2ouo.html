Understanding the ASAM Continuum Model

The ASAM Continuum model serves as the foundation of our residential addiction treatment program at SOZO. This model ensures that each client receives appropriate care tailored to their unique needs and circumstances. It utilizes evidence-based practices to guide individuals through various stages of recovery, simplifying the treatment process while maximizing effectiveness. Through regular assessments and personalized treatment plans, we uphold a commitment to quality and comprehensive support for every individual.

The Importance of Dual Diagnosis Care

Dual diagnosis care is crucial in addressing the complex relationship between addiction and mental health disorders. At SOZO, we specialize in recognizing these interconnected issues in our clients, ensuring comprehensive treatment that targets both addiction and underlying psychological conditions. The integration of mental health support within our residential addiction treatment program fosters a more effective recovery process. By providing therapeutic strategies for both areas, individuals are better equipped to achieve sustainable recovery and improved quality of life.
