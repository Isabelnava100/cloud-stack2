At SOZO Addiction Recovery Center, we offer a dedicated platform for men seeking recovery from addiction challenges. Our specialized Men’s Addiction Treatment Program combines faith-based principles with dual diagnosis methodology, providing a holistic framework for recovery.

In our Men’s Addiction Treatment Program, clients benefit from diverse therapeutic modalities, including individual therapy, group dynamics, spiritual guidance, and holistic treatment, all tailored to their unique circumstances.

SOZO Recovery Center places emphasis on dual diagnosis, working to treat addiction and concurrent mental health disorders concurrently. Comprehensive evaluations by our trained staff lead to personalized treatment plans that reflect both facets of care.

Faith enriches the treatment process at SOZO Recovery Center, as many clients find strength and purpose in their spiritual beliefs. By integrating spirituality into recovery, we enhance the experiences of clients, often forming lasting bonds within our Men’s Addiction Treatment Program.

SOZO Recovery Center distinguishes itself through a unique blend of spiritual and empirical treatment methods. By employing the ASAM Continuum model, we ensure that care is both personalized and holistic, addressing all aspects of addiction and mental health in a supportive setting.

How long is the Men’s Addiction Treatment Program at SOZO?
The length of the Men’s Addiction Treatment Program is variable based on individual client needs and progress. Standard treatment lasts a minimum of 30 days; however, extended options are encouraged to solidify recovery foundations.

What are the steps to begin treatment at SOZO?
Getting started at SOZO Recovery Center is straightforward. You can reach us via the website or call our admissions team directly. We'll walk you through our assessment process to ensure a well-fitted treatment plan for your recovery.
