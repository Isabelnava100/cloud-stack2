Electrical Panel Upgrades and Services for Your Home

Why Upgrade Your Electrical Panel?

Upgrading your electrical panel is essential for enhancing your home's electrical system. A panel upgrade increases your service capacity, typically from 100 to 200 amps, which supports modern appliances and meets the needs of your household. More power means better functionality for all your devices, improving comfort and safety. Additionally, upgrading your breaker box or replacing an outdated fuse box ensures compliance with current codes and reduces the risk of electrical hazards.

What happens during an electrical panel replacement?

During a panel replacement, we will disconnect your power, remove the existing panel, install the new panel, and connect all circuits, ensuring everything is up to code.
