Understanding Special Needs Trusts

Special needs trusts are essential tools for safeguarding the financial future of individuals with disabilities. At Fuller Special Needs Law, we emphasize the importance of creating a trust that allows beneficiaries to maintain their eligibility for government benefits while enjoying additional support. Our experienced attorneys guide you through every step of establishing a special needs trust, ensuring that it is tailored to your child's needs and complies with Michigan laws. Together, we can provide the financial security your loved one deserves without jeopardizing necessary assistance.
