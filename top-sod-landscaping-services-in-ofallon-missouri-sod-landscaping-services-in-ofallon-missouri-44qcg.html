How do I maintain my new sod after installation?
After your new sod is installed, proper maintenance is essential for establishing a healthy lawn. Initially, you should water the sod daily to ensure it roots properly, gradually reducing the frequency as it establishes. Mowing should be limited until the grass reaches the recommended height, which helps promote strong growth. Fertilization and pest management should also be part of your ongoing care.
