The Haltech ECU (Engine Control Unit) serves as the brain of your vehicle's engine management system. It controls essential functions such as fuel injection, ignition timing, and boost pressure. By precisely managing these parameters, the Haltech ECU optimizes engine performance, improving power and efficiency while enhancing overall drivability. It allows for custom tuning, enabling enthusiasts to tailor their vehicle's performance to their specific needs.

A Haltech Dash is a digital display unit that provides real-time data regarding your vehicle's performance. This can include engine RPM, speed, load, and other vital parameters. The benefits of using a Haltech Dash include enhanced visibility of key performance metrics, easier diagnostics, and the ability to customize the layout according to your preferences. This makes it an invaluable tool for serious automotive enthusiasts looking to optimize their driving experience.

Installing a Haltech ECU and Haltech Dash involves a series of steps, typically requiring a basic understanding of automotive electronics. For the ECU, you would need to remove your existing engine management system, connect the Haltech ECU to your vehicle's wiring harness, and configure it via a computer interface. Similarly, the Haltech Dash can be installed by connecting it to the ECU to receive data, followed by mounting it in an accessible location. Detailed installation manuals and guides from Haltech can assist in this process.
