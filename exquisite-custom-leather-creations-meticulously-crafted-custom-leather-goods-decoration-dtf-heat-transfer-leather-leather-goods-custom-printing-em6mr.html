Common Questions

We utilize premium full-grain cowhide leather, known for its long-lasting quality, texture, and propensity to develop an attractive patina over time. Our selections encompass smooth, vintage, and textured finishes, all sourced ethically and consciously selected for their superior craftsmanship.

Clients are able to personalize their leather products with personal names, initials, logos, or unique artwork. Customization choices including font types, placement, colors, and sizes are available at checkout, giving customers the ability to preview their design before it goes into production.

Our customization methods are deliberately created for lasting durability while ensuring that the integrity of the leather is preserved. With appropriate care and maintenance, both the leather and the bespoke design can maintain their beauty over the years.

We suggest gently cleaning leather with a moist soft cloth and steering clear of excessive heat or prolonged exposure to sunlight. Regular conditioning is recommended to maintain the leather’s finish and preserve its natural appearance.

Under usual circumstances, production takes approximately 3-5 business days. However, during peak holiday times or busy seasons, additional processing time could be necessary depending on the complexity of the customization and the number of orders being handled.
