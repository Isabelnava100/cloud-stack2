What if the accident was partially my fault?
Tennessee follows a comparative fault system, allowing you to recover damages even if you were partially at fault, but your compensation may be reduced.

How long does a personal injury claim take to resolve?
The duration of a personal injury claim can vary widely based on the complexity of the case, from a few months to several years.
