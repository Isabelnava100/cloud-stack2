What is THCP?

THCP, or tetrahyrdrocannabiphorol, is a newly discovered cannabinoid that has gained attention for its potency. It was identified for its similarity to THC but differs in its structural makeup. Unlike Delta 8 and Delta 9, THCP presents unique interactions with the body's cannabinoid receptors, potentially leading to enhanced effects. Understanding what THCP is and its unique properties can help consumers make informed choices regarding cannabis products.
