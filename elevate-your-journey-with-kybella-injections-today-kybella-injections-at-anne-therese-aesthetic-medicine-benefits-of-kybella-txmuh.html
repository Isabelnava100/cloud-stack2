What Exactly is Kybella?

Kybella is an injectable treatment approved by the FDA, specifically created to target and reduce fat beneath the chin—commonly called a double chin. This state-of-the-art procedure uses deoxycholic acid to eliminate fat cells in the chin region, promoting a revitalized and youthful appearance. Our skilled staff at Anne Therese Aesthetic Medicine administers Kybella with accuracy to ensure effective outcomes. Numerous patients express satisfaction with the enhancement of their jawlines post-treatment, significantly elevating their self-confidence.

Advantages of Kybella Injections

One of the main advantages of Kybella injections is their capacity to effectively and permanently eradicate fat cells in the specified treatment area. Unlike transient solutions, the effects of Kybella are enduring and lead to a pronounced elevation in your facial silhouette. Furthermore, the procedure is efficient, typically lasting between 15 to 20 minutes, with little to no recovery time required afterwards. Most patients can promptly return to their regular routines, making Kybella a favored option for busy individuals seeking noteworthy aesthetic enhancements.

Can Kybella be used for other areas of the body?
Kybella is currently FDA-approved solely for addressing submental fat under the chin and is not recommended for use in other body parts.

How long do the results of Kybella last?
Results from Kybella are permanent, as it effectively destroys the fat cells in the treated area. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle will aid in preserving your results.
