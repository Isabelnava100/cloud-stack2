How frequently should I change my Pur Waters shower head filter?
To sustain optimal performance and ensure the best water quality, it is advised to replace your Pur Waters shower head filter every six months or after approximately 10,000 gallons of usage, depending on your water quality and usage habits. Regular filter replacements help ensure effective removal of contaminants while providing clean water during your showers. Regularly inspect the filter for signs of wear or reduced flow to determine the best time for replacement.
