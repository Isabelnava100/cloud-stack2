How often should I update my estate plan?
You should review and update your estate plan regularly, especially after significant life events like marriage, divorce, the birth of a child, or changes in your financial situation.

What happens if I die without an estate plan?
If you die without an estate plan, your assets will be distributed according to state laws, which may not align with your wishes. Additionally, your loved ones may face a lengthy and complicated probate process.
