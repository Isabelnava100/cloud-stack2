To remove Luminary Flex Gel, by gently soaking it off rather than filing it aggressively. Start by using a nail buffer to lightly buff the surface of the gel to break the top layer, allowing the remover to penetrate better. Then, apply acetone or a gel remover to a cotton pad and wrap it around your nails with aluminum foil to keep the product in place. Let it soak for about 10-15 minutes. After soaking, gently push off the softened gel with a cuticle pusher, being careful not to damage your natural nails. If any gel remains, repeat the soaking process until it’s completely removed. Always finish with a nail moisturizer to keep your nails hydrated.
