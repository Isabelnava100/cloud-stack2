What is Skin Inflammaging?

Skin inflammaging is a term that combines inflammation and aging, representing the skin's biological response to chronic external aggressors at a silent low grade inflammation. These aggressors can include pollution, UV rays, and even stress, which cause gradual skin changes such as collagen degradation. The underlying mechanisms of inflammaging result in skin that appears less youthful and more prone to sensitivity. Identifying the early signs of skin inflammaging can help you take proactive steps toward maintaining a healthier looking radiant complexion.

Common Signs of Skin Inflammaging

The signs of skin inflammaging can manifest in various ways, including the premature appearance of dullness, uneven skin tone, increased sensitivity, and fine lines. You may also notice a compromised skin barrier, leading to other issues such as unexpected look of dryness and increased feel of irritation. These symptoms may initially seem mild but can progressively worsen without proper care. Using science rooted clean skincare products designed for visible barrier repair can mitigate these effects and restore the skin's natural glow and resilience.
