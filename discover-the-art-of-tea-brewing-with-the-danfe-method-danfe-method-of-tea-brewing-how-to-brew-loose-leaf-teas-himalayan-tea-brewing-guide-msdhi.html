Exploring Cold Brew and Gongfu Techniques

Cold brew and Gongfu methods deliver exciting alternatives to conventional tea brewing practices. For a cold brew, infuse your chosen loose leaf tea in cold water for 6 to 12 hours in your fridge, resulting in a mellow, thirst-quenching beverage, particularly refreshing on warm days. On the other hand, Gongfu brewing empowers you to enjoy the intricate subtleties within your tea. This technique employs multiple brief steeps while utilizing a higher tea-to-water ratio, making it possible to experience all flavor profiles through varying infusions. Our guide offers step-by-step instructions for both techniques, allowing you to thoroughly enjoy every cup while enriching your comprehension of the tea’s components.
