What do you do in case of an emergency?
In an emergency at your residence, we will contact you promptly to discuss appropriate measures. Should we be unable to reach you, we will contact your secondary and emergency contacts. If your pet is in need of immediate help, one of our team leads—trained in first aid and animal CPR—will respond immediately. In the case that your pet requires additional attention, they will be taken to the nearest veterinary clinic, with you kept informed along the way. Fortunately, our diligence has minimized emergencies, yet we are committed to responding effectively and with calmness when necessary.
