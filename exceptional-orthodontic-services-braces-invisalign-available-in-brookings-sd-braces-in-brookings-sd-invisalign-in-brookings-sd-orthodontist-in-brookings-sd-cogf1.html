How frequently will I need to return to the orthodontic office during my treatment period?
Regular visits are typically planned every 4-8 weeks to assess progress and make necessary adjustments, with your orthodontist ensuring you're informed about what to expect during your care program.

What aftercare is necessary once braces or Invisalign treatments conclude?
Post-treatment, wearing a retainer is vital to maintain your newly achieved smile. Your orthodontist will provide insights regarding optimal retainer choices and care that aligns with your progress.
