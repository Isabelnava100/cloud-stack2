Welcome to Carrot & Daikon Banh Mi, your prime destination for authentic Vietnamese fare in Placentia, California. Whether you're a resident or merely visiting, savor a true taste of Vietnam right in the heart of North Orange County.

Delve into Vietnamese Flavors with Carrot & Daikon

The Appeal of Our Traditional Vietnamese Pho

At Carrot & Daikon, we create our Vietnamese pho using age-old recipes that feature the freshest ingredients and a rich, flavorful broth. This iconic dish stands as a cornerstone of Vietnamese cuisine, enjoyable at any mealtime. Our dedication to authenticity means that every bowl of pho delivers a transportive taste, capturing the essence of Vietnam. Choose from a variety of options, including traditional beef pho and tasty vegetarian alternatives.

Savor the Delight of Our Banh Mi Sandwiches

Banh mi sandwiches present a delightful blend of crispy bread and flavorful fillings, representing a wonderful Vietnamese twist. At Carrot & Daikon, our banh mi combines marinated meats like mouthwatering pork belly with fresh vegetables and homemade sauces. Don't miss out – grab one during your next visit! It's an ideal on-the-go meal for hectic days or a splendid complement to a casual dining experience with friends.

Quench Your Thirst with Our Specialty Drinks

No Vietnamese meal is truly complete without an ideal beverage to accompany it! At Carrot & Daikon, we present a variety of refreshing drinks that perfectly complement your pho and banh mi. Choose from traditional Vietnamese iced coffee to delightful smoothies; each beverage is crafted from premium ingredients to elevate your dining experience. Pair your meal with a drink that enhances the bold flavors of our menus.
