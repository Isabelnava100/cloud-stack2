Understanding DSCR Loans

DSCR loans, or Debt Service Coverage Ratio loans, represent a pivotal financing option for real estate investors. Unlike traditional loans that primarily focus on W-2 income, DSCR loans assess the cash flow generated by your investment property to determine eligibility. This means you can secure funding even if your income doesn’t fit the typical mold. With our expertise at Launch Financial Group, you can maximize leverage on your investments while keeping your financing flexible.

Common Questions

DSCR loans, or Debt Service Coverage Ratio loans, are financing options that focus on the income generated by an investment property rather than the borrower's personal income.

Real estate investors who are looking to purchase, renovate, or manage rental properties can benefit greatly from investment property loans.

Hard money loans are ideal for short-term funding needs such as fix and flip projects, rental property acquisitions, or urgent cash requirements.

Bridge loans require an assessment of the property's value and the investor's plan for the asset, focusing more on the asset rather than personal income.

Bank statement loans allow self-employed individuals to qualify based on their bank deposits rather than traditional income documentation like W-2s.
