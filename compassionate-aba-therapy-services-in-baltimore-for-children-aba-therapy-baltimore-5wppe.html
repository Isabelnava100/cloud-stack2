Frequently Asked Questions

ABA therapy, or Applied Behavior Analysis, is an evidence-based approach used to improve specific behaviors while teaching new skills. It is commonly used to support children with autism.

ABA therapy can help your child develop communication skills, social skills, daily living skills, and improve overall behavior. Each program is tailored to meet the unique needs of your child.

Wellness Wave ABA provides services in Baltimore City & County, Howard County, and surrounding Maryland areas, offering both in-home and in-center therapy options.

Our team consists of certified Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) who are trained to deliver high-quality ABA therapy.

To get started, simply contact us to schedule a consultation. We will discuss your child's needs and develop a personalized therapy plan.
