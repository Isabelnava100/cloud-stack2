What is Skin Needling?

Skin needling, also known as microneedling or collagen induction therapy, is a minimally invasive treatment that stimulates the body's own collagen production. This innovative technique involves using fine needles to create micro-injuries in the skin, which triggers the healing process and leads to improved skin texture and reduction of scars. At Vita Felice Medi Spa, we tailor each session to target your unique scar concerns, ensuring optimal results for acne scars, burn scars, and more. Experience the transformative effects of skin needling in a safe, caring environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

We treat a variety of scars including acne scars, burn scars, surgical scars, and stretch marks using skin needling techniques.
