Paintless dent repair (PDR) is a specialized method of removing minor dents from the body of a vehicle without the need for traditional bodywork or painting. This technique involves using specially designed tools to manipulate the metal back to its original shape, preserving the factory finish. PDR is a cost-effective and time-efficient solution, making it ideal for auto hail repair services. At Hail Strategies, we utilize PDR to restore your vehicle's appearance while maintaining its value.
