Signs Your Air Conditioning Needs Repair

Recognizing the signs that your air conditioning system requires repair is crucial for maintaining your comfort. If you notice unusual sounds, fluctuating temperatures, or a rise in energy bills, your AC may need a professional check-up and air conditioning repair. It's always better to address minor issues before they escalate into major malfunctions. At Hancock & Son Plumbing, Heating and Air Conditioning, our technicians are trained to identify and resolve these problems promptly, ensuring your home stays cool and comfortable when it matters most.

How to Maintain Your Air Conditioning System

To extend the life of your air conditioning system, regular maintenance is essential. Simple tasks like changing filters, cleaning condenser coils, and scheduling annual professional inspections can make a significant difference. At Hancock & Son Plumbing, Heating and Air Conditioning, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans designed to keep your AC running efficiently year-round. By investing in routine care, you can avoid unexpected air conditioning repair and ensure optimal cooling performance for your home.
