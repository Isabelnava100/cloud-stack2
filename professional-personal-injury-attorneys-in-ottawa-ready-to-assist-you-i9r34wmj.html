How are the fees structured for personal injury lawyers?
A number of personal injury lawyers work on a contingency-based model, implying they only receive payment if you successfully win your case, thus ensuring access to legal representation without the need for upfront costs.

What happens if I am partially responsible for the accident?
In Ontario, you can still be eligible for compensation even if you share some blame for the accident. However, the amount awarded may decrease in relation to your assigned level of fault.
