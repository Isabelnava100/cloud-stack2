What maintenance services do you offer for air conditioning systems?
Custom Air Systems offers comprehensive maintenance services for air conditioning systems that are vital for their longevity and efficiency. Our maintenance plans typically include cleaning components, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting electrical connections, and testing the thermostat. These services help to prevent breakdowns and ensure that your system operates at peak efficiency. Regular maintenance can prolong the life of your air conditioning unit and save you on energy costs. We recommend scheduling routine checks to keep your system running smoothly.

What should I do if my air conditioning system is not working properly?
If your air conditioning system is not functioning correctly, the first step is to check the basics—ensure that your thermostat is set to the cooling mode and that the circuit breaker hasn’t tripped. If everything seems normal, it’s time to contact Custom Air Systems for professional assistance. Our expert technicians can provide diagnostic and repair services efficiently. Don't wait until the problem escalates; reach out to us for prompt and reliable air conditioning services. We're here to ensure your comfort returns quickly.
