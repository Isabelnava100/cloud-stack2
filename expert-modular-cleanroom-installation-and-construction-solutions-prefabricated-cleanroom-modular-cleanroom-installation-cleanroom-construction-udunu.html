Understanding ISO Cleanroom Classifications

ISO classification standards are critical when designing a cleanroom environment. ISO 5 to ISO 8 cleanrooms present unique requirements that ensure controlled environments for sensitive processes. The differences between these classifications impact HVAC design, air cleanliness, and operational procedures. Identifying the required ISO class early in the planning stages can lead to better decision-making regarding material selection, equipment choice, and compliance processes.
