Our Comprehensive Appraisal Services

Divorce Appraisal Services

Our divorce appraisal services focus on providing neutral and accurate valuations of real estate assets involved in property division during divorce proceedings. We ensure compliance with USPAP standards, yielding reports that are reliable and admissible in court, educating attorneys and clients on the process of equitable distribution. Our dedication to transparency and communication means that our clients receive timely evaluations, along with responsive support from our licensed appraiser. With expertise in both Connecticut and Florida, we understand the unique dynamics of divorces across jurisdictions, ensuring a smooth process with no room for ambiguity.

Tax Appraisal Services

Heritage Valuation Group offers tax appraisal services to accurately determine the market value of properties for tax purposes. Our appraisals are structured to meet IRS compliance requirements, ensuring they withstand audits and inquiries from tax authorities. Whether you're dealing with property taxes, estate taxes, or other tax-related situations, we provide detailed and defensible appraisal reports. Engaging with our highly qualified team guarantees that the process is seamless, informative, and conducted in an efficient manner—particularly crucial for estate tax calculations upon the date of death.

Estate Appraisal Services

Our reports include in-depth market analyses and valuation methods that bring clarity to this often complex process. With offices in both Connecticut and Florida, we leverage extensive market knowledge to support our clients in crafting estate plans and navigating property distributions.
