What is conversion rate optimization?
Conversion rate optimization (CRO) is the process of increasing the percentage of visitors to a website that takes a desired action, such as making a purchase or signing up for a newsletter. This involves analyzing user behavior, testing various elements (like layouts and calls to action), and making data-driven decisions to improve the user experience. At Edgewood Solutions, we employ CRO techniques to ensure that your website converts visitors effectively, thus maximizing your return on investment.
