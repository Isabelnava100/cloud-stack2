Gunite Pool Renovation Methodology

Structural Gunite Repairs

The foundation of our gunite pool renovations begins with important structural repairs to maintain your pool's integrity. This includes wall restoration, main drain replacements, and adjustments to the pool depth as required. Our skilled craftsmen prioritize superior construction techniques suiting New England’s climatic challenges. With our detailed attention, your pool will gain a strong base capable of lasting for years.

Resurfacing and Final Finishes

After the structural work is finalized, we proceed with resurfacing your pool using options like traditional plaster, quartz, or pebble finishes. Each selection is made based on visual appeal and robust durability, ensuring your pool stays both beautiful and functional. We also add new tile and coping that match your backyard's aesthetics, creating a cohesive look for your pool surroundings. Homeowners throughout Essex County appreciate our commitment to excellence in every aspect.

Decking Enhancements and Surrounding Areas

For a comprehensive gunite pool renovation, at Affordable Pool Repair, we are specialists in refreshing the surrounding decking. Our offerings include a variety of materials like stamped concrete, pavers, and travertine, all tailored to boost safety and appearance. This meticulous finishing touch revamps your outdoor environment, crafting a welcoming space for gatherings and relaxation. Our team guarantees a seamless integration of the enhanced pool area and the new decking for improved curbside attractiveness.
