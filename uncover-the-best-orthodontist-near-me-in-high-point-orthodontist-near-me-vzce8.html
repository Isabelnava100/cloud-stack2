Invisalign offers several advantages over traditional braces. The clear aligners are virtually invisible, making them a popular choice for adults and teens who want a discreet treatment option. They are also removable, allowing for easier cleaning and maintenance of oral hygiene. Additionally, Invisalign aligners are custom-made for your teeth, providing a comfortable fit and effective results in straightening your smile.

The American Association of Orthodontists recommends that children have their first orthodontic evaluation by age 7. At this age, our orthodontists can identify any potential issues and determine if early intervention is necessary. What should I expect during my orthodontic appointments?
During your orthodontic appointments, our team will monitor your progress and make any necessary adjustments to your braces or aligners. For traditional braces, this may involve tightening wires or replacing bands. For Invisalign, we will provide you with your next set of aligners. Appointments typically last 20 to 30 minutes, and we recommend visiting us every 4 to 8 weeks to ensure your treatment stays on track.
