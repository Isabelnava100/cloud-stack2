What if the accident was partly my fault?
Louisiana follows a comparative fault law, which means you may still be eligible for compensation even if you share some of the responsibility for the accident.

How much compensation can I expect for my injury case?
The compensation amount varies based on factors such as the severity of the injury, medical expenses, lost wages, and the impact on your lifestyle. We'll assess your case to provide an estimate.
