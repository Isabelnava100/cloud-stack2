The Vision Behind B2T Ministries

B2T Ministries, led by Rick Rene, seeks to create a sanctuary for believers who are eager to learn about biblical teachings and American liberty. Our vision centers on empowering members with the truth found in God’s Word, equipping them to stand firm in their faith amidst societal challenges. We believe that through prophetic teaching, Christians can illuminate the path towards righteousness and perseverance.

FaithNFreedom: A Community for the Bold

As part of the FaithNFreedom network, B2T Ministries provides a unique community platform where believers can interact freely. We prioritize discussions on faith and freedom, encouraging members to share their testimonies and insights without censorship. In this vibrant community, our members find strength in unity and support one another in navigating life’s challenges while standing for biblical truths.

The B2T Show: Insights and Inspiration

The B2T Show, hosted by Rick Rene, is a cornerstone of our ministry, delivering powerful content to inspire and educate viewers. Here, we delve into biblical teaching, current events, and prophetic insights that resonate with the spirit of Christian patriotism. Each episode serves as a beacon of truth, urging believers to examine the world through a biblical lens and fostering a bold stand for faith and freedom.
