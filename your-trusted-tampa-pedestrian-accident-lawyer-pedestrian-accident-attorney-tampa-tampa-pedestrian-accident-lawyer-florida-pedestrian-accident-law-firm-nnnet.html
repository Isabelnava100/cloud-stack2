Extra FAQ

What does working on a contingency fee basis mean?

A contingency fee basis indicates that you will not incur attorney fees unless we successfully win your case. This model enables you to pursue justice without upfront financial obligations.

Is it mandatory to go to court for my case?

Not necessarily. Numerous cases are settled through negotiations with insurance carriers. However, should a fair settlement be unattainable, we are prepared to present your case in court.
