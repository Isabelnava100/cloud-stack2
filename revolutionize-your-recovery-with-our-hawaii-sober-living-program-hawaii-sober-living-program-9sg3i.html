Revolutionize Your Recovery with Our Hawaii Sober Living Program

Welcome to Exclusive Hawaii Rehab, where our innovative Hawaii sober living program can transform your recovery experience. We are dedicated to supporting individuals overcoming SUD, facilitating a smooth transition into a sober lifestyle. Our sober living arrangements, whether communal or private, promote a sense of community and accountability. Participants will engage in skill development, establish healthy routines, and rediscover their self-esteem throughout the process. You'll benefit from access to professional mental health guidance and supportive peer networks. If you’ve recently completed inpatient rehabilitation, our sober living program serves as a pivotal step in maintaining your sobriety and preparing for life ahead.

 

Founded in 2008, we emphasize non-12 step recovery approaches for Substance Use Disorders in a tranquil and inspiring setting on the Hamakua Coast of the Big Island. Our accredited facility is staffed with compassionate professionals, including licensed medical and naturopathic doctors focusing on holistic healing approaches. Begin your path of transformation by addressing addiction and mental health challenges with us.

What role do families play in the recovery process?
Family involvement is vital at Exclusive Hawaii Rehab as it contributes positively to a client’s recovery journey. We provide family therapy sessions and informative resources designed to engage family members in the treatment process. Creating a robust support system enhances the likelihood of sustained recovery for our clients.

Embarking on your healing journey with Exclusive Hawaii Rehab is easy! You can contact our admissions team through our website or by phone, and we will guide you through the next steps.
