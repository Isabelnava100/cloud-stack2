Which Equipment is Best Suited for Large-Scale Property Maintenance Rentals?

For numerous property managers and owners, opting to rent equipment for extensive maintenance tasks rather than making a purchase is the more sensible choice. The optimal rental approach depends on the scale of the project, the conditions at the worksite, and the anticipated duration of equipment usage. Renting generally proves to be more economical if equipment will be needed for less than 8 months annually, and having on-site storage can significantly influence a project's operational efficiency.
