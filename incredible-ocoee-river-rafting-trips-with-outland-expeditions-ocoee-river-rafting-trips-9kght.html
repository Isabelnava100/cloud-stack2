What are the age and health requirements for rafting on the Ocoee?
At Outland Expeditions, we welcome adventurers of all ages for our Ocoee River rafting trips. Generally, participants should be at least 12 years old to join the rafting experience. However, younger children may be allowed on certain trips with parental consent. As for health requirements, participants should be in good physical condition and able to follow safety instructions. If you have any specific health concerns, please consult with our team before booking to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
