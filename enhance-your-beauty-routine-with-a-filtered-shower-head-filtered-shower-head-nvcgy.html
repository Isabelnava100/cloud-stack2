How often should I replace my shower filter?
To ensure optimal performance of your filtered shower head, it is recommended to replace the filter every 6 to 12 months, depending on your water quality and usage. Regular replacement helps maintain effective filtration, ensuring you continue to enjoy purified water for skincare. Some users may notice decreased water flow as a sign that it’s time to replace the filter, so keeping track of your filter change schedule will guarantee you always shower with the best possible water.
