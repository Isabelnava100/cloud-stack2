Experience Modern Southern Comfort Food at Blueberry’s Grill

Welcome to Blueberry’s Grill, where we serve up the finest modern Southern comfort food with a creative twist. Located in the heart of North Myrtle Beach, our farm-to-table breakfast restaurant invites you to indulge in a vibrant Southern-style brunch experience. From classic breakfast favorites to innovative brunch dishes, we take pride in delivering a culinary journey that pays homage to the rich traditions of Southern cuisine. Bring your friends and family to enjoy a meal crafted with fresh, local ingredients and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. At Blueberry’s Grill, we believe that good food brings people together. Our menu is a celebration of Southern flavors merged with Mediterranean influences, perfect for anyone looking to enjoy a delicious farm-to-table breakfast. Whether you're craving our famous Chicken & Waffles or a lighter Fit & Healthy Avocado Toast, there’s something for everyone at our beloved Southern-style brunch spot. Join us and discover why Blueberry’s Grill is a must-visit dining experience in the Carolinas!

Delicious Offerings at Blueberry's Grill

Southern-Inspired Breakfast Classics

Start your day at Blueberry’s Grill with our Southern-inspired breakfast classics. Enjoy our famous Chicken & Waffles, drizzled with maple syrup and dusted with powdered sugar. Each dish is made with farm-fresh eggs and quality ingredients that are sure to satisfy your cravings. Other fan favorites include our indulgent Bourbon Pecan Banana French Toast and the Blueberry Crème Brûlée French Toast – perfect for sweet lovers! Join us for a delightful morning meal that captures the essence of modern Southern comfort food.

Healthy and Wholesome Options

At Blueberry's Grill, we cater to health-conscious diners with a range of fresh and wholesome farm-to-table breakfast options. Indulge in our Fit & Healthy Avocado Toast, packed with nutrients and flavor. Our protein-packed bowls and gluten-friendly alternatives, including gluten-free bread and pancakes, ensure everyone can enjoy a delicious meal. We emphasize the importance of fresh ingredients while keeping your dietary preferences in mind. Everyone deserves to enjoy a memorable meal!

Vibrant Brunch Cocktails

Enjoy an unparalleled brunch experience at Blueberry’s Grill with our selection of vibrant brunch cocktails! Sip on classic Mimosas that complement our delightful dishes, or create your own Bloody Mary with our house-made garnishes, including candied bacon. If you’re feeling adventurous, our popular Mimosa Flights offer a taste of various flavors in a fun, refreshing format. Pair these with our Southern-style brunch offerings for an unforgettable dining experience surrounded by friends and family.
