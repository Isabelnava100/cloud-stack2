Caring for Your Jewelry

To maintain the beauty and integrity of your jewelry from Forever in Harmony, proper care is essential. We recommend storing your pieces in a soft-lined jewelry box or pouch when not in use to prevent scratches. Regularly clean your jewelry with a soft cloth to remove any dirt or oils, ensuring it stays sparkling. Additionally, avoid exposing your jewelry to harsh chemicals or excessive moisture by removing them before swimming or using cleaning supplies for the best longevity.
