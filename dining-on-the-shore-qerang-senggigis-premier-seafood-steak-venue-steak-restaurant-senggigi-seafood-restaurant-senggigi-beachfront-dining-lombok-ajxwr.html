Dining on the Shore: Qerang, Senggigi’s Premier Seafood & Steak Venue

Qerang is an esteemed seaside seafood and steak venue located in Senggigi, Lombok, celebrated for its top-notch fresh seafood, succulent Aussie Angus beef steaks, and enchanting sunset meals right along Mangsit Beach. Visitors come to Qerang to relish grilled lobsters, diverse seafood delicacies, and expertly crafted steaks, all delivered in an exquisite open-air environment by the ocean.

Discover Why Qerang Stands Out as Senggigi's Go-To Seafood & Steak Venue

Locally Sourced Seafood from the Waters of Lombok

The culinary offerings at Qerang include freshly grilled fish, lobsters, prawns, and oysters, in addition to sumptuous seafood platters crafted fresh daily for an outstanding dining experience along the beachfront in Senggigi.

Exquisite Australian Beef Steaks Beneath the Waves

Qerang prides itself on presenting top-quality Australian beef steaks that are skillfully cooked and served in a beautiful open-air atmosphere, solidifying its reputation as a sought-after steak restaurant in Senggigi.

Captivating Sunset Dining Experiences at Mangsit Beach

A romantic setting bolstered by candlelit tables, the refreshing sea breeze, and the dreamy glow of sunset makes for an enchanting dining experience in Lombok. It's a favored destination for couples and significant celebrations every night.
