Experience the Best Surf Camp in Morocco at Salt House

Welcome to Salt House Morocco, your premier surf camp nestled in the beautiful Tamraght, Taghazout Bay. Established in 2017 by El Yazid Kanane, our surf camp is proudly Moroccan-owned, ensuring an authentic experience that connects you with the vibrant local surf culture. At our camp, we provide personalized coaching in small groups, ensuring individualized attention that supports your progress and growth as a surfer. Our community-driven atmosphere pairs perfectly with daily yoga sessions and delicious, home-cooked Moroccan meals, making your stay both enriching and memorable. Immerse yourself in the surf life as you enjoy breathtaking ocean views from our hilltop retreat. Whether you’re a seasoned surfer looking to refine your skills or a beginner eager to catch your first wave, Salt House Morocco offers tailored packages to meet your needs in a laid-back but professional environment. Our experienced ISA-certified coaches focus on your unique surfing journey while also ensuring you enjoy the unmatched beauty of Morocco’s coastline. Join us for a surf adventure that balances exhilaration with relaxation in the heart of Taghazout.

Small-Group Coaching and Personalized Attention

At Salt House Morocco, we believe in quality-over-quantity when it comes to surfing instruction. By limiting our coaching sessions to a maximum of six surfers per coach, we ensure that each guest receives personalized attention and guidance on their surf journey. Our small group sizes foster a supportive learning environment where surfers can progress confidently while forming friendships with fellow participants. Enjoy tailored coaching sessions that adapt to your skill level, along with video analysis to track your improvements and refine your techniques in a professional yet relaxed atmosphere.

Surf and Wellness Packages for All Levels

Our diverse surf and wellness packages cater to surfers of all levels, from beginners to advanced riders. Each package is designed to provide a well-rounded experience, blending surf coaching with daily yoga sessions and relaxation time. With delicious Moroccan breakfasts, beach picnics, and local dinners included, you’ll be nourished both in body and spirit.
