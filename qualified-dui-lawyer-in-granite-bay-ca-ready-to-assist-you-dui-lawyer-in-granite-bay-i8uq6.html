Will a DUI conviction affect my record permanently?
Yes, in California, a DUI conviction generally remains on your record for ten years, impacting your insurance premiums and driving privileges.

How do DUI and DWI differ?
While both pertain to impaired operation of vehicles, a DUI indicates driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs, whereas the DWI, often used in some states, exclusively emphasizes alcohol.
