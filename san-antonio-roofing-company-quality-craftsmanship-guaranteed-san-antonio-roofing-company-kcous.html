How do I know if I need a roof replacement?
Determining the need for a roof replacement can involve inspecting for several signs. If you notice curling, missing shingles, leaks, or significant wear, it may be time to consult a professional. Schulte Roofing can conduct a thorough evaluation to assess the condition of your roof. Our experts will provide honest recommendations on whether repairs suffice or if a complete replacement is necessary for your home or business.
