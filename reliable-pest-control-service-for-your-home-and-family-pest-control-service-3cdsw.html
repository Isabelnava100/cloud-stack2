How often should I schedule pest control services?
The frequency of pest control services depends on several factors, including the type of pests you are dealing with, the severity of the infestation, and your specific property conditions. For general pest prevention, we recommend scheduling services at least once every three months. However, if you are experiencing a significant pest issue, more frequent treatments may be necessary. Our pest control experts can provide personalized recommendations based on your situation, ensuring that your home remains pest-free throughout the year.

What should I do before a pest control service appointment?
Before your pest control service appointment with Pest Control Now, there are a few steps you can take to prepare your home for the best results. First, clear any clutter from areas where pests are commonly found, such as kitchens, basements, and attics. This will allow our pest control experts to access these areas easily. Additionally, ensure that pets are secured in a safe location away from the treatment area. Finally, if you have any specific concerns or observations about the pest problem, please share them with our team during the appointment to help us tailor our approach effectively.
