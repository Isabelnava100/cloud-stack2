Recognizing Signs of Hail Damage on Your Roof

Hail damage can be subtle and hard to detect, but knowing the signs can save you from more extensive repairs later. Look for shingles that are cracked, have missing granules, or appear dented. In addition, interior leaks or water stains may indicate roof damage. It's essential to have a professional inspect your roof after a hailstorm since they can detect issues that might be overlooked by an untrained eye.

How Hail Affects Roofs and What to Do

When hail impacts your roof, it can cause a range of issues. It may dislodge shingles, compromise flashing, and even lead to leaks. If severe damage occurs, a prompt roof replacement may be necessary to protect your home. Our team at Pearson Family Roofing can provide a thorough inspection and help you understand your options regarding repair or replacement after hail damage.
