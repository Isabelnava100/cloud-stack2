What is the frequency of visits to the orthodontist?
Usually, you will be asked to come in for adjustments every 4-6 weeks. These visits help us monitor your progress and make any necessary modifications to your care plan.

Does undergoing orthodontic treatment involve pain?
Patients may experience some level of discomfort post-adjustment, though this is generally minimal and can be managed with over-the-counter medication.
