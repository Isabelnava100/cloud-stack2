What are Zygomatic Implants?

Zygomatic implants are innovative solutions designed specifically for patients experiencing severe bone loss. Unlike traditional dental implants that rely on the jawbone, zygomatic implants anchor into the zygomatic bone, providing a secure and lasting alternative. This technique is ideal for patients who have been told they aren't candidates for standard implants due to insufficient bone density. With zygomatic implants, patients can achieve immediate restoration of their smiles without the need for extensive bone grafting procedures.
