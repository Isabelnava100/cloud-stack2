Grasping the Concept of Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Dual diagnosis treatment simultaneously tackles disorders relating to mental health and substance use. This cohesive methodology is vital for successful recovery, as these issues can often worsen each other. Our skilled team conducts thorough evaluations to develop personalized treatment strategies that cater to distinctive individual requirements. By concentrating on both challenges, we pave a comprehensive journey towards recovery that prioritizes holistic wellness.

Key Programs We Provide

At Kingdom Recovery Center, we have a variety of programs designed to meet the diverse requirements of recovery. Our Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP) deliver intensive daily assistance while permitting patients to rest at home each evening. For individuals who require more adaptable solutions, our Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) furnish ongoing care without interrupting daily activities. Each program is enriched with evidence-based therapeutic interventions tailored to optimize recovery results.

Research-Backed Therapies for Healing

Our therapeutic framework is founded on research-driven methods such as Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT), Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and EMDR. These approaches have consistently proven effective in managing co-occurring conditions, equipping individuals with vital tools to navigate their experiences. Accelerated resolution therapy is also a notable component that aids in emotional healing and processing. We are committed to empowering our patients with robust coping mechanisms that support lasting recovery.
