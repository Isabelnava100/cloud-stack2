How can I prevent kickback when using a chainsaw?
Using a chainsaw with a low kickback chain, maintaining proper cutting angles, and staying aware of your surroundings can help prevent kickback.

What maintenance do chainsaws need?
Chainsaws require regular oiling, chain sharpening, filter cleaning, and spark plug checks to ensure they operate safely and efficiently.
