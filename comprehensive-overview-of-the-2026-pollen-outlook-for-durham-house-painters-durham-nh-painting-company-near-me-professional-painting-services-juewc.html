Impact of Pollen on Paint Adhesion

Particles from pollen can accumulate on newly applied paint surfaces, forming a layer that hinders the paint's ability to adhere effectively. Over time, this can lead to issues such as flaking, bubbling, or peeling, resulting in an unsatisfactory paint job. Many homeowners fail to account for this problem when planning their painting schedules, leading to expensive correction work later. At Handymasters Painting Co., we recognize the significance of choosing the right time for your project to ensure a lasting finish, even with pollen in the air.
