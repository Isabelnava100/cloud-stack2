What are exterior drainage systems, and why are they necessary?
Exterior drainage systems are designed to redirect surface water away from your home’s foundation. They often include French drains, downspout extensions, and grading. These systems are necessary to prevent water accumulation around your foundation, which can lead to damage over time. Correctly installed exterior drainage systems protect your property from moisture intrusion.
