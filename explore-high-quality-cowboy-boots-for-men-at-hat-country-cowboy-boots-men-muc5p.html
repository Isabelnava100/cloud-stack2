How can I maintain my cowboy hat?
To maintain your cowboy hat's quality and appearance, regular cleaning and proper storage are essential. Use a soft brush to remove dust and dirt, and if necessary, spot-clean with a damp cloth. Avoid exposing your hat to extreme weather conditions and store it in a cool, dry place to prevent damage. Consider using a hat box for storage to keep its shape.
