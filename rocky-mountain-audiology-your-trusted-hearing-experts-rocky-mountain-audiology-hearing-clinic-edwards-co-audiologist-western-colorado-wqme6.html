Hearing Care Services

Comprehensive Hearing Evaluations

Our audiologists perform thorough hearing evaluations to assess your hearing loss and identify the best personalized treatment options available. Using state-of-the-art technology, we ensure accurate diagnostics to better understand your unique hearing needs. We prioritize open communication, discussing results with you and providing a clear path forward. This patient-centric approach helps you feel informed and confident in choosing the right hearing care solutions. Once your evaluation is complete, our team will create a tailored hearing care plan, whether that involves hearing aids, tinnitus management, or custom hearing protections. Our goal is to help you hear life again, reconnect with family, and engage in social activities. With ongoing support and follow-up, we are committed to making sure your hearing health journey is seamless and successful.

Tailored Hearing Aid Solutions

Choosing the right hearing aid is an essential part of restoring your auditory experience. At Rocky Mountain Audiology, we offer a wide range of modern hearing aids designed to fit your lifestyle and preferences. Our Board Certified audiologists work closely with you to provide a fitting experience that ensures comfort and optimal functionality. We provide thorough consultations so you can understand the different models and their features. Once fitted, we are here to assist you with adjustments, reprogramming, repairs, and answering any questions about batteries or maintenance. Our community-centered approach emphasizes building long-term relationships with our patients, ensuring continued support as your hearing needs change over time.

Tinnitus Management and Treatment

Tinnitus can be a frustrating and challenging condition to cope with, but Rocky Mountain Audiology is here to help. Our experienced audiologists conduct detailed evaluations to determine the cause and impact of your tinnitus. We offer bespoke management plans, including sound therapy and counseling, which can significantly alleviate symptoms and improve your daily life. In addition to our therapeutic options, we educate our patients about tinnitus and provide support to help them adapt. Our commitment to community well-being drives us to stay informed on the latest research and treatment options, ensuring that our patients receive the best care possible. We believe in empowering our patients, giving them the tools and resources they need to manage their condition effectively.
