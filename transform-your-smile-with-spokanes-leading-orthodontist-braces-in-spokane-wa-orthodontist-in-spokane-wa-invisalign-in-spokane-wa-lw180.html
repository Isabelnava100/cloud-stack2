Exploring Orthodontic Options at Wire Orthodontics

Braces for All Ages

At Wire Orthodontics, we provide a range of braces options tailored to meet the needs of patients of all ages. Our traditional metal braces are effective and durable, while ceramic braces offer a more discreet option for those who prefer a subtler look. Our orthodontic team will work closely with you to determine the best type of braces that will align with your lifestyle and treatment goals.

Invisalign: Clear Path to a Stunning Smile

If you’re looking for a flexible and discreet orthodontic option, Invisalign is the ideal choice for you. At Wire Orthodontics, we utilize advanced technology to create custom clear aligners specifically for your unique dental structure. Let us help you discover how Invisalign in Spokane WA can transform your smile without the appearance of traditional braces.

Extra FAQ

What age should my child see an orthodontist?

The American Association of Orthodontists recommends that children have their first orthodontic evaluation by age 7 years.

Can adults get braces or Invisalign?

Absolutely! We serve patients of all ages, and both braces and Invisalign can be effective options for adults seeking orthodontic treatment.
