The Importance of Upgrading Your Electrical Panel

Initiating an electrical panel upgrade is crucial for your property's safety and performance. With advances in electrical technology and increasing consumption, outdated panels may struggle to meet the requirements of contemporary devices. Elevating your panel can prevent hazardous circuit overloads while improving energy distribution within your establishment. A modern panel equips you to handle future energy demands, all while avoiding potential costly repairs.

Indicators That You Need an Upgrade

Identifying the right time for an electrical panel upgrade is integral for your safety. If you frequently experience tripped breakers, see flickering lights, or detect char marks near outlets, it's recommended to reach out to a specialist. Moreover, if your home still relies on a panel that's 20 years old or older, it strongly suggests the need to meet your current electrical load demands. Our experts at RJ & Son Electric are equipped to assess your system and propose necessary upgrades.
