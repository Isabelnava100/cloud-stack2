Explore Our Custom Lace Products and Quality Lace Products

At Braids and Laces Limited, we specialize in high-quality lace products that meet the diverse needs of our clients across various industries. Founded in 1936, our fourth-generation Canadian company combines craftsmanship with advanced technology to create durable and reliable lace solutions. Our custom lace products are designed to provide high performance in demanding environments, ensuring safety and efficiency for your applications. We pride ourselves on our responsive service and expertise in developing tailored solutions for every requirement. Our facility in Cannington, Ontario, is a hub of innovation where we manufacture a comprehensive range of lace products. From skate laces to specialized cords and twines, our offerings exemplify both quality and versatility. We focus on using premium raw materials, including high-modulus polyethylene and nylon, to ensure that our laces withstand harsh conditions. At Braids and Laces, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional lace products custom made to fit specific needs, whether it's for industrial applications or recreational activities.
