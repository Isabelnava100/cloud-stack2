What Is the Holosun 510c?

The Holosun 510c functions as an open-reflex red dot sight tailored for both rifles and carbines. Unlike standard tube-style red dots, the 510c offers a considerably broader window area, enhancing viewing capability and speeding up target locking. Its assembly includes sturdy aluminum construction, a variety of reticle options, and Holosun’s renowned Solar Failsafe feature.

Outstanding Features Overview

  • Open Reflex Orthogonal – Wider sight field facilitating fast target engagement.

  • Multi-Reticle System (MRS) – Toggle between a 2 MOA dot, 65 MOA circle, or a dual-system.

  • Solar Power Resilience – Automatically harnesses solar energy when batteries are depleted.

  • Shake Activated Functionality – Saves battery by turning on solely when motion is detected.

  • Battery Longevity of 50,000 Hours – Operates for years with a single CR2032 battery.

  • Shielding by Titanium Hood – Enhanced protection against impacts and unexpected drops.

Brightness Adjustability

Innovative Solar Failsafe System

Holosun’s Solar Failsafe assures dependable functionality. If the battery fails operative activity, the scope seamlessly taps into solar power to maintain a vivid reticle, so vital moments aren’t missed.

Motion Activation Technology

Forget the mundane task of switching the optic on or off manually; the 510c responds to movement for activation. After moments without motion, it enters sleep mode to prevent battery drain while powering back instantly at any sign of movement.

Battery Runtime with CR2032

Featuring extraordinary lifespan close to 50,000 hours using just one battery (in mid-setting), the 510c has significantly better performance versus other options availiable. It endures for up to almost five years, even if stayed powered continuously.
