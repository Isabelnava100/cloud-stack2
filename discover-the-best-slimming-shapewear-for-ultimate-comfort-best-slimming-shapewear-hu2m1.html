How do I care for my Esteem Apparel compression shirt?
Caring for your Esteem Apparel compression shirt is easy! We recommend machine washing in cold water on a gentle cycle and air drying to maintain the elasticity and quality of the fabric. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the material and diminish the shirt’s effectiveness. Proper care will ensure that your compression shirt provides lasting comfort and support while maintaining its best slimming appearance.
