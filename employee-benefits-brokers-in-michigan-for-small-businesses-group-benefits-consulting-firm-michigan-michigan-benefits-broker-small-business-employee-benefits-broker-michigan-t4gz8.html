Understanding ICHRA and QSEHRA Solutions

At CFH Insurance Consultants, we empower Michigan small businesses by offering innovative solutions like Individual Coverage Health Reimbursement Arrangements (ICHRA) and Qualified Small Employer Health Reimbursement Arrangements (QSEHRA). These flexible and customizable options allow employers to provide health coverage that meets the unique needs of their employees. With ICHRA and QSEHRA, you can structure your benefits in a way that supports individual health needs while managing costs effectively. Our team will work closely with you to develop a strategy that maximizes the advantages of these modern benefits solutions.
