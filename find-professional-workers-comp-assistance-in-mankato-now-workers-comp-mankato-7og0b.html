Comprehending Workers Comp in Mankato

Defining Workers Compensation

Workers’ compensation is a type of insurance that delivers financial support and medical care for employees who sustain injuries while performing their job duties. This system serves as a shield for both workers and employers, allowing individuals to receive compensation without needing to prove fault. In Mankato, the workers' compensation framework is tailored to ensure that injured employees can access critical medical treatment and essential financial assistance during their convalescence. At Harvey & Carpenter, we are here to help you navigate this important process, making sure you are fully aware of your rights and options available.

The journey of submitting a workers' compensation claim in Mankato consists of key steps necessary for achieving success. Start by reporting your injury to your employer as soon as it happens.
