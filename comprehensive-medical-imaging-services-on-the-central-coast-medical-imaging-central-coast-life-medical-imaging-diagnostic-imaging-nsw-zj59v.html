Comprehensive Medical Imaging Services on the Central Coast

At Life Medical Imaging, we pride ourselves on being the premier choice for medical imaging in the Central Coast of NSW. Our independent clinics in Bateau Bay, Killarney Vale, Umina Beach, and Erina are equipped with state-of-the-art, low-dose imaging technology, making advanced diagnostics accessible and safe for our community. With NATA accreditation and a team of subspecialist experts in women’s health, cardiac, and paediatric imaging, we ensure that our patients receive exceptional care in a welcoming environment. Our commitment to bulk-billing for most Medicare-eligible scans further supports our mission of delivering high-quality, affordable imaging services to all.

Advanced Imaging Technology

Life Medical Imaging utilizes state-of-the-art low-dose imaging technologies to ensure superior diagnostic quality while minimizing patient exposure. Our advanced CT equipment, including cardiac CT and coronary angiography, allows us to offer precise imaging solutions that cater to diverse patient needs. With a focus on diagnostic accuracy, our clinics are equipped to perform a wide range of imaging services, including digital X-rays, ultrasounds, and DEXA scans for bone density assessments. We are committed to providing the community with immediate access to the latest advancements in medical imaging.

Comprehensive Imaging Services

Our comprehensive range of services at Life Medical Imaging offers patients everything from low-dose CT scans to specialized ultrasounds tailored to women’s health, cardiac assessments, and paediatric needs. We provide diagnostic imaging that is both extensive and precise, including obstetric and gynaecological ultrasounds, vascular and musculoskeletal assessments, as well as dental OPG and image-guided procedures. Our clinics are equipped to handle diagnostic challenges, ensuring that referring GPs and specialists have the support they need for their patients.

Patient-Centric Approach

Life Medical Imaging believes in a patient-first approach, making it our priority to create a welcoming and convenient experience for those seeking medical imaging services. Our flexible booking options ensure easy access for patients, whether they choose to schedule appointments by phone, online, or via SMS. We understand the importance of timely diagnostics and work closely with referrers, ensuring seamless communication and support for patients and healthcare providers alike. Our commitment to affordability through bulk-billing for Medicare-eligible scans underscores our dedication to serving the Central Coast community.
