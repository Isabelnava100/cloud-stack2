Sheet Metal HVAC Parts Overview

Types of Sheet Metal HVAC Parts

In the HVAC industry, there are several types of sheet metal parts commonly used. Ductwork is essential for transporting air throughout a building, while fittings help to connect various sections and allow for efficient airflow. Additionally, custom sheet metal components can be designed for specific needs, ensuring the right fit for any application. Understanding these components can aid in optimizing HVAC system performance and efficiency.
