Understanding BHRT

Bioidentical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT) is a specialized treatment designed to restore hormonal balance in women experiencing menopause. Unlike conventional hormone therapies, BHRT uses hormones that are chemically identical to those the body naturally produces, providing a more compatible option for many women.
