How do I care for my DTF printed shirts?
Caring for your DTF printed shirts is essential to maintain their vibrant colors and ensure long-lasting wear. We recommend washing your shirts inside out on a cold cycle and avoiding bleach to protect the print. Tumble drying should be done on a low setting or, preferably, air drying to extend the life of the fabric and the print. If ironing is necessary, make sure to do so on the reverse side of the shirt to prevent direct heat on the DTF transfer. Following these simple care instructions will help keep your custom DTF shirts looking fresh and vibrant.
