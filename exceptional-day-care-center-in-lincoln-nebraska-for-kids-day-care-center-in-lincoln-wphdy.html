Child Development Programs at Hamilton Heights CDC

Learning Through Play: Our Core Philosophy

At Hamilton Heights Child Development Center, we adopt the philosophy that children learn best through play. Our programs are designed to stimulate curiosity and make learning an enjoyable experience. This approach encourages independence and allows children to explore and understand their surroundings effectively. By integrating play into our curriculum, we promote cognitive, emotional, and social development in a fun environment.
