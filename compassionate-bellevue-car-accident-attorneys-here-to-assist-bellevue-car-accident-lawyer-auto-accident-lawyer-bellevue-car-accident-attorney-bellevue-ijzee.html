Steps to Take After a Car Accident in Bellevue

Essential Actions Following an Accident

Following an auto accident, prioritize your safety along with that of others involved. If necessary, dial 911 to access medical services. Gather thorough documentation of the accident scene by capturing photographs of vehicles, injuries, and environmental conditions. Exchange contact and insurance particulars with the other driver, and collect witness details. Above all, seek guidance from a Bellevue car accident lawyer to clarify your rights and determine the subsequent actions required for filing a compensation claim.
