How can I prepare my property for a pressure washing service?
To prepare your property for pressure washing, we recommend clearing any outdoor furniture, decorations, and vehicles away from the areas that will be cleaned. This ensures that our technicians can access all parts of the house exterior easily and perform efficient work. Furthermore, if you have delicate plants or landscaping near the cleaning area, it's a good idea to cover or move them to prevent damage. If you have specific concerns about the surfaces being cleaned, please communicate these with us beforehand so we can tailor our approach effectively.
