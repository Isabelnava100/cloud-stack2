Trauma-Informed Care and Therapy Resources

Understanding Trauma-Informed Therapy

Trauma-informed therapy is an approach that acknowledges the impact of trauma on an individual's life, emphasizing safety, trust, and empowerment. It is essential for clients to feel understood and supported as they share their experiences. By incorporating principles of trauma-informed care, our therapists provide a holistic framework that guides treatment. This process empowers clients to regain control over their narratives and fosters resilience in overcoming their challenges.

Benefits of Evidence-Based Trauma Therapy

At Harbor Point, we utilize evidence-based trauma therapy methods, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT). These therapies have been shown to effectively address the symptoms of trauma and help clients develop coping strategies. Our licensed clinicians work collaboratively with clients to create personalized treatment plans that focus on their individual goals and values. The result is a tailored approach that helps clients achieve meaningful progress towards healing.
