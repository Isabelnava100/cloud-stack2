Experiential Learning in Marketing Curriculum

Experiential learning is pivotal in digital marketing education, enabling students to apply theoretical concepts in real-world settings. Through hands-on projects, competitions, and practical assignments, students engage with actual case studies and marketing challenges faced by businesses today. This practical approach not only reinforces learning but also cultivates essential skills that employers actively seek in graduates. Dr. Parker emphasizes this method to bridge the gap between academia and industry, allowing students to gain confidence and competence.
