Transformative Recovery with Mastectomy Physical Therapy

Essential Mastectomy Rehabilitation Insights

Mastectomy rehabilitation is focused on using physical therapy to help restore mobility, strength, and functionality after surgery. At Thera, our skilled therapists analyze your unique requirements and formulate customized treatment strategies. Key interventions include stretching, strength-building exercises, and lymphatic drainage techniques. Our objective is to support your journey toward optimal recovery and improve your life quality.

Importance of Lymphatic Therapy

Lymphatic therapy is vital in your post-mastectomy recovery journey as it addresses swelling and promotes healing. Our trained therapists implement gentle, rhythmic techniques to stimulate lymphatic circulation and minimize lymphedema. This form of therapy is crucial to restore your motion range and alleviate discomfort. After sessions in lymphatic therapy at Thera, patients commonly share feelings of lightness and increased mobility.

Innovative Scar Management Strategies

Effective scar management is crucial for recovery following a mastectomy that improves skin texture and appearance. At Thera, we incorporate diverse techniques, including massage, the application of silicone gels, and targeted exercises. These modalities aim to reduce scar tissue and promote flexibility. Our therapists will provide guidance on optimal scar management methods, supporting your recovery aspirations.
