Why Orthodontic Treatment is Vital

Orthodontic treatment goes beyond generating an appealing smile; it plays a significant role in your oral health as well. Misaligned teeth can lead to various complications, such as challenges in maintaining hygiene, thereby elevating the risk of cavities and gum diseases. In addition, improper bites can result in jaw pain and unease over time. By consulting an orthodontist in Goldsboro, NC, you are making a valuable investment in your overall health and self-esteem, making the effort count toward your remarkable smile.
