Frequently Asked Questions

During your first visit to Michael Richards Orthodontics, you'll receive a comprehensive evaluation and learn about the best treatment options available for your needs.

The American Association of Orthodontists recommends that children have an orthodontic evaluation by age 7 to monitor development and address potential issues early.

We offer various types of braces, including traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligners, to meet our patients' needs and preferences.

Treatment lengths vary based on the individual case, but most patients can expect to wear braces or aligners for an average of 12 to 24 months.

Some discomfort may occur after getting braces or adjustments, but it usually subsides within a few days. Over-the-counter pain relief can help ease discomfort.
