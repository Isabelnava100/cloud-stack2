How do I care for my garments between cleanings?
To maintain your garments between cleanings by Red Hanger Cleaners, there are several essential care tips you should follow. First, always check the care labels on your clothing for specific washing and drying instructions. For items that are dry-clean only, avoid washing them at home as this can damage the fabric. When wearing your clothes, take measures to protect them from spills and stains. If you notice a stain, address it promptly with a gentle dab, and avoid rubbing it vigorously. Lastly, store your garments properly in a cool, dry place, ideally on appropriate hangers to maintain their shape. By following these care tips, you can extend the life of your garments between your visits to our dry cleaner in Utah.
