Learn About Very Plants Mixes for Vibrant Indoor Gardens

At Very Plants, we specialize in high-quality, soil-free potting mixes designed to optimize plant health and growth. Our products cater specifically to indoor and container-based plants, offering superior drainage, aeration, and nutrient availability. We emphasize using premium ingredients like peat moss, decomposed wood, and coir, which are sustainably sourced to ensure environmental responsibility. We are known for our 'Molly’s Mixes,' including specific formulations for different plant types such as aroids, succulents, and orchids. These mixes are designed to prevent common indoor plant issues like root rot and compaction while promoting healthy root development and growth. We offer detailed guidance on how to choose, pot, and care for plants to ensure they thrive indoors. In addition to our potting mixes, we provide a variety of plant care tips and educational resources on our website, helping customers select and maintain their plants effectively.

Potting your plants with Very Plants mixes is a simple process. Begin by selecting a pot with drainage holes to prevent water buildup. Fill the pot with your chosen Very Plants mix, leaving enough room for the plant's root ball. Carefully place the plant in the pot, ensuring the roots are well-covered with the mix. Water the plant thoroughly after potting to help settle the mix around the roots. For more detailed instructions, check out the potting guide available on our website.
