What to Expect and Questions to Ask Vendors

When planning your corporate retreat, ensure you understand what to expect from your chosen vendor. Here’s a checklist: - Verify all inclusions: Ensure the package covers accommodation, meals, activities, and transport. - Discuss customization options: Ask if the retreat can be tailored to meet your team's specific needs. - Check for flexibility: Understand cancellation and rescheduling policies. - Inquire about facilitation: Learn about the qualifications and experiences of facilitators. - Clarify dietary options: Confirm the availability of varied meal options to suit all preferences. These questions help ensure you select the right partner for your all-inclusive corporate retreat.
