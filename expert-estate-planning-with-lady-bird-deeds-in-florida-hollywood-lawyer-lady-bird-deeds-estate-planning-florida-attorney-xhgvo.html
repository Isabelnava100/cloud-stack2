Understanding Lady Bird Deeds

Lady Bird Deeds represent a unique tool in estate planning, allowing property owners to retain control of their assets while facilitating a smooth transfer of ownership upon their passing. Florida law allows this type of deed, which enables the owner to maintain life estate rights without having to go through probate, thereby saving time and reducing costs associated with estate administration. At Grimaldi Law Firm, our knowledgeable attorneys can guide you through the benefits and legalities of incorporating Lady Bird Deeds into your estate plan.
