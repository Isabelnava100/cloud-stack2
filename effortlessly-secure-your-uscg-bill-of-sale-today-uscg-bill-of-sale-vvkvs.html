What is a USCG Bill of Sale?

A USCG bill of sale is an essential legal document that confirms the sale and transfer of ownership of a vessel. It provides crucial information such as the buyer and seller's details, vessel information, and the sale date. This document must be precisely completed to ensure compliance with the U.S. Coast Guard regulations. Understanding its importance can help avoid potential legal issues down the line, ensuring smooth transitions during vessel ownership changes.
