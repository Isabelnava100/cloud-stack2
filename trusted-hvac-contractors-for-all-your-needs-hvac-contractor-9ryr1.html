What is the process for installation of a new HVAC system?
The installation process for a new HVAC system begins with a consultation to assess your specific needs, including the size of the space, existing systems, and energy efficiency goals. Valley Mechanical Solutions will then recommend suitable equipment based on our evaluation. Once you approve the plan, we schedule the installation, ensuring all preliminary work is completed. Our skilled technicians will install the system, perform necessary checks, and ensure everything is operational before finishing.
