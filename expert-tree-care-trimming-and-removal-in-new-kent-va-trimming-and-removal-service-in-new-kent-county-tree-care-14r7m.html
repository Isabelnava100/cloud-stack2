How do I maintain my trees after trimming?
After trimming, proper maintenance is essential to ensure the health of your trees. Regular watering, especially during dry spells, is crucial, along with monitoring for pests and diseases. Mulching can help retain moisture and regulate soil temperature. Additionally, it’s wise to schedule periodic inspections with a tree care service to address any potential issues before they escalate.
