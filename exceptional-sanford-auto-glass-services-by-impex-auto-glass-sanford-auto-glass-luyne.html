What is ADAS Calibration, and why is it important?
ADAS Calibration, or Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems Calibration, is a critical process performed after a windshield replacement or repair on vehicles equipped with advanced technology features. This process ensures that systems like lane departure warnings, adaptive cruise control, and automatic emergency braking continue to function correctly. At Impex Auto Glass, we utilize advanced tools and technology to perform precise calibration, ensuring your vehicle remains safe and its technology works as intended after any auto glass service.
