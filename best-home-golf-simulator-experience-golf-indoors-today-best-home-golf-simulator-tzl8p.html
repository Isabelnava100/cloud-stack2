Top Features to Look For in a Home Golf Simulator

When selecting the best home golf simulator, consider key features that enhance your experience. Look for accurate ball tracking technology that measures your swing speed, launch angle, and shot direction. It's essential to have a comprehensive software package that provides various courses and practice modes to keep your training engaging. Finally, ensure that your simulator is compatible with other golf tools and accessories for a seamless setup in your indoor space.
