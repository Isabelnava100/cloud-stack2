Qualified Intermediary Services

At The 1031 Group, we serve as your qualified intermediary throughout the 1031 exchange process. This role is vital in holding the proceeds from the sale of your relinquished property until they are reinvested in a qualified replacement property. Our trustworthiness and attention to detail ensure that your transaction adheres to IRS standards, helping you to avoid potential pitfalls that could result in tax liability. Our experienced team ensures the seamless execution of your 1031 exchange, providing peace of mind during this critical investment phase.

Common Questions

A 1031 exchange allows real estate investors to defer capital gains taxes on the sale of investment properties by reinvesting the proceeds into a similar property.

A qualified intermediary helps ensure compliance with IRS rules and protects your funds during the exchange, making it a crucial element of the 1031 exchange process.

We offer consultation, qualified intermediary services, exchange facilitation, and extensive investor education to guide clients through the 1031 exchange process.

Our experienced team navigates the complexities of the 1031 exchange process, providing expert guidance and resources to help you conduct successful, tax-deferred transactions.
