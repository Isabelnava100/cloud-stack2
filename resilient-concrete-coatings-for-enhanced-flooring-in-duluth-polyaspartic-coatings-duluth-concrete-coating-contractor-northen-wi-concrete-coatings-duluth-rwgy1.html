Frequently Asked Questions

Polyaspartic coatings consist of a polyurea material applied to concrete surfaces, known for offering superior durability, flexibility, and resistance to wear compared to standard epoxy coatings.

Polyaspartic coatings typically dry within a few hours, allowing for expedited application and minimizing downtime, unlike traditional epoxy coatings that may take days to fully cure.

We apply our concrete coatings to a broad array of surfaces, including garage floors, patios, commercial spaces, pole barns, and porch steps, enhancing both durability and aesthetics.

Provided they are well maintained and due to the quality of our products and application techniques, our concrete coatings are protected by a generous 15-year warranty, ensuring extended durable performance.

While both coating types offer protection and enhancement for concrete, polyaspartic coatings furnish improved UV resistance, greater flexibility, and faster drying times when juxtaposed with traditional epoxy coatings.

How should I care for my coated concrete floors?
For keeping your coated concrete floors in excellent condition, regularly clean using a neutral pH cleaner and steer clear of harsh chemicals. A basic sweep followed by mopping will suffice to maintain their appealing appearance.

Is it possible to install polyaspartic coatings on my own?
DIY installation is feasible; however, we strongly recommend engaging our professional contractors at Armor Coating Co to achieve optimal results and ensure the longevity of your coatings.
