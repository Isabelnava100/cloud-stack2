Understanding ICHRA and QSEHRA for Your Organization

Individual Coverage Health Reimbursement Arrangements (ICHRA) and Qualified Small Employer Health Reimbursement Arrangements (QSEHRA) are innovative solutions that are revolutionizing the way small to mid-sized employers provide health benefits in Michigan. With these plans, employers have the ability to offer more flexible options to their employees, allowing for personalized care without the burden of traditional group plans. CFH Insurance Consultants specializes in setting up ICHRA and QSEHRA that align with your financial and workforce needs, ensuring compliance while optimizing employee satisfaction. Let us show you how these benefit structures can provide both cost savings and enhanced employee engagement.
