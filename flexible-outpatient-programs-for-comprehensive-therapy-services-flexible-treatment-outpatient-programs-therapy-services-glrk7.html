What should I expect during therapy sessions?
Therapy sessions typically involve discussing experiences, emotions, and behaviors, in a safe and supportive environment, where clients learn coping strategies and tools for recovery.

Yes, one of the benefits of outpatient treatment is that it allows clients to maintain their daily responsibilities, such as work or education, while receiving necessary care.
