Understanding Dental Implants

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots that provide a permanent base for fixed or removable replacement teeth. They are made of biocompatible materials, ensuring a high success rate. Unlike dentures or bridges, implants fuse with the jawbone, making them a reliable long-term solution. At CalSmile Family Dental, we specialize in customizing dental implants for our patients in San Jose and nearby cities like Cupertino and Mountain View.

Who is a Good Candidate for Dental Implants?

Ideal candidates for dental implants are individuals with healthy gums and sufficient bone support, making it crucial to consult with a dental professional. Patients who have lost teeth due to injury, decay, or periodontal disease often benefit from implants. Non-smokers and those committed to maintaining oral hygiene typically see the best results. At CalSmile Family Dental, we assess each patient’s unique condition to determine candidacy for dental implants in San Jose.

The Implant Procedure: Step-by-Step

The dental implant procedure at CalSmile Family Dental typically involves several key steps: examination, implant placement, healing period, and crown placement. Initially, our team conducts a thorough assessment and imaging to plan the procedure effectively. During the surgery, the implant is surgically placed in the jawbone. After a healing period, a custom crown is added to restore functionality and appearance. Our compassionate care ensures your comfort throughout the entire process of receiving dental implants in San Jose.
