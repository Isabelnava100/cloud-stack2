Porcelain Veneers and Smile Aesthetics

Porcelain veneers are thin, custom-crafted shells designed to address concerns such as chips, gaps, staining, or uneven tooth shape. Dr. Atosa Khan focuses on natural-looking results that match each patient's features, with conservative tooth preparation and careful attention to fit and finish. Smile Makeover Planning

A smile makeover combines multiple cosmetic procedures into one coordinated treatment plan. Depending on the patient's goals, a plan may include veneers, professional teeth whitening, ceramic crowns, dental bonding, or replacement of older restorations. The process at JAX Dental Arts begins with photographs, digital planning, and a discussion of what each patient wants to change. For patients exploring a smile makeover St. Augustine, the practice provides detailed timelines, cost estimates, and previews of expected outcomes before any treatment begins.
