When is the best time to go rafting on the Ocoee River?
The best time for whitewater rafting on the Ocoee River is typically from late spring to early fall — specifically from April to October. During this period, water levels are ideal for thrilling rafting experiences, making it great for both beginners and seasoned rafters looking for excitement. River conditions can vary with weather patterns, so we recommend checking our schedule or contacting us for the most current information about rafting conditions. Regardless of when you visit, Outland Expeditions is ready to provide you with an unforgettable outdoor adventure in Tennessee!
