Unforgettable Outdoor Adventures and Party Cruises Await You

At Flipper Finders, we specialize in crafting memorable outdoor adventures and party cruises for all. Our dolphin and party boat tours provide a unique experience you won’t find anywhere else. With our boats operating from Charleston Harbor and Folly Beach, we invite you to explore the beauty of the waters with us. From nature tours to bachelorette parties, we’re here to ensure your adventure is both educational and fun! Our physical location on Folly Beach has been serving joyful experiences since 2010. Whether you're looking for a thrilling ride or a calmer day along the coast, our friendly crew guarantees an enjoyable time. We take pride in offering a blend of relaxation and excitement, making each tour an unforgettable experience. Join us at Flipper Finders and make your next outing a true adventure on the water!

Explore Our Dolphin Tours

Our dolphin tours are designed to bring you up close and personal with the friendly dolphins of Charleston waters. As you cruise through the scenic coastline, our knowledgeable crew will share interesting facts about marine life and dolphin behavior. These tours are perfect for families, couples, and anyone looking to connect with nature. Join us for an unforgettable day of adventure and discovery on the water!
