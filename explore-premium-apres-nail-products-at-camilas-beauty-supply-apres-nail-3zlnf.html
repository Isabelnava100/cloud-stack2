Application Tips for Apres Nail Products

To apply Apres Nail products, start by properly prepping your natural nails. Gently push back your cuticles, remove the shine from your nail surface using a fine-grit buffer, and cleanse the nails with alcohol or Apres pH Bonder to eliminate any oils. Next, apply a thin layer of Apres Non-Acidic Primer to promote better adhesion and prevent lifting. Then, apply a thin coat of Apres Extend Gel to your natural nail and cure it for 30 seconds under an LED or UV lamp. When applying the Gel-X tip, place a small amount of Extend Gel inside the tip, then hold the tip at a 45-degree angle and press it onto your nail, ensuring there are no air bubbles. Finally, flash cure it while holding the tip in place, and then fully cure once it’s secured. Following these steps carefully will help you achieve long-lasting, professional-looking nails with Apres products.

Maintaining Your Apres Nails

Maintaining the longevity of your Apres nails is crucial for lasting beauty. Regularly moisturize your cuticles and nails to keep them healthy. Avoid exposing your nails to harsh chemicals that can weaken the product's integrity. To remove Apres nails, follow the recommended removal process to ensure your natural nails remain undamaged.
