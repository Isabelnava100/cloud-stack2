At Kingdom Recovery Center, we specialize in offering tailored solutions for those battling dual diagnosis, which includes mental health issues alongside substance use disorders. Our offerings encompass Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP) and Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) to ensure we meet diverse individual requirements. Exploring Our PHP Options

Our Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP) provides an intensive care solution for those needing structured assistance but still wanting the comfort of returning home for the night. This innovative method preserves a sense of normal life while providing vital therapeutic support. At Kingdom Recovery Center, we create personalized treatment strategies that reflect the distinct situations and requirements of our clients. Our PHP emphasizes dual diagnosis and incorporates a wealth of evidence-based interventions to promote healing.

Advantages of IOP for Sustainable Recovery

Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) afford a flexible treatment model for individuals seeking continuous assistance post-residential treatment. Our IOP merges frequent therapy meetings with the chance to fulfill everyday roles like career and education. Participants take part in both group and individual therapies tailored to address their unique hurdles, such as managing urges and dealing with triggers. By opting for IOP, individuals are equipped with the necessary skills for enduring recovery while benefiting from a flexible routine that supports independence.

Proven Therapies Addressing Dual Diagnosis

At Kingdom Recovery Center, we employ a collection of evidence-oriented therapies aimed at assisting individuals facing dual diagnosis challenges. Approaches like Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and EMDR are utilized to target the fundamental factors complicating their conditions. Our dedicated professionals personalize these methods to cater to each client, confirming a customized approach to healing. Our focus on verified therapies empowers us to facilitate meaningful and enduring recovery in our clients.
