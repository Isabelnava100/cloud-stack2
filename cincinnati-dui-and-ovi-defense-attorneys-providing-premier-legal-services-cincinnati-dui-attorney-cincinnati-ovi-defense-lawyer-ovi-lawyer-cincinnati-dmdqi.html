How long do OVI charges remain on my record?
OVI offenses can persist on your record indefinitely, influencing your future job opportunities and insurance premiums. Retaining a skilled attorney can help mitigate these consequences.

What defenses can I use against an OVI charge?
Defenses against an OVI accusation can involve questioning the legality of the traffic stop, contesting the use of field sobriety assessments, and challenging the absence of probable cause.
