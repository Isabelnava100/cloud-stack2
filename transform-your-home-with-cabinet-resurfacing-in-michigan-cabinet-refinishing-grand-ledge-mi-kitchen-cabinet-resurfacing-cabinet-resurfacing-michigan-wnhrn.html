Cabinet Resurfacing Process

Our Multi-Stage Resurfacing Process

At Lasting Impressions LLC, our cabinet resurfacing process is designed to deliver exceptional results. We begin with thorough cleaning to ensure all surfaces are free of dust and grease. Next, we employ precision sanding to create a perfect base for finishing. Any necessary repairs are addressed before applying our high-quality, professional-grade coatings. Finally, we allow for proper curing times to ensure durability and longevity, giving you kitchen cabinets that look brand new.
