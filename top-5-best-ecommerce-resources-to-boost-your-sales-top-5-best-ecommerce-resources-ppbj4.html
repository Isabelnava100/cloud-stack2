2. Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — Ecommerce Performance Insights

Understanding your customers and site performance is essential. GA4 provides advanced insights into ecommerce conversions, customer paths, and product performance — helping you make smarter decisions based on real data.

Why it matters: GA4 allows ecommerce brands to track everything from product views to purchase behavior, enabling data-driven optimization.

3. Klaviyo — Ecommerce Email & SMS Marketing Platform

A powerful email and SMS marketing platform purpose-built for ecommerce. Klaviyo helps brands create personalized campaigns that drive retention and revenue.

Hotjar offers heatmaps, session recordings, and user feedback tools that help ecommerce brands improve the customer experience and drive higher conversions.

Why it matters: Understanding where customers engage — and where they get stuck — helps you optimize site UX and increase sales.
