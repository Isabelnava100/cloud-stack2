Dental implants at 4M Dental Implant Center function by providing a strong foundation for artificial teeth. The process begins with the surgical placement of a titanium post into the jawbone, which acts as an artificial tooth root. Over time, the bone fuses with the implant in a process called osseointegration. Once healed, a custom-made crown is attached to the post, allowing you to enjoy a natural-looking and functional smile.

How can I maintain my dental implants after the procedure?
Maintaining dental implants is relatively straightforward and similar to caring for natural teeth. At 4M Dental Implant Center, we recommend brushing at least twice a day, flossing regularly, and scheduling routine check-ups to monitor your oral health. Avoiding tobacco products and limiting acidic or sugary foods can further enhance implant longevity. Our team provides personalized care instructions to ensure your dental implants remain functional and beautiful for years to come.
