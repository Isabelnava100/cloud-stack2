How is property allocated in a divorce according to Texas law?
In Texas, property division adheres to community property statutes, stipulating that assets accrued during the marriage are generally shared equally, whilst individual property stays with the original owner.

Is it possible to amend my divorce decree at a later date?
Yes, you may pursue modifications to your divorce decree—specifically related to aspects like child custody, visitation rights, and child support—subject to adhering to due legal processes.
