Extra FAQ

Is it possible for allergy symptoms to vary as children develop?

Indeed, some allergies may diminish in frequency as children mature, while new allergies may manifest, highlighting the need for regular assessments.

What treatments are accessible for managing allergies?

Available treatment approaches encompass avoidance tactics, pharmacological therapies, and immunotherapy options to assist in alleviating allergy symptoms.
