How can physical therapy help with arthritis management?
Physical therapy plays a crucial role in arthritis management by helping to alleviate pain, maintain joint function, and improve mobility. At PT 360°, our therapists design individualized exercise programs that focus on strengthening the muscles around affected joints, enhancing flexibility, and promoting overall physical activity. Through education on proper body mechanics and advice on activity modifications, we empower patients to manage their condition effectively and improve their quality of life.

Are there specific exercises included in balance training at PT 360°?
Yes, balance training at PT 360° includes a variety of exercises designed to improve stability, coordination, and overall physical functionality. Our skilled therapists will assess your individual needs and incorporate tailored exercises, such as strength training, proprioceptive activities, and gait training into your program. These exercises are crucial in preventing falls and enhancing mobility, particularly for seniors or individuals recovering from injuries. We ensure the program suits your skill level and progresses as you improve.
