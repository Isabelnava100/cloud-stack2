The process typically begins with a careful cleaning of the windows to ensure proper adhesion of the tint. The application process involves cleaning the surface thoroughly, then carefully applying the film to ensure a smooth, bubble-free finish. Is window tinting legal in Roseville, CA?
Yes, window tinting is legal in Roseville, CA, but there are regulations that must be followed. The California Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) specifies that front windshields must allow more than 70% of light in, while rear windows and back side windows can be tinted darker. It's important to ensure that the tinting film used complies with state regulations to avoid fines or issues during inspections.
