Invisalign Treatment in Santa Cruz, CA

What is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a modern orthodontic treatment that uses clear, removable aligners to straighten teeth. Unlike traditional braces, these aligners are virtually invisible, making them a popular choice for both teens and adults looking for a discreet treatment option. The aligners are custom-made to fit your teeth, allowing for effective tooth movement and alignment. At North Coast Orthodontics, we are proud to offer Invisalign services that cater specifically to the needs of our patients in Santa Cruz, CA.

Benefits of Invisalign

There are several benefits to choosing Invisalign for your orthodontic treatment. First, the clear aligners provide a discreet appearance, allowing you to smile confidently during treatment. Additionally, the removable nature of Invisalign means you can continue to enjoy your favorite foods without restriction. Patients also appreciate the comfort of the aligners, as there are no metal brackets or wires that can irritate the mouth. Lastly, the treatment often results in quicker progress, leading to a beautiful smile in less time.
