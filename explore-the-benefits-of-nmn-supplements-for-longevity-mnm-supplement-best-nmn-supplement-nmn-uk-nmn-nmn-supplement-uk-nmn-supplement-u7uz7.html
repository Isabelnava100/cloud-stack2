What is NMN and Why is it Crucial?

Nicotinamide Mononucleotide (NMN) is a naturally occurring compound that serves as a precursor to NAD+, a vital molecule for energy production in our cells. As we age, NAD+ levels decline, leading to various health issues. Supplements containing NMN can help replenish NAD+ levels, potentially delaying the aging process and enhancing overall vitality.
