Why Opt for Artificial Grass Putting Greens?

Artificial grass putting greens provide a multitude of advantages that classic lawns fail to deliver. First, these greens are engineered for resilience against heavy use, making them perfect for enthusiastic golfers. Furthermore, our top-grade synthetic turf replicates the visual and tactile qualities of natural grass, preserving its vibrancy and texture without the demanding upkeep necessary for traditional greens. Additionally, artificial grass is environmentally friendly, conserving water resources and eliminating the requirement for harmful pesticides and fertilizers, catering to eco-conscious property owners.

Step-by-Step Installation of Your Putting Green

Our experienced installers then level the ground and create effective drainage systems to avoid water build-up. Lastly, we skillfully install the synthetic materials, guaranteeing a flawless appearance that rivals a natural putting surface.

Care Tips for Your Synthetic Putting Green

Caring for your artificial grass putting green is straightforward, allowing you to focus on gameplay rather than chores. Regularly comb through the turf to keep the fibers upright and encourage efficient drainage. For any debris, simply sweep or rinse it off. Also, watch out for leaves that may rest on the turf surface and remove them swiftly to avoid staining. By adhering to these simple upkeep practices, your synthetic green will continue to look immaculate for countless years.
