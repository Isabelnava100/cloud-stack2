What is Blue Spirulina?

Blue spirulina is a specific type of blue-green algae known scientifically as Arthrospira platensis. Unlike its green counterpart, blue spirulina is rich in the pigment phycocyanin, which is not only responsible for its stunning blue color but also its remarkable health advantages. Particularly popular in Australia, blue spirulina offers a clean source of plant-based protein and essential nutrients, making it an excellent addition to health-focused diets.
