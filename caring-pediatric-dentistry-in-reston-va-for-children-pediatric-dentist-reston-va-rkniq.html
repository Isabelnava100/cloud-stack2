Extra FAQ

What types of preventative care do you provide for children?

We provide an array of preventative measures such as regular cleanings, fluoride treatments, and dental sealants to shield your child’s teeth from decay.

How can I motivate my child to maintain proper dental hygiene?

Incorporate fun elements into brushing and flossing routines by using vibrant toothbrushes, rewarding good habits, and brushing together. It’s also important to explain the significance of good oral hygiene.
