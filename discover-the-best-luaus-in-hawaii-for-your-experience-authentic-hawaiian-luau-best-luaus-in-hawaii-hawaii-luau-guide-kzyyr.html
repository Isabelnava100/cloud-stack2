What should I wear to a Hawaiian luau?
Casual island attire is recommended for luaus. Light, comfortable clothing and sandals or flip-flops are typically appropriate.

Can I find vegetarian options at luaus?
Many luaus offer vegetarian options, but it’s best to check in advance with the specific luau you plan to attend.
