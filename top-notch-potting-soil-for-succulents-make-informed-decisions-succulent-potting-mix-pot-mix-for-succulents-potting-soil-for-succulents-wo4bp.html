Best Practices for Utilizing Succulent Potting Mix

When incorporating our succulent potting mix, it's essential to employ proper potting strategies to enhance your plants' well-being. Ensure that your container has sufficient drainage holes to facilitate moisture removal. Consider blending in extra perlite or pumice to boost drainage capabilities. Routinely assess the moisture level by checking the topsoil with a finger or moisture meter to time your watering, ensuring your succulents thrive without the danger of root rot.
