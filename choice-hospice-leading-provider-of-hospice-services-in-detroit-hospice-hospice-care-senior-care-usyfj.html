Choice Hospice: Leading Provider of Hospice Services in Detroit

At Choice Hospice, we acknowledge that dealing with end-of-life care can be a daunting journey for families. As a top hospice service in Detroit, our mission is to deliver empathetic and extensive hospice care customized to suit the specific requirements of each individual. Our healthcare professionals, which include experienced physicians, talented nurses, dedicated social workers, and caring aides, prioritize comfort and respect during this significant period. Leading Hospice Services Provided by Choice Hospice in Detroit

In-Depth Hospice Care and Assistance

Choice Hospice provides an extensive array of hospice care options including routine home visits, respite care, ongoing care, and short-term inpatient support. Our skilled staff is devoted to offering tailored assistance to both patients and their families. Collaborating closely with healthcare practitioners and pharmacists, we aim to promote quality of life and comfort in every aspect of care. Whether the need arises for managing medication effects or providing psychological and spiritual guidance, we are here to support families on their journey.

Essential Hospice Equipment and Supplies

At Choice Hospice, we enhance the patient experience by supplying vital hospice materials and equipment for comfort. Our offerings include hospital beds, oxygen tanks, wheelchairs, as well as crucial incontinence products. We make it our priority to provide families with basic essentials like wipes, gloves, and blue pads to aid daily care. Our goal is to eliminate obstacles to receiving care, allowing families to concentrate on the important aspect of merely spending precious time with their loved ones.

Support Services for Emotional and Spiritual Well-Being

Recognizing that hospice care encompasses more than mere physical needs, Choice Hospice provides powerful emotional and spiritual care options. Our chaplains and social workers are qualified to offer solace as patients and their families confront complicated emotions and decisions. We believe it’s vital to nurture the mental and spiritual state alongside physical health, promoting a holistic approach to hospice care. Our professionals foster an environment conducive to candid dialogues, bereavement support, and emotional assistance.
