In-Depth Overview of Mini Split Line Sets

What are Mini Split Line Sets?

Mini split line sets are essential elements that facilitate the connection between your indoor and outdoor mini-split HVAC units. These sets primarily consist of insulated copper tubing that transports refrigerant alongside electrical lines. Choosing the right line set is crucial for maximizing efficiency and ensuring top-level performance from your heating and cooling systems.
