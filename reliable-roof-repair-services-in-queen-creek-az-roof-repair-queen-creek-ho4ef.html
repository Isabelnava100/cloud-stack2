How often should I schedule roof maintenance?
Regular roof maintenance is essential for extending the life of your roofing system. We recommend conducting a professional inspection at least once a year, ideally before the harsh weather seasons. Additionally, it's wise to schedule maintenance after severe weather events, such as heavy storms or high winds, to check for potential damage.
