Discover Mi Casa: The Best Restaurant in Breckenridge

Welcome to Mi Casa Mexican Restaurant and Cantina, established in 1981, our goal has been to serve delicious Mexican dishes, and we have proudly become known as the best restaurant in Breckenridge. Just a short stroll from the Quicksilver Chairlift, our establishment has naturally become the preferred spot for post-ski gatherings and pleasurable dining. Come and see what makes us a favorite among our devoted patrons who return for irresistible meals and delightful beverages, all served with friendliness. Committing to excellence, attentiveness, and community spirit, Mi Casa holds a pivotal role in the Breckenridge dining landscape. Our team is dedicated to delivering dining memories that highlight the rich flavors of Mexican cuisine, utilizing fresh, high-quality ingredients along with traditional family recipes. Be it unwinding after a day in the mountains or marking a special occasion, our attentive staff are here to make your experience truly memorable.
