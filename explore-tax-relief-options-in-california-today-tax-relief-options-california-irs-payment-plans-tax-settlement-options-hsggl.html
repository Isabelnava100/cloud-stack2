Common Questions

The most common tax relief options in California include Offer in Compromise, Installment Agreements, and currently not collectible statuses. Each option is tailored to individual circumstances.

IRS payment plans allow taxpayers to spread their outstanding tax obligations over a predetermined period, making it easier to manage payments. There are short and long-term options available.

An Offer in Compromise is a tax settlement option that allows eligible taxpayers to pay less than the total amount owed to resolve their tax obligations.

Qualifying for tax settlement options typically depends on your financial situation including income, expenses, and assets. Consulting with a tax resolution expert can provide clarity.
