Pro Tips for Keeping Your HVAC System in Top Shape

1. Change Your Filters Regularly

A clogged air filter reduces airflow and forces your system to work harder, leading to higher energy bills and potential breakdowns.

2. Schedule Seasonal Maintenance

Twice-yearly tune-ups can extend the life of your HVAC system and ensure it’s ready to handle the demands of Moapa’s climate.

3. Seal Air Leaks

Check doors, windows, and ductwork for leaks. Sealing these gaps improves efficiency and keeps your home more comfortable.
