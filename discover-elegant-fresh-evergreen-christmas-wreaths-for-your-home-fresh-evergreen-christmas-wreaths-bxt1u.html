Maintaining Your Fresh Evergreen Christmas Wreath

To allow your fresh evergreen Christmas wreath to look its best throughout the festive period, it’s crucial to care for it properly. Keep your wreath in a shaded spot away from harsh sunlight, and lightly mist with water to retain moisture. If displaying the wreath on a door, keep it away from extreme weather for optimal longevity. With a bit of attention, you can appreciate the timeless beauty of your wreath from the beginning of the holiday season through to the New Year.
