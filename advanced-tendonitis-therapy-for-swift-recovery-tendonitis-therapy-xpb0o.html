Essential Information on Tendonitis Therapy

What Is Tendonitis?

Tendonitis refers to the inflammation or irritation of a tendon, often caused by overuse or repetitive strain. It's commonly seen in areas such as the shoulder, elbow, and knee. Identifying the early indications of tendonitis can facilitate faster recovery and help avoid further injuries. Our specialized team at Capstone Physical Therapy is here to inform you about your condition and outline the treatment options that are available.

Customized Treatment Strategies

We at Capstone Physical Therapy understand that effective tendonitis therapy starts with customized treatment strategies. Our experienced therapists will evaluate your individual needs to develop a therapy regimen that is specifically designed for you. Treatments may involve specialized exercises, hands-on therapy, and techniques like ultrasound or electrical stimulation. Our collective goal is to help you regain normal movement capabilities.

Avoiding Future Injury

Preventing future tendonitis injuries is essential for sustained well-being and enjoying physical activities. At Capstone Physical Therapy, we not only address your current issues but also arm you with methods to minimize the potential for recurrent problems. This includes teaching you about correct body mechanics, effective stretching routines, and strength-building exercises devised for your lifestyle. With our expert guidance, you can approach your activities with confidence, free from apprehension regarding re-injury.
