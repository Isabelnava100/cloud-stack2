Frequently Asked Questions

Botox is a refined protein that temporarily inhibits the movement of facial muscles, effectively smoothing out wrinkles and fine lines by blocking nerve signals to the muscles, limiting their contractions.

Results from Botox treatments generally last between three to six months, varying by individual factors. Regular sessions can help sustain a smooth look over time.

Many clients experience very little discomfort during their Botox injections. Our qualified staff exercise methods to minimize any sensation, ensuring the treatment is as comfortable as possible.

You may begin to see results as early as 24 to 48 hours post-treatment, with the complete effects becoming visible within a week to two weeks.

Mild side effects may include minor swelling, redness, or bruising at the injection points. Severe reactions are rare, but should be discussed with your provider.
