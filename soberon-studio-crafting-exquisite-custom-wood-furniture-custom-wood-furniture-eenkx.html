Soberon Studio: Crafting Exquisite Custom Wood Furniture

At Soberon Studio, we redefine luxury with our custom wood furniture that embodies art and functionality. Located in beautiful Hollywood, Florida, our studio, founded by the talented architect Eduardo Martinez Soberon, is dedicated to creating unique pieces that stand out. We utilize premium materials, including reclaimed woods and epoxy resin, ensuring that our furnishings remain sustainable and timeless. Each of our creations tells a story, resonating with the elegant aesthetic of your living spaces.
