Extra FAQ

How do I care for my dental crown?

Caring for a dental crown is similar to maintaining your regular teeth. Brush and floss daily, and visit us for regular check-ups to keep your crown in top shape.

Will my insurance cover a dental crown?

Many dental insurance plans cover a portion of the cost of crowns. We will work with you to understand your coverage and any financial options available.
