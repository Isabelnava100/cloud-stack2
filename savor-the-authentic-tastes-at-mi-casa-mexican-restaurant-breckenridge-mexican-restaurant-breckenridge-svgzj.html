Savor the Authentic Tastes at Mi Casa Mexican Restaurant Breckenridge

Welcome to Mi Casa Mexican Restaurant and Cantina, a cherished dining refuge in Breckenridge, Colorado, renowned for serving authentic Mexican dishes since 1981. Entering our doors, you will experience a welcoming and family-oriented setting that embodies the charm of our vibrant mountain town. Conveniently located across from Peak 9 Village and just a quick walk from the Quicksilver Chairlift, we're the ideal destination for both locals and visitors to savor a meal after a long day hitting the slopes. Our dedication to high-quality ingredients and classic recipes guarantees each mouthful captures the essence of Mexico’s rich culinary heritage. At Mi Casa, we take pride in being more than a dining spot; we serve as a preferred gathering locale for friends and family. Our lively cantina presents an extensive collection of margaritas, tequilas, and cocktails perfect for pairing with your meal or for enjoying during our happy hour. Whether you are in the mood for tacos, enchiladas, or our acclaimed fajitas, our menu caters to every palate, confirming our status as the go-to Mexican restaurant in Breckenridge for over forty years.

Highlights from Our Menu

At Mi Casa Mexican Restaurant and Cantina, we take pride in providing a rich menu filled with authentic Mexican fare. From delectable tacos made with homemade tortillas to our grill-fired fajitas, every dish is crafted to delight the senses. Don’t miss our enchiladas, drenched in house-made mole, or our signature burritos that are brimming with tasty fillings and dressed in your choice of sauces. Complements for every meal includes one of our refreshing margaritas, meticulously crafted using top-tier tequila and fresh ingredients, designating Mi Casa as the finest Mexican restaurant in Breckenridge.

Specials During Happy Hour

Join us at Mi Casa Mexican Restaurant and Cantina for our lively happy hour offers! Delight in reduced prices on both beverages and appetizers each day from 3 PM to 6 PM, establishing the perfect atmosphere for après ski relaxation. Enjoy our well-known margaritas served on the rocks or frozen, combined with flavorful chips and guacamole or zesty salsa. Bring your friends and relax in our inviting environment after your day skiing, where delicious food and drinks come together with the warmth of shared dining in stunning Breckenridge.

Family Dining Experience

At Mi Casa, we value the significance of shared family meals. Our restaurant is tailored to families, providing a children’s menu loaded with beloved dishes in smaller portions to ensure that all diners leave satisfied. Relax in our casual, comfortable atmosphere where joy and delicious cuisine combine. With our focus on high standards and service, Mi Casa Mexican Restaurant proudly stands as a family favorite in Breckenridge, creating lasting memories over every meal.
