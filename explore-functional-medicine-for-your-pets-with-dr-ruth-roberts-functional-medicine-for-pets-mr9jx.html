What is Functional Medicine?

Functional Medicine for pets emphasizes the interaction between various systems within the body and how lifestyle practices can affect your pet’s health. Distinct from conventional veterinary methods that often focus predominantly on symptom relief, Functional Medicine aims to discover and treat the underlying causes of health issues. This integrative model fosters more personalized and effective therapies that ultimately contribute to a longer and healthier lifespan for pets.
