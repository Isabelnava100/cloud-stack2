Why Multi-Tenancy Is Key in SaaS Solutions

Creating a multi-tenant architecture is essential for maximizing resource utilization and scalability of your SaaS MVP. Effective multi-tenancy strategies not only drive down expenses but also improve system performance by facilitating streamlined updates and maintenance.
