Advantages of Choosing Eastern Red Cedar for Ceilings

Eastern red cedar boasts remarkable durability and natural resistance to deterioration, distinguishing it as a prime choice for your barndominium ceilings. This exquisite wood type features deep, warm tones and a pleasant fragrance that enriches the indoor ambiance. Additionally, cedar's inherent qualities offer outstanding insulation, effectively stabilizing your home's climate throughout the year. Opt for eastern red cedar to craft a ceiling that is both functional and steeped in character.

Ceiling Design Inspirations for Barndominiums

In contemplating the design of your barndominium ceiling, various options for integrating ceiling planks await. You might opt for an exquisite coffered ceiling featuring creatively arranged eastern red cedar planks or select a straightforward shiplap pattern for added rustic allure. Merging this natural wood ceiling with contrasting wall hues can create a captivating focal point in every area. Consider mixing diverse textures and materials to craft a more compelling and attractive ceiling.

Care Tips for Your Cedar Ceiling

Maintaining the beauty of your eastern red cedar ceiling is essential. Regular cleaning and dusting will help preserve its inherent charm and keep dirt at bay. Occasionally, applying a layer of quality wood conditioner can protect the surface and augment its appeal. Refrain from using harsh chemicals, as these can harm the wood; instead, choose natural cleaning methods that are gentle on cedar.
