What are the benefits of creating a trust as part of estate planning?
Creating a trust as part of your estate planning has several benefits. Trusts can help your assets avoid probate, which is a time-consuming and often costly process. Additionally, they provide privacy since they don't become public record, unlike wills. Trusts also allow for greater control over the distribution of your assets, ensuring that beneficiaries receive their inheritances under specific conditions. Our Texas Estate Planning Lawyers at M&P Law Offices can guide you through the various types of trusts available and help you choose the best option for your individual circumstances.
