Defining Social Security Disability Insurance

Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) is a federally funded initiative aimed at delivering financial support to those unable to work due to eligible disabilities. It provides monthly financial aid as well as, in certain instances, added help such as healthcare services. Familiarity with SSDI is essential for anyone looking to file a claim due to its exclusive eligibility criteria and required procedures. Our knowledgeable professionals at Harvey & Carpenter are here to uphold your rights during this critical endeavor and assist you in obtaining the benefits you require.

Reasons Often Leading to Denial of Social Security Disability Claims

Multiple factors can contribute to the denial of social security disability claims, including insufficient medical documentation, failure to satisfy the eligibility requirements, or incorrect understanding of the application instructions. It is vital to address the causes of these denials in order to strengthen your appeal chances.
