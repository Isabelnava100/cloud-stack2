Auto glass repair involves assessing the damage to your windshield, typically caused by chips or cracks. At Impex Auto Glass, our technicians first evaluate the size and location of the damage. If the chip is smaller than a quarter and located away from the driver’s line of sight, we will use a specialized resin to fill the chip, which is then cured for strength. This process restores the structural integrity of the glass and minimizes the appearance of the damage. For larger cracks or extensive damage, we may recommend a full windshield replacement to ensure your safety while driving. Our priority is to provide you with top-notch auto glass repair in Charlotte.

ADAS Calibration is a crucial service that ensures advanced safety features in your vehicle, such as lane departure warnings and adaptive cruise control, function correctly after auto glass replacement. When a windshield is replaced, it can affect the sensors and cameras that form part of these systems. At Impex Auto Glass, our experienced technicians perform precise ADAS Calibration to realign and recalibrate these systems, ensuring they operate as intended. This service is vital for your safety and to maintain the functionality of modern vehicles, especially if they are equipped with sophisticated driver assistance technologies.
