What should I anticipate during a ceramic coating application?
During a ceramic coating application, our trained technicians will first thoroughly wash and decontaminate your vehicle's surface to ensure optimal bonding. After drying, we will apply the ceramic coating in a controlled environment to prevent dust and debris from affecting the finish. The process typically takes a few hours, depending on the size of the vehicle and the specific coating being applied. Once completed, you can expect a stunning, glossy finish that provides long-lasting protection.
