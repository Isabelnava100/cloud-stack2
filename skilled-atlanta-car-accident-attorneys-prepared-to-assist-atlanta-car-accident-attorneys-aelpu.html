Steps to Take After a Vehicle Collision in Atlanta?

If you find yourself in a car accident in Atlanta, it's essential to remain composed and evaluate the circumstances. Ensure your safety by moving to a secure spot if you're able. Call 911 immediately to report the incident and obtain medical help for any injuries. Capture the scene by taking pictures, collecting witness details, and exchanging information with the other party. Lastly, inform your insurance provider and consult with our Atlanta car accident attorneys to explore your legal choices and rights.

What types of compensation can I receive?
Victims can seek recompense for medical bills, lost wages, property damage, and emotionally traumatic experiences resulting from the accident.

What if I was partially at fault in the accident?
Georgia follows a modified comparative fault rule, so you might still qualify for compensation even if you share some level of responsibility.
