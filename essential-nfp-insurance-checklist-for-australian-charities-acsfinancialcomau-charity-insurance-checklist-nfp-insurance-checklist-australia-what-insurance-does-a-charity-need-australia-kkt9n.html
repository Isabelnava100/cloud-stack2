Insurance Types for Charities in Australia

Our core competencies

Public Liability Insurance

Public liability insurance is crucial for charities operating in public spaces or engaging with the community. This coverage protects against claims of bodily injury or property damage caused by your charity's activities, ensuring your organisation is not financially burdened in case of unforeseen incidents. It is essential for fostering trust with donors, volunteers, and the public, highlighting your commitment to safety and responsibility. With ACS Financial, we can assess your specific needs and provide guidance on adequate public liability coverage options available.

Management Liability Insurance

Management liability insurance is critical for charities and NFPs, as it covers claims arising from the decisions made by your board and management team. This insurance protects your leaders from legal actions alleging wrongful acts such as mismanagement, discrimination, or failure to comply with regulations. As charities often operate with tight budgets, this coverage ensures that personal assets of board members and staff are safeguarded while they serve the organisation. Partner with ACS Financial to ensure your management team is protected with appropriate liability coverage tailored to the charity sector.

Cyber Insurance

In today’s digital age, cyber insurance is becoming increasingly necessary for charities that handle sensitive information or have an online presence. This coverage protects against data breaches, cyberattacks, and other cybersecurity incidents, ensuring that your organisation can recover from the financial impacts of such events. Given the potential damage to reputation and trust, investing in cyber insurance is vital for any charity operating in Australia. Let ACS Financial help you assess your cyber risk and select an insurance plan that fits your charity’s cybersecurity needs.
