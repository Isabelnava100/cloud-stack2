What is the duration of the white water rafting trips?
Our rafting trips typically last between 4 to 6 days, allowing ample time for rafting, camping, and enjoying the scenic beauty of the river.

When is the best time for white water rafting on the Middle Fork of the Salmon River?
The best time for white water rafting is usually from late spring to early summer when the river’s flow is optimal and the scenery is at its best.
