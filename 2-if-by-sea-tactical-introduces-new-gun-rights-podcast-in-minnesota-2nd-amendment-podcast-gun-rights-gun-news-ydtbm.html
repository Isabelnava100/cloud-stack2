2 If By Sea Tactical Introduces New Gun Rights Podcast in Minnesota

The team at 2 If By Sea Tactical Indoor Range & Store, located in New Prague, MN, is excited to announce the debut of their new show, known as the “Lexington & Concord Podcast”.

 

The primary objective of this podcast is to delve deeply into all aspects of the 2nd Amendment. It provides a comprehensive overview of gun news at the global, national, and regional level, while also shedding light on the historical and political narratives surrounding firearms legislation. Brothers Austin and Alexander Reville co-host "Lexington and Concord Podcast," which combines a family-like rapport with thorough insights – creating an informal yet thought-provoking dialogue.

 

Various subjects have already been explored in the episodes to date, including:

  • Brotherly introductions

  • Gun control measures in Edina, MN

  • Guns seen in classic holiday films

  • Legislation regarding firearms in the UK

  • Understanding the Second Amendment

  • Overview of recent gun-related incidents in the news

Meet the Podcast's Hosts

Austin and Alexander Reville, the brothers steering the podcast's discussions, draw upon their personal experiences and extensive background in firearms to guide their conversations. Their formative years fostered a love for history, which they use to contextualize topics of the Second Amendment and the U.S. Constitution within ongoing debates. Austin, a co-founder of 2 If By Sea Tactical, operates the indoor shooting range and gun shop. With a strong background in security and counter-terrorism, he is certified as both an NRA and USCCA firearms instructor. Initially launching 2 If By Sea in 2019 from a modest location, he has since expanded the establishment into a sprawling 10,000 square foot facility equipped with offices, meeting rooms, a virtual reality section, a firing range, and a retail outlet. His writings on firearm education can be found via the 2 If By Sea blog titled "The Patriot's Almanac" and through various content on YouTube, helping to keep the local populace updated on significant events as well as historical information. Likewise, Alexander is also a recognized writer and content producer. He began with Guns.com, excelling in their warehouse through his profound knowledge of historical firearms. Now, he contributes significantly to their content team. A constitutional advocate, firearms instructor, security team member, and devoted collector, Alexander infuses each episode with his insights and personality.

Why Choose the Name 'Lexington and Concord'?

The American Revolutionary period birthed the Bill of Rights and, notably, the Second Amendment – a cornerstone in the narrative of gun ownership rights. This right to possess arms, carefully enshrined by the Founding Fathers, is critical to safeguarding liberty and personal safety. Both Austin and Alexander Reville uphold this ideology, drawing inspiration from the Founders for their show, which matches the themes of Austin’s Revolutionary War-inspired branding for 2 If By Sea Tactical. Referencing the gun store's name, Austin sees Paul Revere’s famous ride as vital in defining American liberty, exemplifying the essential nature of gun access for freely choosing citizens. By collaborating with Alexander on their podcast, they extend this intricate dialogue through the title 'Lexington and Concord'—a historic reference to the confrontations that initiated the American Revolutionary conflict following Paul Revere's revered trek.

Creating a Firearms Community at 2 If By Sea

At the heart of the podcast lies a mission to enhance the firearms community in Minnesota and beyond. The brothers mix their deep knowledge, unique viewpoints, and sense of humor on these issues, which many consider serious and contentious, empowering enthusiasts and diering perspectives. Listeners are invited to tune into the podcast and potentially join the thriving community associated with 2 If By Sea Tactical.
