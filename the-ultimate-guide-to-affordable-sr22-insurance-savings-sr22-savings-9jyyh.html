Understanding SR22 Insurance and Its Importance

SR22 insurance is a certification required by state DMVs for high-risk drivers. It confirms that you maintain the minimum auto insurance coverage mandated by law. This type of insurance is typically needed after a serious driving violation, such as a DUI or multiple traffic offenses. Complying with SR22 requirements is crucial for reinstating your driver's license and avoiding legal penalties.
