Understanding ACNC Governance Standards

The Australian Charities and Not-for-profits Commission (ACNC) sets forth governance standards that NFPs must adhere to for transparent and effective operation. These standards are essential not just for ensuring compliance but also for fostering trust with donors and beneficiaries. Board responsibilities, including overseeing financial management and decision-making processes, are critical components of governance. Adequate insurance coverage, particularly through ACS Financial, can provide support in fulfilling these responsibilities while complying with ACNC regulations.
