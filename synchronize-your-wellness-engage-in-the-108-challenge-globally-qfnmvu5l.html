Synchronize Your Wellness: Engage in the 108 Challenge Globally

The 108 Event represents a revolutionary movement that unites participants across the globe, aligning with nature's cycles during the solstices and equinoxes. By engaging in the practice of 108, enthusiasts partake in yoga and meditation, fostering a deeper relationship with their circadian rhythm while cultivating community mindfulness. This extraordinary global experience invites everyone, irrespective of their backgrounds, to get involved in yoga meditation practices that facilitate journeys of self-discovery as well as a collaborative consciousness. Each practice becomes a conduit for transformational growth and holistic longevity, drawing upon the vitality of sun salutations and moon salutations that resonate with consistent universal principles. The September Equinox is particularly significant, gathering individuals from diverse cultural backgrounds to honor balance and progress. Participants are encouraged to navigate their unique experience of the practice of 108, with formats including guided meditations or immersive sessions. Established by Carolyn Bentley Wells, The 108 Event accentuates the remarkable advantages of synchronized practices, fostering mental acuity and physical health. Join us on this enlightening expedition towards interconnectedness, resilience, and intentional living, where every gesture moves us closer to our individual and collective truth.

The Essence of 108: A Mindfulness Odyssey

The practice of 108 represents a significant blend of cultural traditions founded in yoga and meditation. This revered numeral signifies completeness and the totality of the cosmos. Participants engage in a range of activities including surya namaskara and chandra namaskara, which foster physical vitality and mental clarity. Undertaking the 108 challenge encourages individuals to seek personal development and a sense of community, further enriching their mindfulness expedition.

Unity in Community Mindfulness: A Connection of Hearts

At The 108 Event, our belief in community mindfulness forms the core of our events. Uniting around momentous astronomical occurrences cultivates a feeling of togetherness and common mission. Whether in person or virtually, participants share narratives and practice through guided meditations and collaborative experiences. This community approach allows individuals to refine their personal practices and cultivate new friendships in a nurturing atmosphere.

Frequently Asked Questions

The practice of 108 involves performing a sequence of 108 repetitions of yoga styles or meditation techniques at designated times, with the aim of enhancing mindfulness and fostering spiritual connections.

To join in, partake in our synchronized wellness activities scheduled during the solstices and equinoxes, either physically with us or through digital participation, featuring guided meditation sessions.

Sun salutations (surya namaskara) are sequences designed to awaken energy in the morning, while moon salutations (chandra namaskara) offer calming practices best suited for evening relaxation.

Engaging in synchronized wellness practices strengthens community bonds, heightens mental clarity, aligns with the circadian rhythm, and supports individual growth and mindfulness.

Typically, participation is free; however, we do offer special immersive passes for those interested in enhanced guided experiences and support during the events.

Is home practice allowed during the event?
Absolutely! Participants can engage in distance practices at home synchronizing their efforts with global activities, ensuring inclusivity without relinquishing community ties.

What equipment is required for the practice of 108?
You will need a yoga mat, comfortable attire, and a dedication to your practice. Additionally, props like blocks, straps, or cushions can be helpful for support.
