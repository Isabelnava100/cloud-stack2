Is there pain associated with orthodontic care?

Most of our patients report mild discomfort during adjustments or switching to a new aligner. This generally fades quickly, with over-the-counter pain relief available if needed.

Does insurance provide coverage for orthodontic treatment?

Insurance coverage for orthodontic services differs by plan. Our staff is here to help you navigate your insurance benefits and any available financing alternatives.
