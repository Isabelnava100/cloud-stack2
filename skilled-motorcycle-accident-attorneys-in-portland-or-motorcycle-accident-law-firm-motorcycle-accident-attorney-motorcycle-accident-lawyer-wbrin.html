How long do I have to file a claim after a motorcycle accident?
In Oregon, you generally have two years from the accident occurrence to file a personal injury claim.

Can I still file a claim if I was partially at fault?
Yes, Oregon has a comparative negligence standard. You may still obtain damages, although the total compensation could decrease based on your degree of fault.
