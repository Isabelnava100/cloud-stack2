Our Broom Finished Concrete Driveway Services

Why Opt for Broom Finished Concrete?

Broom finished concrete provides a textured, non-slip surface making it a safer choice for driveways suitable for both vehicles and pedestrian traffic. This finish enhances traction, securing stability during rain and snow. Beyond safety, broom finished concrete elevates the property's visual appeal with its interesting texture. Its durability means it can resist severe weather, thus representing a wise investment for long-term use.

Caring for Your Driveway: Maintenance Tips

Maintaining your broom finished concrete driveway in optimal condition is vital. We suggest regular sealing to safeguard the surface against stains and environmental deterioration. Routine cleaning, using mild detergent and water, facilitates the removal of dirt and debris. Forward-looking repairs of any cracks or chips are crucial to extend your driveway's lifespan. The professionals at Mike's Concrete ensure you receive guidance and support for keeping your driveway looking new.
