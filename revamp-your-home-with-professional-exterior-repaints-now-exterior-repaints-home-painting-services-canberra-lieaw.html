Additional FAQ

Can you carry out work during inclement weather?

We organise our work around weather conditions to guarantee the best results; however, extreme weather may create delays to protect our team and ensure quality.

What steps should I take for preparation before painting starts?

We suggest removing outdoor furniture and decorations as well as allowing access to all areas requiring repainting. Our team will guide you through each step of the preparation process.
