Ascendant Medical’s RCM Solutions for Physician Practices

Comprehensive Revenue Cycle Management Solutions

Our comprehensive revenue cycle management services cover the entire billing lifecycle—from charge capture to claims submission and account follow-ups. We handle every step with precision, ensuring maximum reimbursement and compliance with payer requirements. By providing real-time insights into your claims status and denial trends, we allow practices to make informed, data-driven decisions that positively impact revenue flow. Additionally, our expertise in specialized areas such as surgical billing and CMS care models means greater accuracy in managing complex billing scenarios.

Fast Claim Processing for Enhanced Cash Flow

Trust Ascendant Medical to deliver fast claim processing that accelerates cash flow and reduces administrative burdens on your practice. Our integration of Robotic Process Automation (RPA) streamlines repetitive tasks such as payment posting and claim submissions. This high level of automation minimizes human error, ensuring that claims are handled quickly and accurately. With our solutions, practices can experience faster turnaround on claims, leading to better financial stability and increased collections over time.

Denials Management and A/R Recovery

Effective denials management and accounts receivable (A/R) recovery are critical to ensuring that your practice maximizes revenue potential. At Ascendant Medical, we specialize in identifying and resolving denied claims promptly. Our team aggressively follows up on unresolved claims to recover lost revenue and reduce write-offs. By employing advanced analytics and industry knowledge, we minimize denial rates and ensure smoother collections, ultimately enhancing your practice’s financial performance.
