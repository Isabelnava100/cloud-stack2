Caring for Your Bamboo Bedding

To ensure the longevity of your bamboo quilt covers and weighted blankets, machine wash them on a gentle cycle with mild detergent. Avoid bleach and opt for line drying where possible to maintain the fabric's integrity. Regular cleaning keeps your bedding fresh and promotes a healthier sleeping environment. Enjoy the benefits of your eco-friendly bedding with minimal care requirements.
