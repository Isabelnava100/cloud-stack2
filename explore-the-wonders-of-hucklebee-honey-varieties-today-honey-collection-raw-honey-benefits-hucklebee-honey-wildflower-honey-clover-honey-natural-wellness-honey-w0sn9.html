Raw Honey vs. Processed Honey: What’s the Difference?

Understanding the difference between raw honey and processed honey is crucial for choosing quality products. Raw honey is harvested directly from the hive and remains unfiltered and unheated, retaining its natural properties. In contrast, processed honey undergoes filtering and heating, which can diminish its nutritional benefits. Choosing raw honey like Hucklebee honey ensures you receive the full spectrum of health benefits and natural flavors.
