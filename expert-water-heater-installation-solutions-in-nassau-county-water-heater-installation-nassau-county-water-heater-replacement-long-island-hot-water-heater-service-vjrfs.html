Tank and Tankless Water Heaters Explained

Are you deciding between tank and tankless water heaters? At Home+ Air and Heat, we will help you understand the advantages of both types. Tank water heaters keep hot water on standby, a great choice for families needing high volumes quickly. Conversely, tankless models provide heated water only as it's needed, improving energy efficiency and ensuring an endless supply. Our specialists will assist you in identifying the best solution catered to your household's requirements, guaranteeing that your investment is well-informed and aligned with your way of living.

How can I enhance the lifespan of my water heater?

Performing regular maintenance, such as flushing the tank and inspecting the anode rod, can greatly prolong the life of your water heater.
