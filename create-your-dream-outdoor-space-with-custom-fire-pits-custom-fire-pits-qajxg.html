What maintenance is required for custom fire pits?
Maintaining your custom fire pit is essential to keep it looking exquisite and functioning properly. Routine maintenance typically involves cleaning out ash and debris after use, which helps prevent rust and buildup. Depending on the materials used, you may need to periodically seal the stone or brick to protect it from the elements. Additionally, inspecting the fire pit for any signs of wear or damage will help ensure longevity. Our team at Bret-Mar Landscaping is always available for maintenance tips, or we offer professional services to keep your outdoor fire features in top condition.

Are your outdoor fire features safe for use?
Yes, safety is a priority when designing and installing our exquisite outdoor fire features. At Bret-Mar Landscaping, we follow local codes and safety regulations to ensure that each custom fire pit is installed safely and securely. We provide recommendations on safety measures, such as clearances from flammable materials and proper ventilation. Our team is committed to education, ensuring you understand how to properly operate your fire pit while minimizing risks. With our careful attention to safety, you can enjoy your fire feature with peace of mind, creating cherished memories around the fire.
