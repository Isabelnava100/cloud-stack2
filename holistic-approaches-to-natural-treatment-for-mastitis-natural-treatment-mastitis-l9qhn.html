Recognizing Mastitis and Its Symptoms

Mastitis represents an inflammation of the breast tissue, often resulting in pain and possible infection. Symptoms may manifest as swelling, redness, and tenderness of the breast. In addition, you might experience flu-like symptoms such as fever and chills, impacting overall health. Identifying these symptoms promptly is essential to facilitate effective natural treatment for mastitis, as timely action can help avert complications.
