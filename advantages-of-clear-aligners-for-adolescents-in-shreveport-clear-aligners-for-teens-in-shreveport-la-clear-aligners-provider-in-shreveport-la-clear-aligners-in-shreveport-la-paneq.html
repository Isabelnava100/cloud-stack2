All About Clear Aligners

What Exactly Are Clear Aligners?

Clear aligners are advanced orthodontic appliances designed to gently guide teeth into proper alignment without the need for metal braces. These discrete, custom-fabricated trays are made to fit comfortably over the teeth and are less conspicuous, making them especially popular among adolescents. Using specialized 3D imaging, we create each set of aligners specifically to match the unique shape of a patient’s mouth. At Graf Orthodontics, we focus on providing clear aligners custom-fit for teenage patients in Shreveport, LA.

How long is the typical treatment duration for clear aligners?

The length of treatment varies per person but usually lasts between 6 months and up to 2 years, influenced by the complexity of each case.

What steps should I take if a clear aligner is lost or damaged?

If an aligner is misplaced or breaks, it's essential to reach out to Graf Orthodontics as soon as possible. We can assist with advice and provide a replacement to ensure the treatment remains on schedule.
