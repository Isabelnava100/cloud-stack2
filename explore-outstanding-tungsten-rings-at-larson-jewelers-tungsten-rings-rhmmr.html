Maintaining Your Tungsten Ring

Proper maintenance of your tungsten ring can enhance its lifespan and preserve its stunning looks. Despite being incredibly durable, it's crucial to keep it away from extreme heat and harsh substances that could tarnish its surface. Regularly cleaning your ring with warm soapy water and a gentle cloth will keep it gleaming as beautifully as the moment you first wore it.
