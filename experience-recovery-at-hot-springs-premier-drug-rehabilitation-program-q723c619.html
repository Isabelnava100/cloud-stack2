Our Approach to Recovery: Merging Faith with Professional Guidance for Transformative Outcomes

A Variety of Comprehensive Treatment Options

At SOZO Recovery Center, we recognize that the path to recovery varies for everyone. That's why we deliver an extensive range of care that includes medical detox, residential services, partial hospitalization (PHP), intensive outpatient (IOP), and outpatient care. Our committed professionals develop treatment plans aimed at meeting the specific needs of each individual man, combining tailored individual and group therapy with faith-based practices. This thoughtful approach ensures that clients receive essential support through every step of their recovery journey.

Integrating Spirituality in the Recovery Process

At SOZO, we emphasize the crucial role spirituality plays in overcoming addiction. Our Christian-informed methods weave faith throughout therapy, empowering clients to build their spiritual foundation while tackling the emotional and psychological aspects of their struggles. This confluence of clinical support and spiritual exploration offers deeper self-awareness and resilience against potential relapses. Our devoted staff accompanies each man on their spiritual journey, enhancing their recovery experience and instilling hope.

Support After Treatment: Reintegration into Society

The process of recovery extends beyond the completion of our programs at SOZO Recovery Center. We highlight the necessity of ongoing aftercare and community reintegration for sustained success in sobriety. Our facility nurtures strong partnerships with local communities and health insurance providers to facilitate continued client support once treatment ends. Offering ongoing counseling, support groups, and practical life skills resources, we prepare individuals to face everyday challenges while firmly anchored in their recovery journey. Our aim is for every client to flourish and re-enter society driven by purpose and newfound hope.
