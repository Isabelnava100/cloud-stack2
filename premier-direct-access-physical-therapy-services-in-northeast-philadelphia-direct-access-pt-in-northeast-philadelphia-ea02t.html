Overview of Direct Access Physical Therapy

Defining Direct Access Physical Therapy

Direct access physical therapy grants individuals the ability to receive treatment from certified physical therapists without a recommendation from a physician. This enables you to initiate your rehabilitation quickly, facilitating a faster recovery process. At Capstone Physical Therapy, we focus on providing a smooth entry into care. By making therapy more accessible, you can enjoy customized treatment without unnecessary waiting.
