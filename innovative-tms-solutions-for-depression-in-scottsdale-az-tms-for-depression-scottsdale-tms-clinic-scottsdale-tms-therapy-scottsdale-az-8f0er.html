Introduction to TMS Therapy

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is an innovative approach for treating major depression and anxiety disorders. This gentle, non-invasive technique uses magnetic fields to activate nerve cells in the brain, focused on alleviating symptoms when conventional treatments have not been effective. Our Scottsdale TMS clinic is equipped with the latest technology and supported by a dedicated team that will guide you throughout the treatment experience.
