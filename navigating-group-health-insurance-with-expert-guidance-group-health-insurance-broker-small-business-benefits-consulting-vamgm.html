Understanding Your Options for Group Health Insurance

Fully Insured Plans

Fully insured plans are a traditional approach where businesses pay a premium to the insurance carrier, covering employee healthcare costs. This option is straightforward, providing predictable monthly expenses and comprehensive coverage. At CFH Insurance Consultants, we guide you through various fully insured options from all major Michigan carriers, ensuring you find the best fit for your employees' needs.

Level-Funded Health Plans

Level-funded health plans are a hybrid option that combines features of fully insured and self-funded plans. Businesses pay a fixed monthly fee, which covers a portion of expected claims and provides cost control. Our team at CFH Insurance Consultants assists you in structuring these plans to optimize cost savings while maintaining quality care for your employees. We simplify the complex decision-making process for your small business.

Self-Funded Health Plans

Self-funded health plans allow small and mid-sized businesses to take more control over their healthcare costs by using their own funds to cover employee health claims. This option often leads to substantial savings, especially for healthy cohorts. CFH Insurance Consultants helps you assess whether a self-funded approach is right for your business, offering year-round consulting support to navigate these options successfully.
