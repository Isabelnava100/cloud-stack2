Frequently Asked Questions

Emily Schupmann Counseling and Associates, PLLC provides personalized counseling services tailored to individual needs. We focus on various areas including anxiety, depression, relationship issues, trauma, and personal development. Our goal is to create a supportive environment where clients can explore their feelings and experiences, ultimately leading to healing and growth.

Counseling at Emily Schupmann Counseling involves open communication and a collaborative approach. During sessions, clients share their stories, and together we identify goals and aspirations. Using measurable tasks and evidence-based tools, we work towards achieving these goals, creating a structured yet flexible plan for healing.

During your first session, you can expect a warm and welcoming environment. The therapist will ask questions to understand your background, current challenges, and goals for counseling. This session is an opportunity for you to share your story and for the therapist to outline how they can support you in your journey.

Scheduling an appointment with Emily Schupmann Counseling is easy! You can visit our website to complete a contact form, or call our office directly. We'll work with you to find a convenient time for your initial consultation. We're committed to making the process as smooth and supportive as possible.

At Emily Schupmann Counseling, we utilize a variety of therapeutic approaches tailored to individual client needs. This may include cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), talk therapy, and mindfulness techniques. The selection of the approach will depend on the client's specific situation and preferences, ensuring a personalized experience.

Is counseling confidential?
Yes, counseling at Emily Schupmann Counseling is strictly confidential. We adhere to ethical guidelines and legal regulations to protect your privacy. Anything discussed in sessions stays between you and your therapist, with exceptions only for safety concerns or legal obligations as defined by state law.

What if I feel hesitant to start counseling?
Feeling hesitant to start counseling is completely normal. Taking the step to ask for help is a courageous move, and Emily Schupmann Counseling is here to support you through that process. We encourage open communication, and during your initial session, you can discuss your concerns and questions. Our goal is to create a safe space where you can explore your feelings and consider the potential for healing.
