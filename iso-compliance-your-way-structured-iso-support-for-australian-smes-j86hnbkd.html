Additional FAQ

Is IntegriSURE an ISO consultant?

IntegriSURE is not positioned as a traditional ISO consultancy. It is a structured ISO management system support environment that provides documents, guidance, tools and staged support. Higher levels of support may include more direct assistance, but the core model is designed to help SMEs maintain ownership of their system.

What is the difference between ISO compliance and ISO certification?

ISO compliance means your management system has been aligned to the requirements of the relevant ISO standard. ISO certification means an independent certification body has audited your system and confirmed that it meets the standard. IntegriSURE supports management system development and certification preparation, but it does not issue ISO certificates.

What is the best starting point for an SME new to ISO?

For most SMEs that are unsure where to begin, the ISO Starter Pack is the lowest-risk starting point. It helps you understand what ISO involves and how the IntegriSURE pathway works. Once you are ready to begin properly, you can unlock the system and progress into implementation.

Can IntegriSURE support integrated management systems?

Yes. IntegriSURE supports integrated management systems covering ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 45001. This can help reduce duplication across policies, procedures, risk management, document control, internal audits, management reviews and continual improvement.

Is IntegriSURE suitable for tender-driven SMEs?

Yes. IntegriSURE is suitable for SMEs that need ISO certification or management system evidence for tenders, client requirements, supply chain prequalification or growth. The pathway helps businesses prepare earlier rather than waiting until a tender deadline creates pressure.

What makes IntegriSURE different from buying templates online?

Template packs usually provide documents only. IntegriSURE provides a structured pathway around the documents, including guidance, learning resources, implementation support options and staged progression. This helps your organisation move from ownership to implementation and ongoing management.

Can we start small and add more support later?

Yes. IntegriSURE is designed around staged support. Your organisation can begin with a low-risk entry point and add more guidance or support as your needs, confidence and certification timeline develop.

Does IntegriSURE replace an external certification body?

No. IntegriSURE does not replace an independent certification body. Your organisation will still need to engage an appropriate certification body if formal ISO certification is required.
