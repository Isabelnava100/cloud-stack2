Seedless Media to Participate in Buds N Bikinis Poolside Celebration Featuring Kirko Bangz

MESA, Ariz. (July 8, 2025) — Seedless Media, an innovative agency dedicated to the progressive cannabis industry and thoughtful brand storytelling, is thrilled to announce our participation in the upcoming Buds N Bikinis event, occurring on Saturday, July 26, 2025, at the Arizona Golf Resort in Mesa, Arizona.

This exclusive 21+ poolside event promises an exhilarating experience with a live show by platinum-selling artist Kirko Bangz. Renowned for his chart-topping hit “Drank In My Cup,” Bangz is anticipated to energize the desert soirée.

Guests will be treated to full pool access, thrilling water slide fun, DJ performances, food vendors, and much more. For the safety and comfort of all attendees, a strict clear bag policy will be implemented.


Seedless Media will be on-site producing content, networking with various brands, and highlighting the confluence of cannabis, lifestyle, and culture that events like Buds N Bikinis encapsulate. “We're eager to engage with both creators and consumers in a vibrant environment that resonates with our mission,” stated Devon Buri, Content & Customer Acquisitions Manager at Seedless Media.

 

In line with our ongoing dedication to supporting culture-forward events and community interaction, we warmly encourage attendees to visit us, share greetings, and follow our event coverage on our digital channels.
