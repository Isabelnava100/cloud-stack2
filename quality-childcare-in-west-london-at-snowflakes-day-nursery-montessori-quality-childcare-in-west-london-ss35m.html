Our Structured 'Work Cycle' System

The structured 'work cycle' at Snowflakes Day Nursery promotes exploration and choice, key components of effective childcare. Each day, children rotate between educational stations that include theme-related activities, practical life skills, and creative play. This system empowers children to make choices about their learning while having access to guided activities that ensure academic progress.
