Types of Landscape Sand

At G3 Enterprises, we offer a variety of landscape sand options to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our decomposed granite is ideal for erosion control and pathways, providing excellent drainage and a natural aesthetic. River sand is perfect for decorative purposes and can be used in landscaping features such as water gardens. For children's play areas, our play sand is safe, clean, and free from contaminants, ensuring a safe environment for kids. Lastly, our fill sand is extremely versatile and serves as a reliable base material for construction and landscaping projects, providing stability and durability in various applications.
