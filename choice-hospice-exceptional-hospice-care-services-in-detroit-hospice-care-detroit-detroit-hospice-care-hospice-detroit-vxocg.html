Frequently Asked Questions

Hospice care refers to a specialized medical service aimed at individuals with terminal illnesses. Its focus lies on comfort, pain management, and overall quality of life rather than seeking curative solutions.

Choice Hospice provides routine care, respite care, continuous care, short-term inpatient services, alongside vital hospice supplies and equipment.

If your loved one's diagnosis includes a terminal illness and they have chosen to forego curative methods, hospice care can offer the necessary comfort and resources.

Most insurance providers, including Medicare, offer coverage for hospice care services. Choice Hospice collaborates closely with insurance organizations to ensure eligible patients receive appropriate coverage.

Our hospice care team is made up of qualified physicians, nurses, social workers, pharmacists, and chaplains who work collectively to deliver all-encompassing care for our patients.
