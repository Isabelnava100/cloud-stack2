Water Damage Restoration and Repair Solutions

Our Water Damage Mitigation Approach

At Advanced Restoration SC, we adopt a methodical approach to water damage restoration. Our process begins with a comprehensive evaluation of your home to gauge the damage's severity. Subsequently, we utilize our advanced extraction tools for effective water removal, reducing the potential for further harm. Post-water removal, we conduct drying and dehumidification interventions to ensure your surroundings are completely dry, eliminating any risks of mold infestation and other complications.

Thorough Repairs Following Water Damage

After completing the water extraction and drying stages, our attention turns to thorough repairs. Acknowledging that water damage can produce structural challenges, our skilled technicians will repair drywall, flooring, and any areas impacted by the flood. Our objective is to revert your property to its condition prior to the damage, utilizing premium materials and expert workmanship. We collaborate closely with you throughout the renovation to ensure every repair meets your expectations.

Managing water damage can be overwhelming, particularly concerning insurance matters. Our dedicated team will meticulously document all damage and furnish you with the required documentation to assist you in obtaining the compensation you need. Our goal is to ease your burden and allow you to prioritize what matters most while we manage the intricacies of your water damage restoration.
