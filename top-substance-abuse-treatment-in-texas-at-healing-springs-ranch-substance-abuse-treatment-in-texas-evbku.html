Understanding Substance Abuse

Substance abuse is a complex issue that affects individuals and families across Texas. It involves the harmful or hazardous use of psychoactive substances, which can lead to significant health problems and altered life circumstances. Effective treatment focuses on addressing the psychological, emotional, and social aspects of addiction, ensuring a comprehensive approach to recovery. Understanding the nature of substance abuse is crucial for finding effective treatment solutions that work.
