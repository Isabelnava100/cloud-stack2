Maintaining Your Nonstick Cookware

To prolong the life of your nonstick pans, appropriate maintenance is crucial. Refrain from using metal tools that might damage the surface and diminish the nonstick effect. Instead, choose silicone or wooden tools during cookware use. Regularly wash your pans by hand with warm, soapy water, and avoid placing them in the dishwasher, which can deteriorate the nonstick finish over time. Following these simple maintenance guidelines will ensure that your nonstick pans deliver years of enjoyable cooking.
