What should I wear to my first class?
For your first class at Element Martial Arts Brisbane, we recommend wearing comfortable athletic clothing that allows for a full range of motion. If you're trying Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, a gi (uniform) is typically worn, but you can start in a t-shirt and shorts if you don't have one yet. Make sure to bring a water bottle to stay hydrated during your training. Our instructors will guide you on any specific attire needed for the class you choose.
