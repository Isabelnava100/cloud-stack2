How long do I have after a car accident to file a personal injury claim?
In Maryland, you generally need to file your personal injury claim within three years of the accident date, although initiating the process sooner is advisable.

What can I do if the other driver does not have insurance?
If the other driver lacks insurance, your own policy may cover the damages if you have uninsured motorist coverage, and securing a lawyer might be crucial for managing this situation.
