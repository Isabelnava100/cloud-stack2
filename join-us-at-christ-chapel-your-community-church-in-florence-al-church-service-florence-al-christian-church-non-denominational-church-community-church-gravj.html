Join Us at Christ Chapel: Your Community Church in Florence, AL

At Christ Chapel, we invite you to experience a vibrant non-denominational Christian church in Florence, AL. Our community church focuses on awakening the spirit within each individual through the powerful message of the gospel. Weekly church services occur every Sunday at 9 am and 11 am, providing an opportunity to connect with God and others. Join us both on-site and online to fellowship and nourish your faith journey. Our mission is to empower every person to live and love like Jesus, the embodiment of compassion and grace. We believe that experiencing the Holy Spirit transforms lives, enabling our church community to grow in faith and love. Whether you are new to church or seeking a fresh spiritual awakening, Christ Chapel welcomes you with open arms. Together, we will embark on a journey of spiritual growth and community service, reflecting Christ’s love in our everyday lives.

Welcome to Christ Chapel: A Beacon of Faith

Worship Services

At Christ Chapel, our worship services play a central role in building connections and deepening faith. We invite everyone to join us for uplifting experiences every Sunday at 9 am and 11 am. Our services capture the essence of community, as we gather to worship, pray, and learn together. Whether you attend on-site or online, you will find a welcoming atmosphere filled with love and purpose.

Community Outreach

Community outreach is a vital part of our mission at Christ Chapel. We believe in living out the gospel not just in church but in our everyday lives. Our church initiatives serve those in need within Florence, AL, fostering a spirit of generosity and support among our members. Join us in making a difference through various outreach programs and volunteer opportunities.

Spiritual Growth Opportunities

At Christ Chapel, we provide numerous opportunities for spiritual growth and connection. From Bible study groups to prayer meetings, we aim to deepen understanding and cultivate faith. Our non-denominational Christian environment encourages open discussion, allowing everyone to explore their beliefs. Join us in these growth activities to enrich your spiritual journey and connect with fellow believers.
