Supplemental Coverage Options Explained

Supplemental health insurance provides additional coverage that can enhance your primary health plan. This type of insurance is especially beneficial for covering out-of-pocket costs or services not included in your primary policy. At SWI Protects, our local health insurance agents can walk you through the various supplemental options available and help you determine if adding this coverage aligns with your healthcare goals. Choosing the right supplemental insurance can offer you peace of mind and greater financial security as you navigate your health journey.

How do I know if I need supplemental health insurance?
You may need supplemental health insurance if you have high out-of-pocket medical costs or want additional coverage for specific services.

Can I change my health insurance plan during the year?
Generally, you can change your health insurance plan during the annual open enrollment period or if you experience a qualifying life event.
