Understanding ACA Compliance Obligations for ALEs

The Affordable Care Act outlines specific obligations for Applicable Large Employers (ALEs) in Michigan. Compliance with these regulations is critical to avoid penalties associated with non-compliance. Employers must provide minimum essential coverage (MEC) and demonstrate that their health plans meet the minimum value (MV) requirements.
