Understanding Arthritis and Its Impact on Mobility

Arthritis is a condition characterized by inflammation of the joints, which can lead to pain, stiffness, and a decrease in mobility. Understanding the types and causes of arthritis is crucial for effective treatment. It affects millions of people but can be managed with proper chiropractic care.
