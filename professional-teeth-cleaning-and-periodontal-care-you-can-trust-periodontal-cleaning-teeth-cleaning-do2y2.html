Teeth Cleaning and Periodontal Cleaning Services

Importance of Routine Teeth Cleaning

Routine teeth cleaning is essential for preventing cavities and enhancing gum health. During a thorough cleaning, our dental hygienists meticulously remove plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing may miss. This proactive approach not only facilitates a brighter smile but also significantly reduces the risk of developing gum disease. Consistent cleanings are crucial for individuals who may be prone to dental issues, ensuring that any concerns are addressed promptly.

Periodontal Cleaning Explained

Periodontal cleaning, also known as deep cleaning, targets areas beneath the gum line where bacteria can thrive. This process not only involves cleaning the teeth but also scaling and root planing to remove stubborn deposits. Our dental professionals provide personalized assessments to determine the frequency of periodontal cleanings you may need based on your health status. By prioritizing this type of cleaning, you are investing in the long-term health of your gums and overall dental hygiene.

Aftercare and Maintenance

After your teeth and periodontal cleaning, it is essential to maintain a diligent oral care routine. Our team will provide you with tailored aftercare instructions to ensure optimal healing and effectiveness of the cleaning. Regular brushing, flossing, and following up with dental check-ups play a vital role in sustaining gum health. We encourage our patients to take an active role in their dental care, preventing the accumulation of plaque between visits, and ensuring a vibrant smile for years to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most dental professionals recommend getting your teeth cleaned every six months. However, if you have gum disease or other dental issues, more frequent visits may be necessary.

Teeth cleaning typically addresses surface plaque and tartar, while periodontal cleaning involves deeper scaling and root planing to remove bacteria below the gum line.

While many patients experience minimal discomfort during a routine teeth cleaning, our team uses gentle techniques to ensure your comfort throughout the process.

A typical teeth cleaning appointment usually lasts about 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the extent of the cleaning required.
