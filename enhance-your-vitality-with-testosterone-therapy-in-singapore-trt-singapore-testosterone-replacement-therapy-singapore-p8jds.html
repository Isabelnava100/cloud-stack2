Understanding Low Testosterone

Low testosterone, or hypogonadism, significantly affects many men as they age, particularly after 35. Symptoms often include profound fatigue, diminished libido, muscle loss, and cognitive fog, which can occur insidiously. At PROTOCOL Health, our focus is on recognizing these changes early through comprehensive health screenings.
