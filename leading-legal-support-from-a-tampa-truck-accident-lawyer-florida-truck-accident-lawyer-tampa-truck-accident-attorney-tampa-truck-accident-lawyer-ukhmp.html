Extra FAQ

What happens if I share some responsibility for the truck accident?

Florida adheres to modified comparative negligence principles, which allow for partial recovery of damages despite shared fault, but such compensation could be reduced.

Is it feasible to manage a truck accident claim without legal representation?

While it’s not impossible, maneuvering through the complexities tied to truck accident claims can be arduous; an experienced Tampa truck accident attorney can notably augment your chances of achieving a satisfactory resolution.
