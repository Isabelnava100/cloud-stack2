Discover Your Ideal Boutique Surf Holiday in Morocco

Welcome to Salt House Morocco, the epitome of a boutique surf holiday. Nestled along the stunning Atlantic coast in Tamraght, we offer an immersive surf and yoga experience tailored for all levels. Since 2017, we have blended local culture with our top-notch facilities, ensuring your stay is not just about surfing but also about relaxation and connection. Our dedicated team is committed to providing personalized coaching and creating a welcoming environment where you can thrive. At Salt House Morocco, we pride ourselves on our quality-over-quantity approach to surf coaching. With a maximum of six surfers per instructor, we ensure that each guest receives focused attention and valuable feedback. Enjoy the beauty of Morocco's consistent right-hand point breaks while benefitting from ISA-certified coaching and a variety of holistic wellness options. Your journey here goes beyond just surfing; it's about community, culture, and unforgettable memories in an idyllic setting.
