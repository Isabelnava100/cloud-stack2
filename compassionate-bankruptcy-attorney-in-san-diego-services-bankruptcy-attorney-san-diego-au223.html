Bankruptcy Services Offered

Chapter 7 Bankruptcy

Chapter 7 bankruptcy is designed for individuals seeking to eliminate most unsecured debts, including credit card bills and medical expenses. This process allows you to quickly discharge your debts and regain your financial footing under the guidance of a knowledgeable bankruptcy attorney in San Diego. Our team assists clients in navigating the paperwork and meeting all necessary eligibility requirements, ensuring a smooth process throughout their bankruptcy journey.

Chapter 13 Bankruptcy

For those with a steady income but overwhelmed by debt, Chapter 13 bankruptcy offers a potential solution by restructuring payments. This type of bankruptcy allows you to keep your property while making manageable payments over a period of three to five years. Our dedicated bankruptcy attorneys in San Diego work with clients to create a personalized repayment plan that fits their financial situation, making sure to protect their rights and interests throughout the process.

Alternatives to Bankruptcy

Not every financial situation requires bankruptcy. At the Bankruptcy Law Center, we explore practical alternatives such as debt settlement, credit counseling, and consumer proposals. Our experienced attorneys in San Diego provide a comprehensive evaluation of your situation, identifying the best strategies to reduce debt and protect your financial future without needing to file for bankruptcy. We’re committed to finding the right solution tailored to your unique circumstances.

What debts can I eliminate through bankruptcy?
Bankruptcy can help eliminate most unsecured debts, such as credit card debt, medical bills, and personal loans. Certain debts like student loans, child support, and tax obligations typically cannot be discharged in bankruptcy.

What should I bring to my bankruptcy consultation?
When attending a bankruptcy consultation, it's helpful to bring financial documents such as income statements, a list of debts, recent bank statements, and any foreclosure notices or eviction letters.
