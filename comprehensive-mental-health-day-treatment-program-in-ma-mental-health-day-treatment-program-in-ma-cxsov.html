Can family members participate in the treatment process?
Yes, family members are encouraged to participate in the treatment process at South Shore Recovery Center. We recognize the importance of a support system in recovery, and involve families through educational sessions, family therapy, and support groups. These interactions help family members understand addiction and mental health challenges, improving communication and strengthening the support provided to individuals in treatment. Family involvement can significantly enhance the recovery experience.

What can I expect during my first visit to South Shore Recovery Center?
During your first visit to South Shore Recovery Center, you will participate in an initial assessment where your mental health and substance use history will be discussed. This session is a safe and confidential space for you to share your experiences and goals. Based on this assessment, our team will explain available treatment options and begin crafting a personalized plan tailored to your needs. Our focus is on ensuring you feel comfortable and supported from the very start of your recovery journey.
