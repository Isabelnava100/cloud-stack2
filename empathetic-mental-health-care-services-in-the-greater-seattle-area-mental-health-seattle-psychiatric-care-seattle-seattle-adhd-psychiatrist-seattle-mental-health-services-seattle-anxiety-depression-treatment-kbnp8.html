Empathetic Mental Health Care Services in the Greater Seattle Area

Lucent Psych proudly offers empathetic, evidence-informed psychiatric care for adults throughout Seattle and its neighboring communities. Under the expertise of Dr. Andrea Ancer Leal, DNP, PMHNP-BC, we provide a variety of mental health services that address conditions such as ADHD, anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, PTSD, OCD, and insomnia. Recognizing mental health as an integral part of overall wellness, we strive to ensure our services are reachable and inclusive for all who seek assistance. We operate through appointment-only, allowing for both face-to-face consultations and telehealth appointments for our Washington-based patients, streamlining the process of receiving support.

Thorough Psychiatric Assessments

At Lucent Psych, we conduct thorough psychiatric assessments intended to comprehensively evaluate mental health status. During your consultation, Dr. Andrea Ancer Leal will perform a detailed evaluation that will lead to developing an appropriate treatment strategy for issues like anxiety, depression, or bipolar disorder. Ongoing Medication Oversight

In Seattle, managing medications is a critical aspect of our psychiatric services. We carry out continuous evaluations to track the efficacy and potential side effects of the medications prescribed. Our aim is to refine medication management to ensure that every patient achieves optimal results. If you are in search of help for ADHD, relief from anxiety, or support for OCD, our knowledgeable team is dedicated to accompanying you on your healing path.
