Indications You Require Repair

Recognizing the signs that your hot water heater needs attention can save both time and money. Keep an eye out for symptoms like unpredictable water temperatures, unusual sounds from the heater, or puddles forming around the system. Furthermore, if you observe rust-colored water or a burning odor, prompt contact with McQuillan Home Services is crucial. Swiftly addressing these concerns can help avert more significant problems and guarantee a steady supply of hot water.

Tips for Maintenance

Effective upkeep can greatly enhance the longevity of your hot water heater. We advise performing a flush of your unit at least annually to eliminate built-up sediments. Additionally, taking a moment to verify the temperature setting, ensuring it hovers around 120 degrees, can optimize performance. Regular checks for any leaks or corrosion on visible pipes are also important.
