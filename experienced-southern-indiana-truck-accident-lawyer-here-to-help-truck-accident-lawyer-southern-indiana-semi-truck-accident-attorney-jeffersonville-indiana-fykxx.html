Common Questions

After a truck accident, ensure your safety and call for medical assistance. Document the scene with photos and gather as much information as possible, including witness contacts. Then, contact a truck accident lawyer to discuss your legal rights.

A truck accident lawyer can navigate the complexities of your case, handle communications with insurance companies, and advocate for your right to fair compensation for medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering.

Victims of truck accidents may be entitled to compensation for medical bills, rehabilitation costs, lost wages, pain and suffering, and property damage. Each case is unique, so discuss your specific situation with our attorneys.

At Karl Truman Law Office, we operate on a contingency-fee basis, meaning you only pay if we win your case. We offer a free consultation to discuss your situation without any financial obligation.

The timeline for a truck accident case can vary depending on the complexity of the case and the willingness of parties to negotiate. Generally, cases may take several months to a few years to resolve.
