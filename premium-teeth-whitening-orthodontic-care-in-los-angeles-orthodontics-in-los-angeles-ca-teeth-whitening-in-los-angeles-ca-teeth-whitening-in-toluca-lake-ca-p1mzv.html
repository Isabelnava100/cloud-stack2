Extra FAQ

Can I get teeth whitening with braces?

Whitening teeth while braces are in place is not recommended. We advise waiting until the end of your orthodontic treatment for optimal results.

What aftercare should I follow post-teeth whitening?

After teeth whitening treatment, it's advisable to avoid foods and beverages that may stain, uphold good oral hygiene practices, and continue to consult with our orthodontists for the best long-term results.
