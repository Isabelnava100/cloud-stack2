Frequently Asked Questions

At Fred Bloem MD LLC, we offer a wide range of holistic and integrative medicine services tailored for both men and women. Our key offerings include bioidentical hormone replacement therapies, anti-aging medicine, regenerative joint therapies, and intravenous therapies. We also specialize in biooxidative therapies, supportive cancer care, and the management of complex chronic illnesses like Lyme disease. Our goal is to address the root causes of health issues rather than merely alleviating symptoms.

Integrative medicine focuses on treating the whole person rather than just symptoms of a disease. It combines traditional medical practices with alternative therapies to promote optimal health. At our practice, we work with patients to develop personalized treatment plans that may include lifestyle changes, dietary modifications, and various therapies like bioidentical hormone replacement. By addressing the physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects of health, we aim to empower our patients and help them achieve their wellness goals.

Bioidentical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT) involves using hormones that are chemically identical to those naturally produced by the body. At Fred Bloem MD LLC, we assess hormone levels and, when necessary, provide tailored BHRT to alleviate symptoms associated with hormonal imbalances, such as fatigue, mood swings, and weight gain. This therapy aims to restore your hormonal balance, enhancing your overall health and quality of life.

Regenerative joint therapies focus on repairing and restoring damaged joints using the body’s natural healing processes. Conditions that can be treated include arthritis, tendonitis, ligament injuries, and other degenerative joint diseases. Our holistic approach encourages the body’s healing capabilities, aiming to reduce pain, improve mobility, and enhance overall joint function.

At Fred Bloem MD LLC, our approach to complex chronic illnesses begins with a thorough assessment of each patient’s medical history and current symptoms. We believe in identifying the underlying causes of these illnesses through detailed diagnostics, personalized treatment plans, and ongoing support. By utilizing integrative medicine strategies, including lifestyle modifications, dietary changes, and targeted therapies, we work closely with patients to improve their overall health and manage their conditions effectively.

What role does patient education play in your practice?
Patient education is a cornerstone of our practice at Fred Bloem MD LLC. We believe that informed patients are empowered to make better health decisions. Therefore, we take the time to explain diagnoses, treatments, and health strategies thoroughly. Our team encourages open dialogue and questions, ensuring that patients understand their health conditions and the rationale behind our chosen treatment plans, fostering a collaborative partnership in achieving their health goals.

Can you provide support for cancer care as a holistic doctor?
Yes, as a holistic doctor, we provide supportive cancer care at Fred Bloem MD LLC. Our approach focuses on enhancing the quality of life for patients undergoing cancer treatment through integrative therapies. These may include nutritional counseling, biooxidative therapies, and stress management techniques. We aim to support not just the physical aspects of cancer care but also the emotional and spiritual well-being of our patients, helping them navigate their journey with a comprehensive and empathetic approach.
