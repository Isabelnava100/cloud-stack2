What should I bring for my pet's stay?
When preparing for your pet's visit to Happy Pups Manor, we recommend bringing their favorite food, any necessary medications, and a few familiar toys or blankets to help them feel comfortable. While we provide all the essentials, having personal items can ease the transition for your furry companion and enhance their stay.

Can I visit Happy Pups Manor before booking?
Certainly! We encourage prospective clients to tour Happy Pups Manor prior to making a reservation. This gives you the chance to explore our facility, meet our dedicated team, and witness firsthand the safe and enjoyable environment we offer for pets. Please contact us to arrange a visit, and we’ll be delighted to show you around!
