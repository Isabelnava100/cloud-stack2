How can I introduce catnip to my cat?
Introducing catnip to your cat can be a fun experience! Start by offering a small amount of our organic catnip in a safe space where your cat feels comfortable. You can sprinkle it on their favorite toys or scratching posts, or even use it in our specially designed cat toys. Observe your cat's reaction; most cats will show excitement and playfulness. If your cat enjoys it, you can gradually increase the amount, but remember to give them breaks to prevent overstimulation.
