The installation process of our epoxy flooring begins with a thorough assessment of your space. Our epoxy garage floor installers prep the surface by cleaning and repairing it as needed. Once the surface is ready, we apply a primer, followed by the epoxy base coat. After that, we may add decorative flakes or colors, and finally seal the surface with a topcoat for enhanced durability. The entire process typically takes one to three days, depending on the size of the area and the specifics of your project.

The curing time for epoxy floors varies depending on temperature, humidity, and the specific products used. Generally, your new epoxy floor will be ready for light foot traffic in about 24 hours and can handle heavier loads in about 3 to 7 days. We recommend avoiding excessive moisture exposure and prolonged weight on the floor until it has fully cured. Our team will provide guidance on care and maintenance to ensure longevity and appeal.
