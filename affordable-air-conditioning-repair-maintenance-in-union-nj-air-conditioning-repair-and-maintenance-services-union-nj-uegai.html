What are the signs that my air conditioning system needs repair?
There are several indicators that your air conditioning system may require repair services. Common signs include unusual noises, such as rattling or grinding sounds, warm air blowing from the vents, fluctuating temperatures, increased energy bills, or water pooling around the unit. If you notice any of these symptoms, it's crucial to contact Kay’s Affordable HVAC&R for an assessment. Early diagnosis and repair can prevent larger issues and more expensive repairs down the line.

How can I ensure my HVAC system runs efficiently and lasts longer?
To ensure your HVAC system operates efficiently and has a long lifespan, regular maintenance is key. At Kay’s Affordable HVAC&R, we recommend scheduling preventive maintenance at least once a year for your heating and cooling systems. This includes cleaning filters, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting crucial components, and ensuring overall system functionality. Additionally, you can help by changing air filters regularly, keeping outdoor units clear of debris, and monitoring your energy usage for any discrepancies that may indicate problems.
