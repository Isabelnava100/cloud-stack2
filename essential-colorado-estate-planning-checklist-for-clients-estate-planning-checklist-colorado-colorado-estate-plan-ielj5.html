Understanding Key Estate Planning Documents

To create an effective Colorado estate plan, it's important to understand the essential documents involved. A will outlines how your assets will be distributed after your passing. On the other hand, a trust can help manage your assets during your lifetime and after. Powers of attorney designate someone to make decisions on your behalf, while healthcare directives ensure your medical wishes are honored. By having these documents in place, you can ensure your wishes are followed and minimize the burden on your loved ones.
