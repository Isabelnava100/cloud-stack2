Are there specific exercises to avoid injuries when over 30?
Whilst there is no specific exercise to avoid injury, there are movement patterns that we want to focus on to develop your resilience to injury. These are the super six movement patterns: squat, hinge, push, pull, press and rotary stability.

How can I tell if I need to adjust my training to prevent injury?
Signs to look for include persistent pain, fatigue, or decreased performance levels. Listening to your body and recognizing these signs can help you adjust your training to prevent injury.
