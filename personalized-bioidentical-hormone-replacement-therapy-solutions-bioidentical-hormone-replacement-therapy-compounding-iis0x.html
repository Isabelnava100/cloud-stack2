Understanding Bioidentical Hormones

Bioidentical hormones are molecularly identical to those naturally produced in the human body. This similarity allows for precise matching of hormone levels to individual patient needs. At UniMed Pharmacy, we focus on creating customized formulations that take into account both hormonal imbalances and patient-specific factors such as age, sex, and medical history. Our licensed pharmacists partner with healthcare providers to ensure that each bioidentical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT) regimen is safe and effective, adhering to strict compliance regulations while enhancing patient health outcomes.

Role of Compounding Pharmacies in BHRT

Compounding pharmacies play a vital role in the administration of bioidentical hormone replacement therapy. Unlike commercially available hormones, compounded hormones are tailored to meet the specific needs of individual patients. Our pharmacists at UniMed Pharmacy carefully formulate medications based on the prescriptions provided by licensed medical practitioners. This collaborative approach ensures that all aspects of a patient’s wellness are considered, resulting in therapies that are customized not only for strength but also for delivery methods and absorption rates, ultimately enhancing efficacy and patient satisfaction.
