Exceptional Daycare and Preschool Services at Discovery Time Learning Center

At Discovery Time Learning Center, we recognize the critical role of a supportive environment for early childhood education. Situated in Alexandria, VA, we provide outstanding daycare and preschool services specifically tailored for infants, toddlers, and preschoolers. Our devoted team of educators is dedicated to facilitating each child's personalized learning experience, fostering their development and well-being. Through our evidence-based curriculum, we inspire children to not only engage in learning but also discover their individuality and reach their full potential. Our primary goal is to assist families with their childcare requirements while cultivating a dynamic and affectionate setting for all children. We firmly believe that enriching early experiences are foundational to a child's success in school and life. By promoting exploration, play, and organized activities, children enrolled in our daycare and preschool programs engage in learning that nurtures curiosity, creativity, and confidence. The Discovery Time Learning Center stands not just as a childcare facility but as a community dedicated to supporting their growth and development.
