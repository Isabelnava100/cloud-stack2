Authentic matcha from Uji is a high-quality powdered green tea that is cultivated in the Uji region of Japan, known for its ideal climate and soil conditions for tea cultivation. Uji matcha is characterized by its rich umami flavor, vibrant green color, and fine texture. It is made from shade-grown tea leaves, which are hand-picked and stone-ground into a fine powder. This traditional method of production ensures that the matcha retains its natural nutrients and health benefits, making it a superior choice compared to conventional matcha by DoMatcha.

Matcha is renowned for its numerous health benefits, making it a popular choice among health-conscious individuals. It is rich in antioxidants, particularly catechins, which help combat oxidative stress and inflammation. Matcha also contains L-theanine, an amino acid that promotes relaxation and mental clarity without the jitters associated with caffeine. Regular consumption of matcha can boost metabolism, enhance energy levels, and support overall wellness. These matcha health benefits make it a valuable addition to a balanced diet.

Preparing matcha from DoMatcha is simple and rewarding. Start by sifting 1-2 teaspoons of matcha powder into a bowl to avoid clumps. Add a small amount of hot water (not boiling, around 175°F or 80°C) and whisk vigorously using a bamboo whisk (chasen) until frothy. You can adjust the water amount to achieve your desired consistency. For a creamier drink, you can add milk or a milk alternative. Enjoy your matcha hot or iced, and feel free to experiment with different recipes!
