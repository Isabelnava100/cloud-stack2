Cupping Therapy Overview and Benefits

What is Cupping Therapy?

Cupping therapy is an ancient holistic treatment that involves placing cups on the skin to create suction. This suction helps to improve blood flow, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation. Originating from traditional Chinese medicine, cupping therapy is gaining popularity due to its effectiveness in alleviating muscle pain and tension.
