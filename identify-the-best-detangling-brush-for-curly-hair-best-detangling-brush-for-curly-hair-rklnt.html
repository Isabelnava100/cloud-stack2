Can I use a detangling brush on wet hair?
Yes, you can use a detangling brush on wet hair, and it is often recommended for curly hair. Wet hair is more pliable, making it easier to detangle without causing breakage. However, it's important to use a brush specifically designed for wet hair, as these brushes are built to handle the extra tension and moisture. Always apply a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to help the brush glide through your curls more smoothly.
