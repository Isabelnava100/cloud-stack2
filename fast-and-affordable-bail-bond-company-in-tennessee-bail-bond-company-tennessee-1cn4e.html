The bail bond process begins when someone is arrested and a bail amount is set by the court. If the individual cannot afford to pay the bail, they can contact a bail bond company in Tennessee, like AffordABail, to help secure a bond. The bail bondsman will charge a fee, usually a percentage of the total bail amount, to post the bail on behalf of the individual. Once the bond is posted, the individual can be released from jail while awaiting their court date.
