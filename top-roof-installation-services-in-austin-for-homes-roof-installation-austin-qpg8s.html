How do I know if I need a roof replacement?
Determining whether you need a roof replacement involves evaluating the age of your roof, as most roofs last between 15-25 years, and assessing its overall condition. If your roof shows significant wear, including extensive leaks, mold growth, or severe damage, it may be time for a replacement. Regular inspections by an Austin roofing contractor can help identify these issues before they escalate. Cool Roofs Inc. provides thorough assessments and will guide you on the best options for your roof replacement needs.
