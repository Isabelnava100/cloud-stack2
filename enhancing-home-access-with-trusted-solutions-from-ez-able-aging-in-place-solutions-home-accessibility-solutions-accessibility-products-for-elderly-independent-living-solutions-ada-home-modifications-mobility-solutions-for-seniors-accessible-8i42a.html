Common Questions

Home accessibility solutions are products and modifications designed to enhance safety and mobility for seniors and individuals with disabilities within their living environments.

Installing features like curbless showers, grab bars, and non-slip flooring are key to creating an accessible bathroom that promotes safety and independence for seniors.

Common ADA home modifications include widening doorways, installing ramps, adding grab bars in bathrooms, and ensuring access to essential spaces like kitchens and living areas.

Curbless showers eliminate obstacles and provide a seamless entry, making it easier for elderly individuals with mobility challenges to bathe independently and safely.

Grab bars provide support and stability for seniors when moving around or using the bathroom, significantly reducing the risk of falls and enhancing overall home safety.
