Caring Psychiatry in Seattle for Mental Health Support

At Lucent Psych Seattle, we are committed to offering evidence-based psychiatric support for adults throughout Washington. Our focus encompasses mental health improvement and personal wellness. Under the leadership of Dr. Andrea Ancer Leal, DNP, PMHNP-BC, we are equipped to address a variety of mental health issues, including ADHD, anxiety disorders, depression, bipolar disorder, PTSD, OCD, and insomnia. Recognizing the solitude that can accompany mental health struggles, we work to foster a nurturing and empowering environment for our patients. Whether you prefer face-to-face appointments in our Seattle practice or opt for telehealth sessions, you will receive compassionate and professional care tailored specifically to your needs.
