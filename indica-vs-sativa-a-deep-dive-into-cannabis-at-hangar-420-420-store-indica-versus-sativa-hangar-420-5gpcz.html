Grasping Indica and Sativa

Indica and Sativa represent the two main classifications of cannabis, each bringing forth distinctive effects and advantages. Indica strains are often famed for their relaxing properties, making them an excellent choice for unwinding and promoting sleep. Conversely, Sativa strains tend to deliver revitalizing effects suitable for boosting creativity and aiding social engagement. At Hangar 420, we highlight the value of recognizing these differences to enrich your cannabis experience.
