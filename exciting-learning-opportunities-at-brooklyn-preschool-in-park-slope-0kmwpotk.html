Interactive Scientific Learning

At Brooklyn Preschool of Science, our curriculum places a strong emphasis on interactive science learning. We immerse children in enjoyable experiences that fuel curiosity and ignite creativity in the sciences. Our young learners handle insects, assemble basic machines, and dig for fossils, which not only delights their senses but also promotes exploration. These engaging activities build foundational STEM principles that are crucial for cognitive development.

The design of our preschool encourages children to explore their instinctual curiosity through active participation and gameplay.
