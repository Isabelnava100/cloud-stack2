What should I do if my air conditioning system stops working?
If your air conditioning system stops working, the first step is to check if the thermostat is set correctly and ensure that the circuit breaker hasn’t tripped. If everything seems fine but the system still isn’t working, it’s time to contact Romeo Air Conditioning, Inc. for our air conditioning repair service. You can reach us at any time for emergency air conditioning repair services to quickly diagnose and rectify the issue.
