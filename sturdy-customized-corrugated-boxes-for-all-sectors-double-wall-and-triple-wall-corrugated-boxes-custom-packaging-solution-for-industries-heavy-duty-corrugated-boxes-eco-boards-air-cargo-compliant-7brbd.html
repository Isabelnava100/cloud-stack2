Advantages of Double and Triple Wall Corrugated Packaging

Boxes with double and triple wall construction deliver exceptional durability and support, ensuring the safety of both fragile and heavy items during transit. Perfect for sectors such as electronics, automotive, and food service, these packaging solutions are crafted to endure rigorous handling and various environmental stresses. Their resilient design is pivotal in ensuring your merchandise arrives intact, minimizing damage progresses, and boosting customer contentment. Moreover, they can be tailored to suit specific dimensions as well as brand imagery, merging practicality with aesthetics.

Eco Boards: A Responsible Packaging Choice

Eco boards are a vital aspect of our sustainability mission at Oxbox. These eco-friendly materials provide the same resilience as traditional options while significantly lessening ecological impact. By opting for eco boards in your packaging, you not only ensure the safety of your goods but also contribute to reducing waste and supporting environmentally responsible business practices. Our eco boards conform to air cargo regulations, making them an excellent choice for shipping without sacrificing your company’s ecological commitments.
