Understanding Alabama Auto Insurance Requirements

In Alabama, it's crucial to understand the minimum auto insurance requirements to avoid penalties. The law mandates that drivers must have liability insurance that covers bodily injury and property damage. Moreover, having uninsured motorist coverage is also recommended to protect yourself against drivers without insurance.
