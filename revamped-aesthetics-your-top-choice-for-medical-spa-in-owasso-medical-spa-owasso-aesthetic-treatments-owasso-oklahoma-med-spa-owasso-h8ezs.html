Services at Revamped Aesthetics

Cutting-Edge Laser Solutions

Revamped Aesthetics stands out with its advanced laser solutions that target a variety of skin issues. The MOXI laser is exemplary for refreshment of skin brightness and texture—ideal for anyone wanting to revitalize their look. Moreover, our BBL HEROic can tackle issues such as redness, uneven pigmentation, and age spots, providing a radiant, youthful appearance that lasts. Botox and Facial Fillers

Our Owasso med spa excels in offering a selection of injectables and facial fillers aimed at highlighting your intrinsic beauty. With our dedication to achieving harmonious results, you can trust that you will leave feeling vibrant and confident in your skin.
