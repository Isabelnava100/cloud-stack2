Exploring Compassion Recovery's Virtual Treatment Methods

Exploring Dual Diagnosis

The term Dual Diagnosis describes the simultaneous presence of a mental health disorder and an addiction. At Compassion Recovery, we understand that successful recovery requires addressing both conditions concurrently. Our virtual treatment facility provides focused programs to navigate the complexities of dual diagnosis. By merging resources for mental health care with addiction recovery, we seek to disrupt the cycle of relapse and enhance lasting wellness.

Advantages of Virtual Treatment

Online treatment for mental health and addiction allows for convenience and versatility for those seeking assistance. At Compassion Recovery, our digital sessions offer you exceptional care from qualified professionals in a comfortable environment. This method enables you to concentrate on healing without the pressure of traveling. Just a few clicks connect you readily with your therapist, who will guide you through your therapeutic process from the comfort of your own home.

What tech setup is necessary for virtual therapy sessions?
To participate in virtual sessions, you will need a device with internet connectivity, such as a computer, tablet, or smartphone, plus a quiet area for the sessions.

What will happen during my first session of online therapy?
In your first online therapy session, your therapist will review your history, current struggles, and therapy goals, leading to the creation of a customized treatment plan.
