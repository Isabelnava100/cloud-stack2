How should I prepare for my laser hair removal appointment?
Preparing for your laser hair removal appointment at Meljestic Spa is simple. We recommend avoiding sun exposure and tanning for at least two weeks prior to your session, as well as refraining from waxing or plucking the hair in the treatment area. Shaving is encouraged to ensure hair follicles are still present while minimizing surface hair. Additionally, it's vital to discuss any medications or skincare products you are using with our professionals to ensure a safe and effective treatment.
