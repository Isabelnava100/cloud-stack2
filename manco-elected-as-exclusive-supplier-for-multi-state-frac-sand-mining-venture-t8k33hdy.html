Manco Elected as Exclusive Supplier for Multi-State Frac Sand Mining Venture

Eight Customized Containers Developed and Deployed in Louisiana and Texas, with the final two expected shortly.

Manco Rentals & Sales, LLC has secured the role of the primary supplier of containers for a confidential frac sand mining endeavor spanning across Louisiana and Texas. This initiative features eight customized units that include site offices, operational control units, and Motor Control Center spaces, with six units already operational and two prepared at the Manco facility in Eunice for their imminent deployment.
