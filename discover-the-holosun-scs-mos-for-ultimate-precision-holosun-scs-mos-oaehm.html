What is the Holosun SCS MOS?

The Holosun SCS MOS is a solar-powered, direct-mount red dot sight designed specifically for full-size Glock Modular Optic System (MOS) handguns, such as the Glock 17, 19, and 34. Unlike traditional optics that require adapter plates, the SCS MOS attaches seamlessly to the slide, maintaining a low profile and enhancing the firearm’s aesthetics and performance. Its innovative solar charging system, durable construction, and versatile reticle options make it a favorite among competitive shooters, law enforcement professionals, and concealed carry enthusiasts.

Key Features of the Holosun SCS MOS

  1. Solar Charging Technology: The SCS MOS eliminates the need for battery replacements by utilizing a solar panel with multi-directional light sensors. This system powers an internal, rechargeable 20,000-hour battery, ensuring the optic remains operational even in low-light conditions. Solar Failsafe Technology provides a backup power source that automatically adjusts reticle brightness based on ambient light.

  2. Direct Mount Design: Engineered to fit Glock MOS platforms without adapter plates, the SCS MOS reduces the bore axis, improving accuracy and handling. Its low-profile design enhances co-witness with standard-height iron sights, making it ideal for both tactical and competitive applications.

  3. Multiple Reticle System (MRS): The SCS MOS offers three reticle configurations: a 2 MOA green dot, a 32 MOA green circle, or a combination of both. The green Super LED is optimized for human eye sensitivity, providing superior visibility in bright sunlight and low-light environments.

  4. Durability and Construction: Crafted from Grade 5 Titanium, the SCS MOS boasts double the tensile strength of 7075 aluminum and exceptional resistance to corrosion, making it suitable for maritime environments. It is IPX8 rated, capable of withstanding submersion up to 1 meter for 30 minutes.

  5. Automatic Brightness Adjustment: The optic’s auto-brightness feature adapts to ambient lighting conditions, ensuring optimal reticle visibility without manual adjustments. An intensity override function locks the optic in high-brightness mode for 30 minutes, ideal for dynamic shooting scenarios.

  6. Shake Awake Technology: The SCS MOS includes Holosun’s Shake Awake feature, which conserves battery life by powering down the optic when idle and reactivating it upon movement.
