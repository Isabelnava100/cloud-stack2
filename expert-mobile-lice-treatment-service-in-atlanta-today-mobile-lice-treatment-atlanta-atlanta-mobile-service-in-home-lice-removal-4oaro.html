Understanding Lice and Its Impact

Lice infestations can be a distressing experience for both parents and children. It's important to understand that lice are not a sign of uncleanliness; they can affect anyone, regardless of hygiene habits. Lice spread easily through close contact, such as sharing personal items or being in close proximity to an infected individual. By recognizing the nature of lice infestations, parents can address any concerns more effectively.
