How Peptides Support Weight Loss

Peptides play a crucial role in weight management by influencing the hormones that regulate hunger and fat accumulation. For example, peptides like GLP-1 can help suppress appetite while increasing feelings of fullness. Others, like growth hormone-releasing peptides, can stimulate muscle growth that helps increase metabolism.
