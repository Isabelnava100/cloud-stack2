Frequently Asked Questions

360 Behavioral Health is a provider of professional behavioral health treatment programs and services in California. We specialize in ABA-based therapies designed to support individuals with behavioral challenges. Our dedicated team offers both in-person and virtual sessions, ensuring accessible care tailored to the unique needs of each client. Our goal is to empower families and promote positive behaviors through effective, evidence-based strategies.

ABA-based parenting strategies refer to techniques derived from Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) that aim to reinforce positive behaviors and decrease negative behaviors in children. By focusing on observable behaviors and implementing structured interventions, parents can create a more supportive environment. This approach helps parents understand their child's needs, improves communication, and fosters a closer parent-child relationship, ultimately leading to a more harmonious family dynamic.

Parent behavior training involves equipping parents with the skills and strategies necessary to effectively manage their child's behaviors. This training includes understanding ABA-based techniques, identifying reinforcement strategies, and learning how to set appropriate behavioral expectations. Sessions can be conducted in-person or virtually, allowing for flexibility and convenience. By actively participating in this program, parents can apply what they learn in real-time, leading to better outcomes for their children.

Our services are designed to promote positive behavior change while addressing the specific needs of each client. Our ABA-based behavioral health services are designed for children and families experiencing a wide range of behavioral challenges. Parents seeking to enhance their skills and improve their child's behavior will also find our services beneficial. Ultimately, anyone looking to foster positive behavior change in their family can gain from our programs.

How do I get started with 360 Behavioral Health?
Getting started with 360 Behavioral Health is simple. You can reach out to us through our website or call our office to schedule an initial consultation. During this session, we will assess your child's needs and discuss appropriate treatment options, including ABA-based parenting strategies and parent behavior training. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process and ensure you receive the support you need as soon as possible.

What can I expect during a session?
During a session with 360 Behavioral Health, you can expect a collaborative and supportive environment. Sessions will typically involve direct interaction between the therapist, child, and/or parents, focusing on specific behavioral goals. The therapist will review progress, implement ABA-based strategies, and provide ongoing support. For parent behavior training, sessions may also include discussion on implementation strategies at home, ensuring that skills learned are reinforced in daily life.
