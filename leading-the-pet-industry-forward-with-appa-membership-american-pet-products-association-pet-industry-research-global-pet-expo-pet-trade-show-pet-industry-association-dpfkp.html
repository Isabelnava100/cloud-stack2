Leading the Pet Industry Forward with APPA Membership

The American Pet Products Association (APPA) has proudly served the pet industry for over 65 years, establishing itself as the premier nonprofit trade association. With a mission to empower pet product manufacturers, suppliers, retailers, and service providers, APPA acts as a strategic hub fostering industry growth and engagement. Our wide-ranging member-focused programs offer invaluable resources, from proprietary research publications to flagship trade events like the Global Pet Expo and The Pet Summit. By joining APPA, members gain access to essential market intelligence, regulatory guidance, and impactful networking opportunities tailored to drive the pet industry forward. As a leading pet industry association, APPA is committed to delivering comprehensive insights into market trends, consumer behavior, and economic forecasts. Our annual National Pet Owners Survey and the State of the Industry Report provide key information that helps businesses thrive. Furthermore, we advocate for the interests of the pet industry at the regulatory level, ensuring that our members succeed in a dynamic and competitive landscape. With over 1,000 businesses connected through our platform, APPA fosters collaboration and knowledge sharing that ultimately benefits the entire pet products ecosystem.

Membership Benefits and Resources for Pet Businesses

Comprehensive Research Publications

APPA is dedicated to providing its members with authoritative research that drives decision-making and strategic planning. Our flagship reports, such as the National Pet Owners Survey and the State of the Industry Report, offer critical insights into pet ownership trends, spending patterns, and economic forecasts. These resources equip pet businesses with the knowledge needed to navigate the evolving marketplace and make informed choices. Members can leverage this data to enhance product development, marketing strategies, and overall business operations, solidifying their competitive edge in the pet products sector.

Flagship Events: Global Pet Expo and The Pet Summit

As the premier pet trade show, the Global Pet Expo brings together thousands of exhibitors and attendees from across the pet industry, creating ample opportunities for networking, education, and product discovery. APPA also hosts The Pet Summit, an exclusive event designed to share the latest knowledge on pet industry innovations, consumer trends, and market risks. Participation in these flagship events enhances visibility and fosters connections among pet businesses, forging partnerships that are vital for success in today's marketplace.

Advocacy and Regulatory Support for Members

APPA serves as a strong advocate for the interests of its members within the pet industry. By providing regulatory guidance and connecting members with policymakers, we ensure that the voices of pet businesses are heard. Our advocacy efforts are aimed at driving favorable legislation that promotes growth and innovation within the sector. Being part of APPA means your business is represented in critical discussions affecting the pet products landscape, giving members an influential role in shaping the industry's future.

Who can become a member of APPA?
Any business involved in the pet products ecosystem - including manufacturers, suppliers, and service providers - can join APPA.

Where is APPA headquartered?
The American Pet Products Association is headquartered in Stamford, Connecticut, and operates a hybrid workforce to better serve its members across the nation.
