Can I cut Bread Elastic Shoelaces to fit my shoes better?

How do I care for my Bread Elastic Shoelaces?
Caring for your Bread Elastic Shoelaces is simple. They are made of durable polyester, which is resistant to wear and tear. If they become dirty, simply wipe them with a damp cloth or hand wash them gently in soapy water if needed. Avoid using harsh chemicals or bleach, as this could damage the elastic material. By taking these steps, you can keep your purple shoelaces looking fresh and vibrant while extending their lifespan for daily use.
