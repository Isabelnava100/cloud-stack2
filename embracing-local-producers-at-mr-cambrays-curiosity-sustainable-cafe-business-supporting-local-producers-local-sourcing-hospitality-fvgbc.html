Discover Our Dedication to Local Sourcing

The Importance of Local Producers

At Mr Cambray’s Curiosity, choosing local producers defines our core mission. We exclusively source our ingredients from neighboring farms and suppliers to ensure both freshness and superior quality in our offerings. Supporting local producers means bolstering our local economy and minimizing our carbon footprint—an essential stride toward sustainability. Our pledge towards local sourcing is reflected in an ever-refreshing menu that embraces seasonal ingredients and highlights the best produce Cheltenham can offer.

Sustainability Practices in Our Café Bar

For us at Mr Cambray’s Curiosity, sustainability encompasses much more than sourcing practices. We take a comprehensive approach to incorporate eco-friendly methods across our operations to minimize waste and enhance energy efficiency. By composting waste and utilizing biodegradable materials, we strive to establish a friendly space that aligns with our ideals as a sustainable café business. Through our conscious choices, we inspire our guests to actively participate in environmental preservation as they relish our exquisite local offerings.

Become a Part of Our Supportive Community

More than just a café and bar, Mr Cambray’s Curiosity serves as a vibrant community hub where individuals join together to support local producers and experience the pleasures of local sourcing hospitality. We organize events that honor local artisans and encourage our guests to engage with the creators of their culinary and beverage delights. Join us in cultivating a community based on sustainability and appreciation for regionally crafted goods, where every visit plays a part in a larger vision.
