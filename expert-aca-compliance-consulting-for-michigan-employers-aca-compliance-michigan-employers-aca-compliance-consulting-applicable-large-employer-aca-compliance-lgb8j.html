Common Questions

The ACA compliance refers to the requirements set forth by the Affordable Care Act, which mandates that Applicable Large Employers provide health insurance to their employees. Compliance is essential to avoid hefty penalties and to ensure employees have access to affordable health care.

An Applicable Large Employer is defined as a business with 50 or more full-time employees or full-time equivalent employees. They are subject to specific ACA compliance requirements.

The key forms for ACA compliance are Form 1094-C and Form 1095-C. These forms report health coverage offers made to employees and support compliance with ACA mandates.

Employers can face significant penalties for failing to comply with ACA requirements, including fines for not providing health coverage and failing to file the necessary forms accurately.

CFH Insurance Consultants provides expert consultancy to help employers navigate ACA compliance, including form preparation, understanding reporting requirements, and minimizing penalties.
