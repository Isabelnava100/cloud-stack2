Skin Lesion Removal Services

What is Mohs Surgery?

Mohs surgery is a specialised technique for skin lesion removal, primarily used for treating skin cancers. It involves removing the cancerous tissue layer by layer, ensuring complete excision while preserving healthy skin. This method is highly effective for basal and squamous cell carcinomas, minimising the risk of recurrence. Understanding Mohs surgery allows patients to appreciate the meticulous approach taken for skin lesion removal and the importance of expert care in this process.
