Herringbone Tile: Adding Depth and Elegance

Herringbone tile patterns are a timeless choice that adds a sense of movement and sophistication to any room. This zigzag design is particularly popular in hallways, kitchens, and bathrooms, creating a stunning visual impact that draws the eye. The versatility of herringbone allows it to be combined with various materials, such as zellige and cement tiles, to create unique layering effects. Selecting herringbone tile from our collection ensures that you're investing in a durable, stylish option that will remain relevant for years.

Checkerboard Tile: Classic and Versatile

Checkerboard tile patterns are known for their classic appeal and can effortlessly adapt to both modern and traditional interiors. This pattern works wonderfully in high-traffic areas like entryways and dining rooms, where it can make a statement while being functional. Zia Tile offers a range of checkerboard options, allowing you to experiment with color combinations and materials. Whichever tiles you choose, the checkerboard design will bring a playful yet elegant element to your space.
