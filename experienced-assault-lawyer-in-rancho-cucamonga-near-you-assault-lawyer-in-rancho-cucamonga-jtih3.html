Potential Consequences of Assault Charges

Assault charges can lead to severe penalties, including jail time, fines, and a criminal record. In California, penalties vary based on the assault's severity and whether any aggravating factors are present. A misdemeanor assault may result in less severe consequences compared to a felony, which can result in significant prison time.
