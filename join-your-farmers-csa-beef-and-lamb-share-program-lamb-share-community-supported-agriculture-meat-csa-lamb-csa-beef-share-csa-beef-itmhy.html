Join Your Farmer's CSA Beef and Lamb Share Program

At Your Farmer, we believe in community supported agriculture, offering premium beef and lamb shares right from our Southern Highlands farm. Founded by David and Melissa Savill, our CSA programs connect you directly with the source of your meat, providing transparency and quality. Our beef and lamb are 100% grass-fed and pasture-raised, ensuring you receive nutrient-dense meat that is both wholesome and delicious. With a paddock-to-plate subscription model, fresh meat is delivered directly to your doorstep, supporting local agriculture in New South Wales and ACT. Choosing a CSA beef share or a lamb share from Your Farmer means you embrace sustainable farming practices. Our dedicated family farms uphold the same ecological vision, ensuring the health of our environment as well as your family. Each seasonal box provides a selection of cuts, making your meals exciting and diverse throughout the year. With the option to skip, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time, our service is designed to fit seamlessly into your lifestyle while empowering you to make informed choices for your diet.

Understanding CSA Shares and Their Benefits

Community supported agriculture (CSA) beef and lamb shares are an innovative way of directly connecting consumers with family farms. By subscribing to a CSA from Your Farmer, you ensure you are eating high-quality, grass-fed meat while also supporting local farmers. The benefits of a CSA go beyond just freshness; they promote sustainable farming practices, strengthen community ties, and often provide produce that is better for the environment than mass-produced alternatives. Enjoy the peace of mind that comes from knowing where your food originates and how it is raised.
