Key Areas to Inspect for Storm Damage

After winter, there are critical areas Pittsburgh homeowners should inspect. Check for missing or curled shingles; these might indicate underlying issues or potential leaks. Additionally, evaluate the granule loss from your shingles, as this can affect their protective qualities against storms. Pay attention to flashing around chimneys and vents, as damage in these areas can lead to leaks and further deterioration of your roof.

Documenting Roof Damage Effectively

If damage is found during your inspection, document it meticulously. Take clear photographs of any damaged areas, such as missing shingles, as well as the entire roof from various angles. This documentation will be vital for your insurance claim process. Our team at Malick Brothers is skilled in helping homeowners compile the necessary evidence to support their claims for roof repairs or replacements due to storm damage.

When to Call a Professional vs. DIY Inspection

While minor issues can sometimes be handled personally, serious damage should be referred to professionals.
