The Top 3 Things Minnesota's New Indoor Shooting Range Membership Brings to the Holidays

At Southern Minnesota's new shooting range facility at 2 If By Sea Tactical, open since May 2025, a membership is the best way to get the most value. The range includes 8 lanes with digitally controlled targets, an air filtration system that refreshes the air every 90 seconds, and comfortable shooting benches. As we approach the 2025 holiday season, for families looking to bring experiences to their loved ones, a membership to the indoor range could be a great investment.

 

(Although if you aren't much of a shooter, there's also a virtual reality experience.)

 

With monthly plans starting at $49.99/month, you still might be wondering if it's worth it to you.

 

Let's break down the top three benefits of all plans so that you can get the most out of your membership. Exploring all the features is essential to enjoying your time and becoming a part of the community.

 

Ready. Aim. Belong. Today.

The Top 3 Things Minnesota's New Indoor Shooting Range Membership Brings to the Holidays

1) Priority Indoor Range Lane Reservations for Members Only

It's never fun to pack up all your gear (did I remember ammo? Ear and eye protection? The key to my gun lock?), come to the range, and have to wait. Sure, there's comfortable seats, TVs, free coffee, and fun merch to view. Your wait time would pass quickly. But maybe you really need to be in and out. Members can avoid this with lane reservations. While the public is welcome to use the range, only members can make reservations. And the lane use is included as a part of your membership, and membership gets some number of free guest passes. Normal walk-in pricing is $35/half hour. Do the math right now on how much you could save if you came to the shooting range twice a month. There are even family plans, so you can take your whole family out for an outing once or twice a month for less than the cost of eating out.

2) Learn From Experienced Instructors and Staff On and Off the Range

Whether you're new to gun ownership, helping someone choose their first gun, or have a lot of personal experience with firearms, there's always more to learn. In addition to range time, members get access to Austin Reville -- founder, NRA and USCCA firearms instructor, and private security expert -- and Mike Konyu -- lead instructor of Aegis Defense Solutions and security consultant. They lead regular classes at 2 If By Sea Tactical, and members get to go to classes for free or at reduced prices, including the popular Permit to Carry class.

3) Members Enjoy Other Community Perks

Membership benefits don't stop with the range or classes. Everything in the store is for our members to enjoy. Public community freebies include notary services and document shredding. But members get a little bit more: free book checkout from our Washington Library, store discounts, and custom merch. Perks vary based on membership level, so explore the plans. If you enjoy browsing our firearms and accessories, or drink a lot of Smokin Gun Coffee, the discount can help balance the membership cost. Try the range once. Try a membership for a month. And then save big by going annual. It could be the perfect Christmas gift.

Are firearms available for rent at the range?
Yes, we have guns for rent for our indoor shooting range. There are several kinds of new and historic firearms that can be rented, including machine guns. Come in to view our selection! Please note, gun rentals cannot leave the facility.

What types of training do you offer to members?
We offer classes to both the public and members in the following areas: Permit to Carry, Mama Bear Tactics, Red Dot Lab, Handgun, and Rifle.

Do you allow shotguns at your range?
Yes, we do allow shotguns to be used on the gun range – but only with shotgun slugs. Shellshot is harmful to our target tracks and shooting lanes.
