Regular Color Refresh Guidelines

The ideal schedule for refreshing your color takes into account your hair type, tone, and daily routine. Typically, root touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks, while glossing can occur every 3-4 weeks to preserve brilliance. Clients with balayage can generally go for 8-12 weeks between sessions. We'll create a tailored approach during your consultation to assist you in keeping your color looking stunning between visits.
