What are the benefits of inpatient rehab?
Inpatient rehab provides a structured environment with 24-hour support. This immersive setting can be beneficial for individuals with severe addictions or those needing intensive care.

How long do drug rehab programs usually last?
The duration of drug rehab programs varies depending on individual needs but typically ranges from 30 to 90 days. Long-term programs may extend beyond this period for those requiring additional support.
