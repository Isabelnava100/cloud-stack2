CFH Insurance Consultants: Your Michigan Benefits Broker

At CFH Insurance Consultants, we are committed to providing Michigan-based businesses with the top-tier employee benefits solutions they need to thrive. Founded in 2007 and headquartered in Bloomfield Hills, we specialize in the needs of middle-market companies with 50-500 employees. Our expertise in ACA compliance, health plan structuring, and multi-line benefits ensures that your organization can offer competitive and compliant options to your workforce. As a fully independent broker, we work with all major Michigan carriers to tailor solutions that align with your organization’s goals and employee needs. As Michigan's leading independent employee benefits broker, we pride ourselves on our consultative approach—supporting our clients year-round rather than just at renewal time. Our services include assistance with fully insured, level-funded, and self-funded group health insurance plans, as well as ICHRA and QSEHRA implementation. Our team is equipped with the knowledge and resources to help you navigate the complexities of employee benefits, ensuring that you remain compliant and competitive in today’s market.
