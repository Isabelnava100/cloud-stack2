How do I know if my heating or cooling system needs repairs?
Several signs indicate that your heating or cooling system may need repairs. These include unusual noises (like banging or hissing), inconsistent temperatures throughout your home, poor air quality, or an increase in energy bills without any change in usage. If you notice these symptoms or if your system is not functioning as it used to, it's time to contact Elite HVAC Heating & Air for a professional assessment and necessary repairs.
