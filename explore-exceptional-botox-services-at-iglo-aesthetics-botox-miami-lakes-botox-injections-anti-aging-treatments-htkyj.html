How should I prepare for my Botox procedure?
To minimize potential bruising, it's advisable to stay away from blood thinners, alcohol, and anti-inflammatory drugs leading up to your appointment.

Can I carry on with my normal routine after Botox injections?
Yes, most individuals can return to their everyday activities shortly after Botox injections, but it’s prudent to refrain from vigorous exercise for a day.
