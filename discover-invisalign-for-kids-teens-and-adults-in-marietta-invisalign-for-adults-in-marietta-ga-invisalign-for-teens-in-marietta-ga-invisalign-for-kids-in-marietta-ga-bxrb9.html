Frequently Asked Questions

Invisalign treatment can start for kids as young as 7, depending on their orthodontic needs. Early evaluation is crucial.

The duration of Invisalign treatment varies by patient but generally takes about 12 to 18 months for teens and adults, and can be shorter for kids.

One of the benefits of Invisalign is that you can remove the aligners while eating, allowing you to enjoy all your favorite foods.

Some discomfort may occur as you adjust to new aligners, but Invisalign is generally more comfortable than traditional braces.

Invisalign aligners must be worn for 20 to 22 hours a day for the best results. They can be removed for eating and brushing.
