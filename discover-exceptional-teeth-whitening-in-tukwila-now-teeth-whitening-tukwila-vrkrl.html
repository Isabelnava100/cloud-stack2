Extra FAQ

For how long can I expect the whitening results to linger?

Whitening results may last from six months up to two years, contingent on your dental care routine and lifestyle habits.

What dietary choices should I avoid post-whitening?

To prolong your results, avoid staining foods and beverages such as coffee, tea, and red wine for at least 24 to 48 hours.
