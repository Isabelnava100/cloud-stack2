Getting to Know THC Edibles

THC edibles are delectable items infused with tetrahydrocannabinol (THC), the active psychoactive ingredient in cannabis. These enticing snacks provide a unique experience compared to the conventional methods of smoking or vaping. The effects may take time to emerge, typically ranging from 30 minutes to 2 hours, which can differ based on personal metabolism and tolerance levels. This slower onset enhances and prolongs the overall experience, so it’s wise to start with a smaller amount if you're trying THC edibles for the first time.
