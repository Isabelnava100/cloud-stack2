What is an F1 Boiler Fault?

An F1 boiler fault indicates a problem with the boiler’s pressure or safety system. This can manifest as a failure to ignite or maintain consistent heating. Understanding this fault is crucial for homeowners to take appropriate action. Knowing when to call plumbing experts helps ensure quick restoration of your heating system.

Common Causes of F1 Boiler Faults

F1 boiler faults can stem from various issues including low water pressure or malfunctioning components. Another common cause is a blocked flue or vent that prevents proper airflow. Identifying these issues early can save you time and money on extensive repairs. Our team at Kentish Plumbers is experienced in diagnosing these problems efficiently.
