Defining Root Cause Wellness Coaching

Root Cause Wellness Coaching represents a guiding framework dedicated to pinpointing and resolving the essential factors impacting women's health. It gives women the opportunity to reflect on their health background, lifestyle choices, and visible symptoms, revealing the primary causes behind persistent issues like chronic tiredness and hormonal irregularities. Janice Maynard, a Board-Certified Functional Diagnostic Nutrition Practitioner, leverages a holistic perspective to formulate bespoke wellness strategies for her clientele. This methodology promotes a profound comprehension of each woman’s distinct body and healing progression, ensuring a more fruitful avenue toward invigorated health and enhanced well-being.
