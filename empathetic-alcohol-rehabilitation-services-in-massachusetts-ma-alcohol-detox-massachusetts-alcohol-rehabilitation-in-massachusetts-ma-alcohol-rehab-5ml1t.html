Frequently Asked Questions

Indicators that you might need alcohol rehabilitation include problems controlling your alcohol intake, experiencing withdrawal symptoms, and effects on your health, life, or personal relationships.

Alcohol detoxification is a clinically overseen procedure where the body is cleansed of alcohol, while closely managing its withdrawal effects to ensure safety and comfort.

The duration of an alcohol rehabilitation program varies, usually falling between 30 to 90 days, influenced by individual circumstances and treatment plans.

Many health insurance policies do include coverage for alcohol rehabilitation; however, it’s crucial to consult your provider to fully understand your specific benefits.

While in rehab, expect a well-structured schedule that includes counseling sessions, group activities, scheduled meals, and personal time for reflection and relaxation.
