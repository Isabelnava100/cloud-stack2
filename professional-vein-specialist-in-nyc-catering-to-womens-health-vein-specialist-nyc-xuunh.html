What Leads to Vein Problems?

Vein complications can stem from many sources, including genetics, hormonal variations, and lifestyle choices. Women may be more vulnerable to these issues because of hormonal changes, especially during situations like pregnancy and menopause which can worsen vein conditions. It is important to recognize the root causes to choose the right treatment.
