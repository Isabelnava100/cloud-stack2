SOZO Addiction Recovery Center: A Faith-Based Pathway

Our Treatment Programs

SOZO Recovery Center offers a comprehensive range of treatment programs designed to meet every individual’s needs. Our medical detox, residential treatment, and outpatient services ensure that clients receive care at every stage of their recovery journey. We recognize the diversity of our clients’ experiences and create personalized treatment plans that incorporate both clinical and spiritual elements for optimal recovery outcomes. Our 12-step program serves as a foundational guide as clients work towards sustainable sobriety and healing.

Faith-Centered Care

At SOZO, we believe that faith plays a crucial role in the recovery process. Our Christian-centered approach encourages clients to explore their spirituality while healing from addiction. Spiritual guidance, prayer sessions, and discussions about faith are integral to our programs. By fostering personal connections with God, clients can find strength and resilience on their journey towards recovery, reinforcing their commitment to lasting sobriety through faith-driven practices.

Community Reintegration

After completing treatment at SOZO, our focus transitions to community reintegration, ensuring that clients have the necessary support for a sustainable recovery. We provide aftercare planning and access to local resources that help men reintegrate into society successfully. Our relationships with various community organizations enhance our clients’ opportunities for employment, housing, and ongoing support. We understand that recovery is a lifelong journey, and our commitment to our clients extends beyond their time at our center.
