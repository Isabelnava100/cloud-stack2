What is Gynaecomastia?

Gynaecomastia is a condition characterised by the enlargement of breast tissue in males. This can occur due to hormonal imbalances, genetics, or certain medications. It is important to consult a qualified surgeon to understand the underlying causes and the best course of action for treatment. This condition can affect self-esteem, leading men to seek discreet and effective treatment options that help restore body confidence. At Contoura Medical, we specialise in diagnosing and treating gynaecomastia through advanced surgical techniques, tailored to your individual needs.

Why Choose Gynaecomastia Surgery?

Gynaecomastia surgery, or male breast reduction, is a safe and effective way to correct enlarged male breasts. This procedure removes excess fat and glandular tissue, helping to sculpt a flatter and more masculine chest contour. At Contoura Medical, our advanced VASER techniques ensure minimal scarring and faster recovery times. Choosing surgery can significantly improve confidence and body image for many men, allowing them to feel more comfortable in their skin. A comprehensive consultation is essential to evaluate your suitability for surgery and to discuss any safety concerns.
