Understanding the Lead Generation Funnel

The lead generation funnel is crucial for contractors looking to attract and convert potential clients. At ContractorScale, we break it down into four main stages: Awareness, Interest, Decision, and Action. Awareness involves attracting attention through various marketing channels like SEO and paid ads. Interest is piqued when prospective clients engage with your content and seek more information about your services. The Decision stage sees contractors provide value through personalized communication, while the Action stage is where high-quality leads convert into clients.
