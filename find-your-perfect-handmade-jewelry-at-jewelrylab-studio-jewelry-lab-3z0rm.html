How do I care for my jewelry made from precious metals?
To maintain the beauty and integrity of your jewelry, we recommend cleaning it regularly with a soft cloth to remove any dirt or oils. Store your pieces in a dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent tarnishing. Additionally, avoid exposing your jewelry to harsh chemicals or wearing it in water, as these can damage the finish. With proper care, your jewelry will remain a cherished accessory for years to come.
