What safety measures are in place at Volt Raceway?
Safety is our top priority at Volt Raceway. We implement a variety of measures to ensure a secure racing environment. All participants receive a comprehensive safety briefing before entering the track. Our electric go-karts are equipped with speed limiting features, and the track is designed with safety barriers to prevent collisions. Additionally, our trained staff is always present to monitor the activity on the track and provide assistance as needed. We encourage all racers to follow the provided guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
