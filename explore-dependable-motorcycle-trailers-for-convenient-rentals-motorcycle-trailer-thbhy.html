Properly loading a motorcycle onto a trailer is crucial for safe transport—be it headed to a convention, a racing event, or just relocating it within the city. At Big Loads Trailer Rental Marketplace, we not only assist you in locating the perfect motorcycle trailer but also ensure that your bike remains safeguarded on every segment of the trip.

When placing your motorcycle on a trailer, begin by positioning it over the axles to achieve optimal balance. Utilize a wheel chock to secure the front wheel, then fasten the motorcycle with ratchet tie-down straps at four durable points—usually the handlebars and rear frame or foot pegs. Ensure the straps are angled slightly downward and outward to avoid movement. Tighten just enough to cause minor compression of the suspension—if they’re too loose, the bike may shift; too tight can cause damage to parts.
