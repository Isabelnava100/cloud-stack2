Frequently Asked Questions

Anna McGee Coaching is an online fitness coaching service specifically designed to empower women over 50. The program focuses on transforming health and building lasting strength through personalized fitness and nutrition plans. With over 30 years of experience in the fitness industry, Anna combines her expertise and passion to tailor solutions for each client's unique needs.

The coaching process at Anna McGee Coaching begins with a personalized consultation to assess your health goals, lifestyle, and fitness level. Based on this assessment, Anna recommends the ideal program for you. After implementation, you receive ongoing support, guidance, and regular progress tracking to ensure you achieve your goals effectively.

Anna McGee Coaching offers three signature programs: Fit 4 Life, a group fitness option that includes live workouts and a community group; Built 2 Last, a personalized 1-on-1 coaching experience; and Fit 4 U, a specialized program for clients using GLP-1 medications. Each program is designed to cater to the unique needs of women over 50.

The Fit 4 Life program includes five live home workouts each week, access to recorded sessions for flexibility, email access to Coach Anna for personalized support, and a private community group for motivation and accountability. It's ideal for women who thrive in a group fitness environment and seek a supportive and energetic atmosphere.

The Built 2 Last program is a 1-on-1 custom coaching service tailored to your fitness and nutrition goals. It includes personalized fitness plans, comprehensive nutrition coaching, direct communication with Coach Anna, and regular coaching calls to track progress and make necessary adjustments, ensuring you stay on course for your objectives.

How does the Fit 4 U program support clients using GLP-1 medications?
The Fit 4 U program is specifically designed for clients using GLP-1 medications. It features five live workouts weekly, a structured 12-week fitness plan, a high-protein recipe guide, and nutrition guidance to complement the program. Additionally, members benefit from a private community group for ongoing support and coaching throughout their journey.
