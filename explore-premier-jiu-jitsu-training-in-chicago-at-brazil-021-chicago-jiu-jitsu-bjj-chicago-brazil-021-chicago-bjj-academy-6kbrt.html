Frequently Asked Questions

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling-based martial art focusing on ground techniques and submissions. It stresses skill and leverage over brute strength.

We encourage participants of all age groups and skill levels, from newcomers to seasoned practitioners, to join our academy.

For your initial class, don comfortable athletic wear, such as a rash guard and shorts, or a gi if available.

No previous experience is necessary! We have beginner-focused classes available.

Training in Jiu-Jitsu enhances fitness and flexibility while developing self-defense skills and mental strength. It also fosters confidence and promotes a sense of community.
