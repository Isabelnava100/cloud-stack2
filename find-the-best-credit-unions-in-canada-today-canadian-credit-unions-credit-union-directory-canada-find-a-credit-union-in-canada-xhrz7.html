CreditUnionDirectory.ca is Canada's independent resource for comparing credit unions. We list 180+ credit unions from every province and territory, with verified rate data on HISAs, GICs, and mortgages pulled directly from official sources. No sponsored directory placements. No credit union commissions. No paid credit union rankings. 

Credit unions are member-owned cooperatives. Profits stay within the membership, which consistently produces better deposit rates, lower borrowing costs, and fewer fees. Millions of Canadians already bank with one. Millions more haven't made the switch yet. Our directory lets you filter by province, compare rates side by side, and read straightforward guides on how credit union membership actually works. We also build and maintain free Canadian financial calculators covering mortgages, TFSA, RRSP, FHSA, RESP, GIC, RRIF, HELOC, and debt repayment. All of them are built for Canadian tax rules and contribution limits. Independent. Data-verified. Just the information you need to decide whether a credit union is right for your money.
