What is the proper way to store my spill containment kit?
Position your spill containment kit in a readily accessible spot that is likely to see spills. Make sure it is shielded from environmental factors that could affect its contents.

What maintenance should be performed on spill containment kits?
Regularly check your spill containment kits to confirm that all components are intact and adequately stocked. Promptly replace any used or damaged items.
