Common Questions

Dental veneers are thin shells made from porcelain or composite resin that are bonded to the front surface of teeth to improve their appearance.

Good candidates for dental veneers typically have healthy teeth and gums, seek aesthetic improvements such as covering chips or discolorations, and have realistic expectations.

With proper care, dental veneers can last anywhere from 5 to 15 years, depending on the material used and individual oral hygiene practices.

Additional FAQ

Can dental veneers fix gaps between teeth?

Yes, dental veneers can effectively close gaps between teeth, creating a more cohesive and aesthetically pleasing smile.

How should I care for my dental veneers?

Care for your dental veneers by practicing good oral hygiene, avoiding hard foods that may chip the veneers, and visiting your dentist regularly for check-ups.
