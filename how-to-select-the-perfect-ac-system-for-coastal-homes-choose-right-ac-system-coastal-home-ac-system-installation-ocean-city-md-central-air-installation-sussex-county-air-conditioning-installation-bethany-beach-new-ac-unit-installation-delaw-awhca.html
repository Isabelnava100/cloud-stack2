How to Choose the Right AC System for Your Ocean City or Bethany Beach Home

Types of AC Systems

When it comes to selecting the right AC system for coastal homes like those in Ocean City and Bethany Beach, there are three main types to consider: central air systems, ductless mini-split systems, and heat pumps. Central air systems are best for larger homes, providing consistent cooling through ductwork. Ductless mini-splits offer flexibility by allowing for zoning, making them ideal for smaller spaces or homes without existing ductwork. Alternatively, heat pumps serve dual purposes, providing cooling in the summer and efficient heating in the winter, making them a great all-year-round option for homeowners. Our team at PROTECH HVAC helps you evaluate which system works best for your specific home needs.

Sizing Considerations for Coastal Homes

Properly sizing your AC system is paramount to ensure optimal efficiency and comfort, especially in coastal environments like Sussex County. Factors like the home's square footage, window placements, insulation levels, and even local climate conditions must be taken into account. Under-sized systems can lead to inadequate cooling, while over-sized systems may cause increased humidity and energy wastage. We at PROTECH HVAC recommend a detailed assessment to accurately size your new AC unit, ensuring it meets the demands of your unique coastal home.

Energy Efficiency Ratings and Coastal Climates

Energy efficiency ratings, such as SEER, play an important role in determining your AC system's performance and operating costs. Higher SEER ratings indicate better energy efficiency, which can be especially crucial given the humidity levels and salty air found in Ocean City and Bethany Beach. Investing in a unit with a high SEER rating can save homeowners significant money on energy bills over time. At PROTECH HVAC, we can guide you toward the most energy-efficient solutions available that cater to the coastal climate and your specific cooling needs.
