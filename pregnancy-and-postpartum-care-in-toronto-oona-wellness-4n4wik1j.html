Pregnancy and Postpartum Care in Toronto | Oona Wellness

Oona Wellness Group offers connected pregnancy and postpartum care in Toronto, all under one roof. Our multidisciplinary team includes chiropractors, pelvic floor physiotherapists, registered massage therapists, naturopathic doctors, mental health professionals, lactation consultants and osteopathic practitioners who understand the physical and emotional changes that can come with pregnancy, birth and postpartum recovery.

Depending on your concerns and goals, your personalized care plan may include support for pelvic girdle or back pain, pelvic floor function, bladder concerns, birth preparation, feeding, emotional health or returning to movement after birth. Because our practitioners work as one team, it is easier to receive coordinated care without having to connect every part on your own.

Oona’s Toronto clinic is located at 656 The Queensway, near Mimico, Etobicoke and Humber Bay Shores. Daytime, evening and weekend appointments are available. No referral is required, and new patients can book directly or begin with a complimentary 20-minute Care Navigator call.

Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy During Pregnancy and Postpartum

Pelvic floor physiotherapy is about much more than doing Kegels. During pregnancy, it may support pelvic comfort, movement, birth preparation and a better understanding of how the pelvic floor coordinates with breathing and pressure. After birth, care may address bladder or bowel concerns, pelvic heaviness, painful sex, diastasis recti, scar recovery and returning to exercise. Every appointment begins with a conversation, and no part of an assessment happens without your understanding and agreement. Explore pelvic floor physiotherapy in Toronto.

What should I expect during my first visit?
Your first appointment usually includes a conversation about your health history, current concerns and goals, followed by an assessment appropriate to the service you have booked. Your practitioner will explain their recommendations and create a personalized care plan with you.

Can I bring my baby with me to appointments?
Yes. Babies are welcome at Oona, and our team understands that feeding, soothing or taking a break may be part of an appointment during the postpartum season.
