How long does a laser tattoo removal session take?
The duration of a laser tattoo removal session can vary based on several factors, such as the size and complexity of the tattoo. Typically, sessions can last anywhere from 15 to 60 minutes. Our professionals will assess your specific case and provide an estimated time frame, ensuring that you are well-informed throughout the process.

What should I expect after a laser tattoo removal session?
After a laser tattoo removal session, you may experience some redness, swelling, or mild discomfort in the treated area, similar to a sunburn. This is a normal response, and it should subside within a few days. Our team will provide aftercare instructions to help promote healing and ensure the best results. Remember that multiple sessions may be required for complete tattoo removal.
