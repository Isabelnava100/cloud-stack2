The Benefits of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a widely recognized and effective treatment for anxiety and trauma. This evidence-based approach focuses on identifying and challenging negative thought patterns that contribute to emotional distress. By helping individuals recognize cognitive distortions, CBT empowers them to develop healthier thinking patterns and coping strategies, ultimately leading to improved emotional well-being. At Kelly Zanetich, PhD, we tailor CBT to each client's unique experiences, ensuring that therapy is both personalized and impactful.
