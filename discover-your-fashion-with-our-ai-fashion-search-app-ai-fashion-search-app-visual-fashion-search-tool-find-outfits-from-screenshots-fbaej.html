How Copped Works: The Visual Search Journey

At Copped, our AI-powered visual fashion search tool allows you to effortlessly find outfits from screenshots. Simply upload your screenshots from popular social media platforms, and let our app do the rest. Utilizing advanced image recognition technology, Copped scans our extensive database to identify exact matches and trendy alternatives, providing users with fresh ideas for styling their look. This seamless process transforms how you shop, empowering you to dive deeper into the world of fashion.
