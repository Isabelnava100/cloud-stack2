What are the consequences of missing a court appearance after bail is posted?
Failing to appear in court after securing bail can lead to severe repercussions, including the forfeiture of the bail amount and potentially facing new charges.

Are the fees for bail bonds refundable?
Bail bond fees are typically non-refundable. However, the bail itself is refundable once the case concludes, providing all court appearances have been adhered to.
