What preparation is needed before applying Woodrich Brand products?
Before applying Woodrich Brand protective treatments for wood, the surface of the timber for outdoor decking should be thoroughly cleaned to remove any dust, dirt, or oils. It's essential to ensure that the wood is dry to allow for optimal absorption of the product. Assess the wood for any prior coatings that may interfere with adhesion and sand smooth if necessary. This preparation helps guarantee a successful application and enhanced durability of your outdoor decking.
