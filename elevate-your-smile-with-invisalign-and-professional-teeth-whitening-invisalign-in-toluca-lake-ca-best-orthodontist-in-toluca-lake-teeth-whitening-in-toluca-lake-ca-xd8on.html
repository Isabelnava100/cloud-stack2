Who can use Invisalign?

Invisalign is appropriate for both teenagers and adults. We have specialized aligners available for teens to facilitate effective treatment.

How frequently will I need to come in for Invisalign follow-ups?

Typically, a follow-up visit will be scheduled every 6 to 8 weeks for progress checks and to receive your consecutive sets of aligners.
