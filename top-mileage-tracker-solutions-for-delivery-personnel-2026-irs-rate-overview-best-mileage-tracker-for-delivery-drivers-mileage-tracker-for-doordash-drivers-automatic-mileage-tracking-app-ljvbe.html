Overview of IRS Mileage Rates

Every year, the IRS updates its standard mileage rate, taking into account fuel prices, maintenance costs, and vehicle depreciation. The new rate of $0.725 per mile for 2026 reflects an increase from $0.70 in 2025, $0.67 in 2024, and $0.655 in 2023. For a delivery driver accumulating 20,000 business miles, this equates to a substantial $14,500 deduction, which is often the largest tax benefit available to 1099 workers. ShiftTracker effectively handles automatic logging of miles, applying the correct rate at tax time, ensuring you benefit from the full deduction permissible under IRS guidelines.
