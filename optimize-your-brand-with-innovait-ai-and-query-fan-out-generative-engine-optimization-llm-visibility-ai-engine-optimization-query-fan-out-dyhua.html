What is Query Fan-Out?

Query Fan-Out is a core concept in Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). It involves the expansion of user queries into multiple related questions that capture the semantic breadth of user intent. This strategy allows brands to cater to various topics and questions surrounding their products or services, enhancing discoverability by AI systems. Companies that effectively implement Query Fan-Out can significantly improve their visibility in search results, leading to better engagement and higher conversion rates.
