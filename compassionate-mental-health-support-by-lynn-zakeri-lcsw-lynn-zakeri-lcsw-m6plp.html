Understanding EMDR Therapy

EMDR therapy is a powerful approach used by Lynn Zakeri, LCSW to help clients process traumatic memories. This specialized therapy allows individuals to heal from PTSD and emotional distress effectively. Lynn utilizes EMDR to facilitate the integration of memories while cultivating resilience and emotional regulation. Clients who have undergone EMDR therapy often report significant improvements in their overall well-being and quality of life.

The Benefits of Somatic Therapy

Somatic therapy is a body-centered approach practiced by Lynn Zakeri, LCSW, that focuses on the connection between mind and body. This method is particularly effective for individuals who experience trauma, stress, or emotional blockages. By incorporating body awareness and movement, Lynn helps clients release tension and gain new insights into their emotional experiences. Somatic therapy fosters a deep sense of healing and self-acceptance.
